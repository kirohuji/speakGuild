# 基础④ · 疑问句实战 · 出行·消费·社交·电话·应急（L1）

## 学习包概览

本学习包是 **基础系列（Foundation）· 第 4 包**，也是从"会造句"到"会聊天"的 **能力跃升包**。前三个包你学会了描述自己、描述他人，这一包你学会**提问**——真正的对话是问答互动，**不会问 = 不会聊**。

> 🎯 **核心目标**：学完本包，你将在 5 大真实场景中脱口而出正确问句，建立"疑问词优先"的英语思维——**从"我会说"到"我会问"。**

### 为什么疑问句需要专门一个包？

中文问句太简单了——加个"吗"就行："你是学生吗？""你喜欢咖啡吗？" 动词不用动，词序不用变。

但英语不一样——**疑问词必须前置，助动词必须倒装**：

- 中文：你是学生吗？→ 英语：**Are** you a student?（不是 You are a student?）
- 中文：你来自哪里？→ 英语：**Where are** you from?（不是 You are from where?）
- 中文：他喜欢什么？→ 英语：**What does** he like?（不是 He likes what?）

这是中国学习者最核心的**思维转换**——中文问句的结构和陈述句一样，英语问句的 Wh- 词永远在最前面。**必须通过大量场景化练习形成条件反射。**

> ❌ 中国人在真实场景中常犯：*Where I can buy a ticket? / *How long the bus takes? / *You have a map?
> ✅ 正确：**Where can I buy a ticket? / How long does the bus take? / Do you have a map?**

> ⚠️ **语法边界**：本包严格只用 ①-③ 已学语法（be 动词、一般动词 do/does、第三人称单数）。情态动词（can/should）、过去时（did/was）、完成时（have been）均未学，不在本包出现。

### 设计理念：场景驱动，而非语法分类

传统教材按语法分类教疑问句（Yes/No 问句 → Wh- 问句 → How 问句），学生学完还是不会在实际场景中提问。本包反过来——**按"你在什么场景下需要问什么"来组织**：

| 你身处… | 你需要会问… |
|:---------|:------------|
| 🅰️ 街头/车站 | Where is...? / Which bus...? / How far...? |
| 🅱️ 商店/餐厅 | How much...? / Do you have...? / What do you recommend? |
| 🅲 社交场合 | What do you do? / Where are you from? / Are you free...? |
| 🅳 打电话时 | Is Mr. Wang there? / Who's calling? / When is he back? |
| 🅴 紧急情况 | Help! Where is...? / Is there a doctor? / What do I do? |

> 🧠 **核心思维**（贯穿全包）：英语问句的 Wh-词**永远在最前面**，一上来就告诉对方"我要问什么类型的信息"。这和中文"先铺垫再问"的思维完全相反。

## 设计哲学

### 🧩 两层词汇分配策略

每个话题的词汇分为两层：

| 层级 | 占比 | 定义 | 判断标准 |
|------|:----:|------|----------|
| **核心词 (Core)** | 60~70% | 词义与该场景/话题**直接语义相关** | "在这个场景里一定会用到这个词吗？" |
| **扩展词 (Extension)** | 30~40% | 词义不直接相关，但**能高效练习本话题的问句/句型** | "这个词能放进本话题的问句里练吗？" |

> **示例**：`问路与位置` 话题中，`where, street, corner, left, right, station` 是核心词；`pharmacy, museum, hotel` 是扩展词——虽然本身不是方向词，但 "Where is the nearest pharmacy?" 能有效练习 `Where is the nearest ___?` 问句。

**三条判断标准**：
1. 这个词跟话题标题有语义关联吗？ → 有 → **核心词**
2. 这个词能放进本话题的问句/句型里练吗？ → 能 → **扩展词**
3. 两个都不满足 → **不该放在这**

### 🗺️ 场景驱动，而非语法分类

传统教材按语法分类教疑问句（Yes/No 问句 → Wh- 问句 → How 问句），学生学完还是不会在实际场景中提问。本包反过来——**按"你在什么场景下需要问什么"来组织**：

| 你身处… | 你需要会问… | 涉及的疑问词 |
|:---------|:------------|:-----------|
| 🅰️ 街头/车站 | Where is...? / Which bus...? / How far...? | Where, Which, How far, How long |
| 🅱️ 商店/餐厅 | How much...? / Do you have...? / What do you recommend? | How much, Do/Does, What |
| 🅲 社交场合 | What do you do? / Where are you from? / Are you free...? | What, Where, Are/Do |
| 🅳 打电话时 | Is Mr. Wang there? / Who's calling? / When is he back? | Is, Who, When |
| 🅴 紧急情况 | Help! Where is...? / Is there a doctor? / What do I do? | Where, Is there, What, How |

> 🧠 **核心思维**（贯穿全包）：英语问句的 Wh-词**永远在最前面**，一上来就告诉对方"我要问什么类型的信息"。这和中文"先铺垫再问"的思维完全相反。本包的每个句块都在训练这个思维转换。

### 🔗 累加式设计 (Cumulative Design)

本包是基础系列第 4 包，25 个话题按 5 个场景递进。**语法上严格只用包①-③ 已学内容**（be 动词、一般动词 do/does、第三人称单数），不引入新语法点：

```
包① be 动词        → Is/Are 疑问句（Is there...? / Are you...?）
包② 一般动词       → Do/Does 疑问句（Do you have...? / Does this bus...?）
包③ 第三人称单数   → What does he...? / When is she...?
包④ 场景A 出行交通 (Topic 1-5)   → 打好 Where/How/Which 问句基础
包④ 场景B 消费服务 (Topic 6-10)  → 复用A + 新增 How much/Do you have 问句
包④ 场景C 社交寒暄 (Topic 11-15) → 复用A+B + 新增 What do you/Are you 社交问句
包④ 场景D 电话联络 (Topic 16-20) → 复用A+B+C + 新增 Is...there/Who's calling
包④ 场景E 应急求助 (Topic 21-25) → 复用全部 + 综合实战
```

> **关键原则**：本包的"新"不在语法，而在**场景组合**——把已学的 be 动词、do/does、三单变化放到 5 个真实场景中练到脱口而出。

### 📐 问句驱动词汇分配

问句中出现的**槽位词**（slot words）优先成为该话题的扩展词：

| 问句模板 | 槽位类型 | 扩展词来源 |
|------|----------|-----------|
| `Where is the nearest ___?` | 地点名词 | 包② 地点词、Topic 01 核心词 |
| `How much is/are ___?` | 商品名词 | 包② 物品词、Topic 06 核心词 |
| `Do you have ___?` | 物品/属性 | 包①/② 名词、Topic 07 核心词 |
| `What do you do? / Where are you from?` | 职业/地点 | 包①/③ 职业词/地点词 |
| `Is ___ there/available?` | 人名/职位 | 包① 家庭词、Topic 16 核心词 |
| `Do you speak ___?` | 语言名 | 包① 国籍语言词、Topic 25 核心词 |

### 📚 牛津 3000 词参考

本包词汇以 **牛津 3000 核心词表（Oxford 3000™）** 为基准筛选。由于本包聚焦"问句实战"，核心词侧重高频疑问词（where, what, how, when, which, who, why）和 5 大场景的必用名词/动词。扩展词优先复用包①-③ 中已选的牛津 3000 词汇，形成"新场景 + 旧词汇"的高效组合。

## 句块参考（按场景分类）

> ⚠️ **学习方式**：每个句块 = 问句 + 自然回答 + 替换练习。先整句模仿跟读，再用替换练习换槽位自己造句。

### 🅰️ 出行与交通（5 主题，14 句块）

#### 第 1 课：问路与位置

> 🧠 **Where 思维**：迷路了？先想"我要问地点"→ 脑子弹出 **Where** → 再搭后面的结构。

| 主题 | 问句 | 自然回答 | 替换练习 |
|:----|:-----|:---------|:---------|
| 问路与位置 | **Excuse me, where is the nearest subway station?** | It's just around the corner. | Where is the nearest ___? (bus stop / ATM / restroom / pharmacy / hospital / bank) |
| 问路与位置 | **How do I get to the museum?** | Go straight and turn left at the corner. | How do I get to ___? (the airport / the train station / the city center / your hotel) |
| 问路与位置 | **Is there a pharmacy near here?** | Yes, there's one across the street. | Is there a ___ near here? (supermarket / post office / park / parking lot / coffee shop) |
| 问路与位置 | **How far is it from here?** | It's about 10 minutes on foot. | How far is ___ from here? (the airport / your hotel / the beach / downtown / the hospital) |
| 问路与位置 | **Am I going the right way to the station?** | Yes, keep going straight. | Am I going the right way to ___? (the museum / the park / the library / the stadium) |
| 问路与位置 | **Do you have a map?** | Sure. Let me show you. | Do you have ___? (a city map / a guidebook / the address / GPS / the directions) |
| 问路与位置 | **Is this seat taken?** | No, go ahead. | Is this ___ taken/free? (seat / table / spot / chair / parking space / locker) |

#### 第 2 课：时间与交通

> 🧠 **What time / Which 思维**：赶车赶飞机？先锁定你要问什么——时间用 **What time**，交通线路用 **Which**。

| 主题 | 问句 | 自然回答 | 替换练习 |
|:----|:-----|:---------|:---------|
| 问时间 | **What time does the museum open?** | It opens at 9 am. | What time does ___ open/close/start? (the store / the bank / the movie / the meeting) |
| 问时间 | **What time is the next train to Shanghai?** | At 3:15 pm. | What time is ___? (the last bus / your flight / the check-in / the show / the appointment) |
| 问交通 | **Which bus goes to the city center?** | Bus number 5 goes there. | Which ___ goes to ___? (train / the airport / line / the museum / subway / the stadium) |
| 问时长 | **How long does it take to get there?** | About 30 minutes by bus. | How long does it take to ___? (walk there / drive / fly / get through security / get a taxi) |
| 问地点 | **Where do I buy a ticket?** | You can buy one at the machine over there. | Where do I ___? (pay / check in / get a taxi / wait for the bus / pick up my luggage) |
| 问线路 | **Does this bus stop at the museum?** | Yes, it's the third stop. | Does this ___ stop at/go to ___? (train / the airport / line / downtown / bus / the hospital) |
| 打车问询 | **How much does it cost to go to the airport?** | About 120 yuan. | How much does it cost to go to ___? (the station / downtown / this address / the hotel) |

### 🅱️ 消费与服务（5 主题，14 句块）

#### 第 3 课：购物问询

> 🧠 **How much / Do you have 思维**：进商店先想"我要问价格（How much）还是问有没有（Do you have）"。

| 主题 | 问句 | 自然回答 | 替换练习 |
|:----|:-----|:---------|:---------|
| 问价格 | **How much is this?** | It's 120 yuan. | How much is ___? (that / the red one / this shirt / a cup of coffee / the ticket) |
| 问价格 | **How much are these?** | They're 80 yuan each. | How much are ___? (these shoes / the apples / those flowers / the souvenirs) |
| 问尺寸 | **Do you have this in a small size?** | Let me check. Yes, here you are. | Do you have this in ___? (blue / another color / medium / large / a different style) |
| 试穿 | **I want to try this on. Where is the fitting room?** | Over there, on the left. | I want to try ___ on. Where is the fitting room? (this jacket / these shoes / that dress / those pants / this hat) |
| 问选择 | **This is a little expensive. Do you have other ones?** | Yes, we have these. They're on sale. | Do you have other ___? (colors / styles / sizes / options / brands) |
| 问支付 | **Do you take cards?** | Yes, we take all major cards. | Do you take ___? (cash / WeChat / Alipay / credit cards / phone payments) |
| 要收据 | **I need a receipt, please.** | Of course. Here you are. | I need ___. (a bag / a refund / an exchange / a discount / your business card) |

#### 第 4 课：餐厅与服务

> 🧠 **I want / What do you 思维**：坐下先想——要东西用 **I want**，问推荐用 **What do you recommend**，问信息用 **Is/Does**。

| 主题 | 问句 | 自然回答 | 替换练习 |
|:----|:-----|:---------|:---------|
| 看菜单 | **I want to see the menu, please.** | Here you go. | I want to see ___. (the wine list / the dessert menu / the specials / the options) |
| 问推荐 | **What do you recommend?** | The grilled fish is very popular. | What do you recommend for ___? (a starter / the main course / dessert / someone who likes spicy food) |
| 问成分 | **Does this dish contain nuts?** | No, it doesn't. It's nut-free. | Does this contain/have ___? (dairy / gluten / pork / alcohol / any allergens) |
| 买单 | **I want the bill, please.** | Sure. It's 185 yuan in total. | I want ___. (more water / some napkins / a fork / a takeaway box / the Wi-Fi password) |
| 问服务费 | **Is service charge included?** | Yes, it's already included. | Is ___ included? (tax / tip / breakfast / parking / Wi-Fi / the entrance fee) |
| 问选择 | **Do you have any vegetarian options?** | Yes, we have several. | Do you have any ___ options? (vegan / gluten-free / halal / kids' / non-spicy / local) |
| 要位置 | **Is there a table by the window?** | Of course. Right this way. | Is there a table ___? (outside / in the corner / at the bar / near the stage / somewhere quiet) |

### 🅲 社交与寒暄（5 主题，14 句块）

#### 第 5 课：认识新朋友

> 🧠 **社交问句链思维**：英语社交靠"问"来推进对话。从 **What do you do?** → **Where are you from?** → **What do you like?**，形成自然的"认识一个人"问句链。

| 主题 | 问句 | 自然回答 | 替换练习 |
|:----|:-----|:---------|:---------|
| 问职业 | **What do you do?** | I'm a student. / I work in a hospital. | What do you do? — I'm a ___. / I work in ___. (teacher / a bank / designer / IT / marketing) |
| 问来源 | **Where are you from?** | I'm from Chengdu. How about you? | Where are you from? — I'm from ___. (Beijing / Guangdong / Japan / a small town / the US) |
| 随意问候 | **How's it going?** | Pretty good! And you? | How's it going? — ___. (Not bad / Great / Can't complain / Same as usual / Busy but good) |
| 问爱好 | **What do you do for fun?** | I like watching movies and hiking. | What do you do ___? (on weekends / after work / in your free time / to relax / for exercise) |
| 问喜好 | **What kind of music do you like?** | I like pop and a little bit of jazz. | What kind of ___ do you like? (movies / food / books / sports / games / TV shows / animals) |
| 问兴趣 | **Do you like traveling?** | Yes, I love it! | Do you like ___? (cooking / hiking / reading / watching sports / trying new food / learning languages) |
| 问愿望 | **What countries do you want to visit?** | I want to go to Japan and France. | What ___ do you want to ___? (cities / visit / food / try / places / see / activities / do) |

#### 第 6 课：约见面与关心

> 🧠 **约见三步曲**：Are you free → Do you want to → What time / Where。

| 主题 | 问句 | 自然回答 | 替换练习 |
|:----|:-----|:---------|:---------|
| 约时间 | **Are you free this weekend?** | Yes, I am. Why? | Are you free ___? (tonight / tomorrow / after work / next Friday / on Sunday / this evening) |
| 提建议 | **Do you want to grab lunch together?** | I'd love to! | Do you want to ___? (get a coffee / go shopping / see a movie / take a walk / try that new place / hang out) |
| 确认时间 | **What time do you want to meet?** | How about 12:30? | What time do you want to ___? (meet / start / leave / arrive / have dinner / come over) |
| 确认地点 | **Where do you want to meet?** | Let's meet at the entrance. | Where do you want to ___? (meet / go / eat / sit / wait) |
| 问近况 | **How is your day?** | It's good! A little busy, but good. | How is ___? (your day / everything / your work / your family / your new job / life) |
| 表达关心 | **Is everything okay? You look tired.** | Yeah, I'm fine. Just didn't sleep well. | Is everything ___? (alright / okay / going well / ready for tomorrow / clear) |
| 主动帮助 | **Do you need help?** | That's very kind of you. I think I'm okay. | Do you need ___? (help / anything / a hand / some water / a break / directions) |

### 🅳 电话与联络（5 主题，14 句块）

#### 第 7 课：打电话找人

> 🧠 **电话思维**：Hello, this is... → Is ... there? → Who's calling? 三步走。

| 主题 | 问句 | 自然回答 | 替换练习 |
|:----|:-----|:---------|:---------|
| 找人 | **Hello, is Mr. Wang there, please?** | Speaking. / Just a moment, please. | Is ___ there? (the manager / your parents / the doctor / your colleague / someone in charge) |
| 问谁 | **Who's calling, please?** | This is Li Ming from ABC Company. | Who's calling? — This is ___. (your student / Tom's friend / the delivery person / the landlord) |
| 问是否在 | **Is Mr. Wang available?** | Sorry, he's in a meeting right now. | Is ___ available? (the doctor / your boss / the technician / customer service) |
| 问何时回 | **When is he back?** | He is back in about an hour. | When is ___ back? (she / the manager / your colleague / the doctor) |
| 问合适时间 | **I want to call back. When is a good time?** | Try after 3 pm. He is free then. | When is a good time to ___? (call / reach you / come by / have a meeting / chat) |
| 信号问题 | **Do you hear me? The signal is bad here.** | Yes, I hear you. Do you hear me now? | Do you ___ me? (hear / see / understand / follow) |
| 打错了 | **Sorry, I think you have the wrong number.** | Oh, I'm sorry. I'll check the number again. | Sorry, ___ (this isn't the hospital / there's no Tom here / I'm not the person you are looking for) |

#### 第 8 课：留言与跟进

> 🧠 **留言思维**：I want to leave a message. → Please ask him to call me back. → What's your number?

| 主题 | 问句 | 自然回答 | 替换练习 |
|:----|:-----|:---------|:---------|
| 留言 | **I want to leave a message. Is that okay?** | Sure. Go ahead. | I want to leave a message for ___. (Mr. Wang / the doctor / your boss / the manager) |
| 传话 | **Please take a message for me.** | Of course. What do you want me to tell him? | Please ___ a message. (take / pass on / send / leave) |
| 要求回电 | **Please ask him to call me back.** | Sure. What's your name and number? | Please ask him to ___. (call me / email me / text me / confirm the time) |
| 告知来电 | **Please tell him I called.** | I will. Don't worry. | Please tell him ___. (I called / I need to change the time / the meeting is at 3 / I sent an email) |
| 问知晓 | **Does he know I called?** | Yes, he does. He says he calls you tonight. | Does ___ know ___? (she / I called / the boss / about the meeting / your parents / you are here) |
| 二选一 | **Do I call back or wait for his call?** | He says he calls you, so just wait. | Do I ___ or ___? (wait / call again / email / text / come in person / try tomorrow) |
| 问联系方式 | **What's your phone number?** | It's 138-5555-1234. | What's your ___? (phone number / email address / WeChat / office number) |

### 🅴 应急与求助（5 主题，14 句块）

#### 第 9 课：紧急呼救

> 🧠 **紧急思维**：先短呼 Help! / Fire! → 再问 Where is...? / How do I...? / Is there...?

| 主题 | 问句 | 自然回答 | 替换练习 |
|:----|:-----|:---------|:---------|
| 紧急呼救 | **Help! Is anyone here?** | I hear you! What's wrong? | Help! ___! (I'm stuck / I've fallen / someone broke in / there's a fire / my child is missing) |
| 问医院 | **Where is the nearest hospital?** | It's about 3 kilometers from here. | Where is the nearest ___? (police station / fire station / emergency room / pharmacy / shelter / exit) |
| 问方法 | **How do I call an ambulance?** | Dial 120. Tell them your location. | How do I ___? (call the police / report a fire / get emergency help / find a doctor / contact my embassy) |
| 问人在否 | **Is there a doctor here?** | I'm a doctor. What happened? | Is there a ___ here? (nurse / police officer / security guard / translator / someone who speaks Chinese) |
| 确认报警 | **Is anyone calling the police?** | Yes, I call them right now. They are on their way. | Is anyone ___? (calling an ambulance / helping / looking for my child / reporting this / contacting his family) |
| 确认安全 | **Is everyone okay?** | I think so. One person is a little hurt. | Is everyone ___? (safe / accounted for / out of the building / okay / alright / still inside) |
| 问发生了什么 | **What happened? Tell me what you see.** | A car hits the pole over there. | What happened? Tell me ___? (what you see / who is here / when it happens / if anyone is hurt) |

#### 第 10 课：看病与求助

> 🧠 **看病思维**：先说自己（I don't feel well / I have a...）→ 再问（Where is? / What do I do? / Do I need?）。

| 主题 | 问句 | 自然回答 | 替换练习 |
|:----|:-----|:---------|:---------|
| 看病问诊 | **I don't feel well. Where is the nearest hospital?** | It's about 10 minutes by taxi. | I don't feel well. Where is ___? (a pharmacy / a clinic / a doctor / the emergency room) |
| 问怎么办 | **What do I do? I have a bad headache.** | You lie down and rest. Take some medicine. | What do I do? I have a ___. (fever / stomachache / sore throat / toothache / back pain / cold / cough) |
| 问是否需要 | **Do I need to see a doctor?** | If it doesn't get better in a day, yes. | Do I need to ___? (go to the hospital / take medicine / get an X-ray / call an ambulance / stay overnight) |
| 问药店 | **Is there a pharmacy open right now?** | Yes, there's a 24-hour pharmacy two blocks away. | Is there a ___ open? (doctor's office / clinic / hospital nearby / pharmacy that delivers) |
| 迷路求助 | **Please help me. I'm lost.** | Of course. Where do you want to go? | Please help me. ___ (I'm lost / my phone dies / I can't find my hotel / I don't speak the language / I miss my bus) |
| 语言困难 | **Do you speak Chinese?** | A little. Let me find someone who does. | Do you speak ___? / Does anyone speak ___? (English / Japanese / Korean / French / Spanish) |
| 问费用 | **How much does it cost to see a doctor here?** | The consultation fee is about 200 yuan. | How much does it cost to ___? (get a check-up / stay overnight / get an X-ray / fill this prescription / call an ambulance) |

## 附：疑问句结构速查卡

```
📐 疑问句基本结构

  Wh-词 + 助动词 + 主语 + 动词 + 其他？
  ─────   ─────   ───   ───   ───
  What    do      you   do?
  Where   does    he    live?
  When    is      the   meeting?
  How     do      I     get to the station?

💡 核心公式（记这一个就够了）：
  [Wh-] + [Do/Does/Is/Are] + [主语] + [动词] + [其他]?

📝 两大类型

  1️⃣  Yes/No 疑问句（回答 Yes/No）
      ─────────────────────────────────
      Do you like coffee?      → Yes, I do. / No, I don't.
      Does he speak Chinese?   → Yes, he does. / No, he doesn't.
      Is there a pharmacy?     → Yes, there is. / No, there isn't.
      Are you free tonight?    → Yes, I am. / No, I'm not.

  2️⃣  Wh- 疑问句（回答具体信息）
      ─────────────────────────────────
      What do you do?          → I'm a student.
      Where are you from?      → I'm from China.
      When is he back?         → He is back at 3.
      How much is this?        → It's 50 yuan.
      Which bus goes to...?    → Bus number 5.

⚠️ 中国学习者最常犯的错误：
  ❌ You are student?                ✅ Are you a student?
  ❌ Where you are from?              ✅ Where are you from?
  ❌ He like coffee?                  ✅ Does he like coffee?
  ❌ What he do?                      ✅ What does he do?
```

## 输出训练流水线（Warmup Pipeline）

每个训练主题配有 `warmup_pipeline.json` 输出训练流水线，包含题型：

| 题型 | 说明 | 示例 |
|:----|:-----|:-----|
| **question_drill (zh_to_en)** | 中文场景→英文问句 | 问最近的医院在哪。→ Where is the nearest hospital? |
| **question_drill (en_to_zh)** | 英文问句→中文理解 | How far is it from here? → 从这里有多远？ |

| **question_construction** | 用给定词构造问句 | you / like / coffee → Do you like coffee? |

## 设计检查清单

每个话题完成后，用这个清单自查：

```
□ 核心词 ≥ 4 个（与该场景话题直接语义相关）
□ 扩展词 ≤ 4 个（每个都能说清"服务于哪个问句/句型"）
□ 没有"不知道为什么在这里"的单词
□ 话题词数控制在 8-12 个
□ 本话题的问句槽位被核心词+扩展词覆盖
□ 扩展词优先复用包①/②/③ 或前面场景的核心词（累加原则）
□ 同语义类别的词集中在正确的话题（疑问词→Topic 01-05, 支付→Topic 08, 症状→Topic 23）
```

### 常见分配错误

| 错误类型 | 示例 | 正确做法 |
|----------|------|----------|
| 疑问词散落各处 | where 在 5 个场景都当核心词 | → 每个 Wh-词只在首次出现的场景作为核心词，后续作为扩展复用 |
| 地点词未集中 | hospital 在出行、应急都出现 | → 核心定义在 Topic 01（出行），Topic 23（应急）作为扩展复用 |
| 固定短语当词汇 | "How's it going" 列在词汇表 | → 社交问候是固定表达块，不作为独立核心词汇 |
| 非问句词汇混入 | "I want the bill" 中的 want 当核心词 | → want 在包②已学，本包仅作扩展复用 |
| 症状词分散 | headache 在应急、看病都列 | → 症状词集中到 Topic 23「看病问诊」 |
