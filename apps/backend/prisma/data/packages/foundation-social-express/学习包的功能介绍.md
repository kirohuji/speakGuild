# 基础⑥ · 一般过去时 & 完成时入门 · 动词变形与不规则动词（L1）

## 学习包概览

本学习包是 **Foundation 基础系列** 的 **第六包**。你将学习 **一般过去时（Past Simple）** 和 **现在完成时入门（Present Perfect Basics）**，并以 **动词变形** 为核心主线——规则动词的 -ed、不规则动词的过去式与过去分词。

> 💡 **为什么过去时和完成时放在一起？** 两种时态共享"过去分词"这个核心形式。规则动词（work→worked→worked）两种时态形式相同，不规则动词（go→went→gone）则需要同时记住过去式和过去分词。放在一起学，动词变形一次性打通。

### 本包核心语法

| 语法点 | 说明 | 示例 |
|:------|:-----|:-----|
| **be 动词过去式** | was / were | I was happy. They were at home. |
| **规则动词过去式** | 动词 + ed | I worked. She studied. They played. |
| **不规则动词过去式** | 30个核心不规则动词 | went / ate / had / did / saw / said / got / made |
| **否定句** | didn't + 动词原形 | I didn't go. She didn't eat. |
| **疑问句** | Did + 主语 + 动词原形 | Did you go? Did she eat? |
| **时间表达** | yesterday / last week / in 2020 / ago | I saw him yesterday. |
| **完成时结构** ⭐ | have/has + 过去分词 | I have been to Beijing. / She has lost her keys. |
| **完成时标记词** ⭐ | ever / never / just / already / yet / for / since | Have you ever...? / I haven't finished yet. / for 3 years / since 2020 |

> ⚠️ **完成时仅教最基础用法**（L1 级别）：① 经历 "去过/做过" (I have been to...)  ② 刚刚发生 (I have just...)  ③ 还没做 (I haven't...yet)  ④ 持续多久 (for/since)。不涉及与过去时的细微辨析——那个是 L2 的事。

### 适用对象

- **难度**：L1（零基础~初级）
- **前置要求**：一般现在时 + 现在进行时 + be动词
- **目标**：能用过去时描述经历，能用完成时表达"去过/做过/还没做"，掌握 30 个核心不规则动词的三种形式（原形→过去式→过去分词）

### 🅳 完成时入门 · 第 7 课：去过/做过（经历）

> 🧠 **核心思维**：一般过去时说"具体什么时候做了某事"（I went to Beijing **last year**），完成时说"有过这种经历，不强调时间"（I **have been** to Beijing）。完成时的结构永远是 **have/has + 过去分词**，动词用第三列形式。

| # | 句块（直接背） | 释义 | 语法点 | 替换练习 |
|:-:|---------------|------|:------|:---------|
| 41 | **I have been to Beijing.** | 我去过北京。 | have been to（去过某地） | I have been to ___. (Japan / the US / Hong Kong / the Great Wall / three countries) |
| 42 | **Have you ever been to Shanghai? — Yes, I have. / No, I haven't.** | 你去过上海吗？——去过。/ 没去过。 | Have you ever been to...? | Have you ever been to ___? (Paris / a concert / this restaurant / the beach at night / abroad) |
| 43 | **I have never tried hot pot.** | 我从来没试过火锅。 | have never + 过去分词 | I have never ___. (been abroad / driven a car / seen snow / eaten Indian food / ridden a horse) |
| 44 | **She has seen that movie three times.** | 那部电影她看过三次了。 | has + 过去分词 + 次数 | He/She has ___ + 次数. (read this book / twice / visited Japan / once / watched it / many times) |
| 45 | **Have you finished your homework? — Yes, I have. / Not yet.** | 你做完作业了吗？——做完了。/ 还没。 | Have you + 过去分词...? | Have you ___? (eaten / called her / seen this / read the news / thought about it / decided) |
| 46 | **I have lost my keys! Do you see them?** | 我钥匙丢了！你看到了吗？ | have lost（刚刚发生，结果在眼前） | I have ___! (lost my phone / forgotten my password / broken my glasses / found the answer / made a mistake) |
| 47 | **She has already left the office.** | 她已经离开办公室了。 | has already + 过去分词 | He/She has already ___. (finished / eaten / called / seen that movie / paid the bill / gone home) |

### 🅳 完成时入门 · 第 8 课：刚刚/还没/多久了

> 🧠 **核心思维**：完成时 + **just** = 刚刚（I have just arrived.） / + **yet** = 还没（I haven't eaten yet.） / + **for/since** = 持续了多久（I have lived here for 5 years.）。这三个是最常用的完成时时间标记。

| # | 句块（直接背） | 释义 | 语法点 | 替换练习 |
|:-:|---------------|------|:------|:---------|
| 48 | **I have just arrived at the station.** | 我刚到车站。 | have just + 过去分词（刚刚） | I have just ___. (finished / eaten / heard the news / talked to him / seen her / realized something) |
| 49 | **I haven't eaten lunch yet.** | 我还没吃午饭。 | haven't...yet（还没做） | I haven't ___ yet. (decided / finished / told him / started / gotten a reply / packed my bag) |
| 50 | **Have you eaten yet? — No, not yet. Let's eat together.** | 你吃了吗？——还没。一起吃吧。 | Have you...yet? — Not yet. | Have you ___ yet? (finished / called her / seen the doctor / booked the ticket / thought about it) |
| 51 | **I have lived here for five years.** | 我在这里住了五年了。 | have + 过去分词 + for + 时间段 | I have ___ for ___. (worked here / three years / known her / a long time / waited / an hour / studied English / two months) |
| 52 | **She has worked here since 2020.** | 她 2020 年以来一直在这里工作。 | have/has + 过去分词 + since + 时间点 | He has ___ since ___. (been a teacher / 2018 / played piano / he was 6 / lived alone / last year) |
| 53 | **How long have you been here? — For about an hour.** | 你在这里多久了？——大约一小时。 | How long have you...? | How long have you ___? (worked here / known him / waited / studied English / been married / felt this way) |
| 54 | **This is the best movie I have ever seen!** | 这是我看过的最好的电影！ | the best...I have ever... | This is the ___ I have ever ___. (best meal / had / most beautiful place / visited / hardest test / taken / funniest joke / heard) |
