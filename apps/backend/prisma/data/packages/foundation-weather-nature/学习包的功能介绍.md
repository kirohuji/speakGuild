# 基础⑩ · 基础补缺与综合运用 · 数字·时间·祈使句 · 礼貌表达（L1）

## 学习包概览

本学习包是 **Foundation 基础系列** 的 **第十包**，也是最后一包。它有两个使命：① **补缺**——把前面 9 个包没系统覆盖的基础知识点（数字、时间、星期、祈使句）补上；② **综合**——用前面学过的语法做一些更自然的日常表达。

> 💡 **为什么是补缺包？** 前 9 个包覆盖了语法主线，但有些"生存必需"的基础内容——数数、看时间、说日期、下指令——散落各处没有系统教。这一包集中补上，作为基础系列的收尾。

### 本包核心内容

| 类别 | 说明 | 示例 |
|:------|:-----|:-----|
| **数字与计数** | 1-100、序数词(first/second)、电话号码、价格 | one, two, three... / first, second, third... / It's 120 yuan. |
| **时间与日期** | 时刻表达、星期、月份、日期 | It's 3 o'clock. / on Monday / in July / January 1st |
| **祈使句** | 日常指令、提醒、建议 | Sit down. / Don't worry. / Let's go. / Please wait. |
| **礼貌表达** | 请求、提议、邀请 | Could you...? / Would you like...? / Let's... |

### 适用对象

- **难度**：L1（零基础~初级）
- **前置要求**：完成前 9 个基础包
- **目标**：补全数字/时间/日期/祈使句四项基础生存技能，能用更礼貌的方式表达请求和提议

### 🅰️ 数字与计数 · 第 1 课：数字 1-100

> 🧠 **规律速记**：1-12 没规律，死记。13-19 加 -teen（thirteen, fourteen...）。20-90 整十加 -ty（twenty, thirty...）。"几十几" = 整十 + 个位（twenty-one, thirty-five）。

| # | 句块（直接背） | 释义 | 语法点 |
|:-:|---------------|------|:------|
| 1 | **one, two, three, four, five, six, seven, eight, nine, ten** | 1-10（必须背） | 基数词 1-10 |
| 2 | **eleven, twelve, thirteen, fourteen, fifteen, sixteen, seventeen, eighteen, nineteen** | 11-19（注意 13/15 拼写） | 基数词 11-19（-teen） |
| 3 | **twenty, thirty, forty, fifty, sixty, seventy, eighty, ninety, one hundred** | 20-100 整十 | 整十（-ty）；注意 forty 没有 u |
| 4 | **twenty-one, thirty-five, sixty-eight, ninety-nine** | 21, 35, 68, 99 | 几十几 = 整十-个位 |
| 5 | **I am 25 years old.** | 我 25 岁。 | 年龄 = I am + 数字 + years old |
| 6 | **My phone number is 138-5555-1234.** | 我的电话号码是 138-5555-1234。 | 电话号码逐位读（one-three-eight...） |
| 7 | **It's 120 yuan.** / **It's 50 dollars.** | 120 元。/ 50 美元。 | 价格 = It's + 数字 + yuan/dollars |

| # | 句块（直接背） | 释义 | 语法点 | 替换练习 |
|:-:|---------------|------|:------|:---------|
| 8 | **first, second, third, fourth, fifth, sixth** | 第一 ~ 第六 | 序数词 1-6（前三个不规则） | the ___ one (first / second / third / fourth / fifth) |
| 9 | **It's on the second floor.** | 在二楼。 | on the + 序数词 + floor | It's on the ___ floor. (first / third / fifth / tenth) |
| 10 | **This is my first time here.** | 这是我第一次来这里。 | my + 序数词 + time | This is my ___ time. (second / third / first / last) |

| 13 | **I have two brothers and one sister.** | 我有两个兄弟和一个妹妹。 | 数字 + 名词（数量表达） | I have ___ and ___. (three cats / one dog / two phones / one computer / five books / one pen) |

### 🅱️ 时间与日期 · 第 3 课：时刻表达

> 🧠 **时间表达公式**：整点 = ...o'clock / 几点半 = half past... / 几点一刻 = quarter past... / 差一刻几点 = quarter to...

| # | 句块（直接背） | 释义 | 语法点 | 替换练习 |
|:-:|---------------|------|:------|:---------|
| 15 | **What time is it? — It's 3 o'clock.** | 几点了？——三点。 | What time is it? — It's... | What time is it? — It's ___ o'clock. (1 / 5 / 7 / 10 / 12) |
| 16 | **It's half past seven. (7:30)** | 七点半。 | half past + 整点 | It's half past ___. (one / three / six / nine / twelve) |
| 17 | **It's a quarter past eight. (8:15)** | 八点一刻。 | a quarter past + 整点 | It's a quarter past ___. (two / five / ten / eleven) |
| 18 | **It's a quarter to nine. (8:45)** | 差一刻九点。 | a quarter to + 整点 | It's a quarter to ___. (three / six / ten / twelve) |
| 19 | **I get up at 7 o'clock every morning.** | 我每天早上 7 点起床。 | at + 时间 | I ___ at ___ o'clock. (go to bed / 11 / have lunch / 12 / come home / 6 / start work / 9) |
| 20 | **The meeting starts at 2:30 pm.** | 会议下午 2:30 开始。 | at + 时间 + am/pm | The ___ starts at ___. (movie / 7 pm / class / 9 am / party / 8 pm / show / 6:30 pm) |
| 21 | **It's 5:20. We're late!** | 5:20 了。我们迟到了！ | 数字时间（直接读：five twenty） | It's ___. We're ___! (8:45 / almost there / 3:10 / on time / 9:55 / really late / 12:05 / just in time) |

| 23 | **January, February, March, April, May, June, July, August, September, October, November, December** | 一月 ~ 十二月 | 月份（首字母大写） | in ___ (January / March / July / October / December) |

### 🅲 祈使句 · 第 5 课：日常指令

> 🧠 **祈使句公式**：动词原形开头 = 命令/请求（Sit down.）/ Don't + 动词原形 = 禁止（Don't worry.）/ Let's + 动词原形 = 提议（Let's go.）

| # | 句块（直接背） | 释义 | 语法点 | 替换练习 |
|:-:|---------------|------|:------|:---------|
| 29 | **Sit down, please.** | 请坐。 | 动词原形（命令/请求） | ___ please. (Stand up / Come in / Open the door / Close the window / Wait here / Listen to me) |
| 30 | **Don't worry. Everything is fine.** | 别担心。一切都好。 | Don't + 动词原形（禁止/劝慰） | Don't ___. (worry / be late / forget / be sad / give up / touch that / tell anyone) |
| 31 | **Let's go! We're late.** | 走吧！我们迟到了。 | Let's + 动词原形（提议） | Let's ___! (go / eat / start / dance / take a break / try it / call her / watch a movie) |
| 32 | **Please wait a moment. I'll be right back.** | 请稍等。我马上回来。 | Please + 动词原形（礼貌请求） | Please ___. (wait / come in / sit here / fill out this form / sign here / follow me / help yourself) |
| 33 | **Look! The bus is coming.** | 看！公交车来了。 | Look! / Listen!（引起注意） | Look! ___ / Listen! ___ (the bird is singing / someone is calling you / it's snowing / I have something to tell you / the baby is crying) |
| 34 | **Be careful! The floor is wet.** | 小心！地板是湿的。 | Be + 形容词（提醒） | Be ___! (careful / quiet / patient / brave / honest / on time / safe) |
| 35 | **Have a seat. Make yourself at home.** | 请坐。别客气。 | Have a + 名词 / Make yourself + 形容词 | Have a ___. / Make yourself ___. (drink / comfortable / good day / at home / nice trip / ready / safe journey / presentable) |

### 🅳 礼貌表达 · 第 7 课：请求与提议

| # | 句块（直接背） | 释义 | 语法点 | 替换练习 |
|:-:|---------------|------|:------|:---------|
| 43 | **Could you help me, please? — Of course.** | 能请你帮我一下吗？——当然。 | Could you + 动词？（礼貌请求） | Could you ___? (open the window / pass me the salt / tell me the time / wait a moment / repeat that / speak more slowly) |
| 44 | **Would you like some coffee? — Yes, please. / No, thank you.** | 来点咖啡吗？——好的。/ 不了，谢谢。 | Would you like + 名词？（礼貌提供） | Would you like ___? (some tea / a cookie / more water / something to eat / a seat / help with that) |
| 45 | **Shall I open the window? — Yes, please.** | 要我开窗吗？——好的。 | Shall I + 动词？（主动提供） | Shall I ___? (turn on the light / get you a drink / call a taxi / help you / wait for you / carry that for you) |
| 46 | **Let me help you with that.** | 让我帮你拿。 | Let me + 动词（主动帮忙） | Let me ___. (help you / carry that / open the door / show you / explain / check / clean this up / pay for this) |
| 47 | **I'd like a cup of coffee, please.** | 我想要一杯咖啡。 | I'd like + 名词（委婉表达意愿） | I'd like ___. (a glass of water / the menu / to order / to pay / to make a reservation / a table for two) |

| 49 | **I was wondering if you could help me.** | 我在想你能不能帮我。（极其委婉） | I was wondering if you could... | I was wondering if you could ___. (take a look / give me advice / lend me a pen / check this / tell me more about it) |

### 🅳 礼貌表达 · 第 8 课：邀请与回应

| # | 句块（直接背） | 释义 | 语法点 | 替换练习 |
|:-:|---------------|------|:------|:---------|
| 50 | **Would you like to join us for dinner? — I'd love to!** | 你愿意和我们一起吃晚饭吗？——我很乐意！ | Would you like to + 动词？（礼貌邀请） | Would you like to ___? (come with us / watch a movie / go for a walk / try this dish / sit here / dance / have a drink) |

## 附：基础数字与时间速查卡

### 数字 1-100 速查

```
1-10:    one two three four five six seven eight nine ten
11-19:   eleven twelve thirteen fourteen fifteen sixteen seventeen eighteen nineteen
20-90:   twenty thirty forty fifty sixty seventy eighty ninety
组合:    几十-个位 (twenty-one, thirty-five, ninety-nine)
100:     one hundred
1,000:   one thousand
```

### 时刻表达速查

```
整点:     It's 3 o'clock.
半点:     It's half past 3. (3:30)
一刻:     It's a quarter past 3. (3:15)
差一刻:   It's a quarter to 4. (3:45)
数字直接: It's three twenty. (3:20)
上午/下午: 3 am / 3 pm
```

### 星期与月份速查

```
星期:  Monday Tuesday Wednesday Thursday Friday Saturday Sunday
月份:  Jan Feb Mar Apr May Jun Jul Aug Sep Oct Nov Dec
日期:  on Monday / in July / on May 1st (May first) / in 2024 (twenty twenty-four)
```
