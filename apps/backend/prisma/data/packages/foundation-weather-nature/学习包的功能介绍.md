# 基础⑩ · 综合表达 · 喜好与建议 · 不定式与动名词 · 完成时基础 · 礼貌请求（L1~L2）

## 学习包概览

本学习包是 **Foundation 基础系列** 的 **第十包**，也是最后一包。你将综合运用前九包学到的所有知识，并学习一些更高频的实用结构——不定式与动名词、现在完成时基础、礼貌请求等。

> 💡 **最后一包 = 升维**：前九包你学会了各种"基本句"，这一包让你学会"更自然的表达"——I like swimming（动名词）、I want to go（不定式）、I have been to Beijing（完成时）、Could you help me?（礼貌请求）。这些是让你从"课本英语"走向"真实英语"的桥梁。

### 本包核心语法

| 语法点 | 说明 | 示例 |
|:------|:-----|:-----|
| **like / enjoy / hate + doing** | 动名词作宾语 | I like swimming. / I enjoy reading. / I hate waiting. |
| **want / need / hope + to do** | 不定式作宾语 | I want to go. / I need to study. / I hope to see you. |
| **would like to** | 委婉表达意愿 | I would like to order. / Would you like some coffee? |
| **could / would 礼貌请求** | 更礼貌的请求 | Could you help me? / Would you mind...? |
| **现在完成时基础** | have/has + 过去分词 | I have been to Beijing. / Have you ever...? |
| **for / since** | 持续时间的表达 | for two years / since 2020 |
| **综合运用** | 混合时态/结构 | 在真实交流中自然切换 |

### 🅰️ 喜好与活动 · 第 1 课：我喜欢做...

| # | 句块（直接背） | 释义 | 语法点 | 替换练习 |
|:-:|---------------|------|:------|:---------|
| 1 | **I like swimming.** | 我喜欢游泳。 | like + doing（动名词） | I like ___. (reading / cooking / traveling / dancing / painting) |
| 2 | **I enjoy reading books.** | 我享受读书。 | enjoy + doing（必须接doing） | I enjoy ___. (listening to music / watching movies / hiking / playing chess) |
| 3 | **I hate waiting in line.** | 我讨厌排队。 | hate + doing | I hate ___. (getting up early / being late / doing homework / cleaning) |
| 4 | **I love playing basketball.** | 我热爱打篮球。 | love + doing | I love ___. (singing / taking photos / trying new food / spending time with family) |
| 5 | **I don't mind working late.** | 我不介意加班。 | don't mind + doing | I don't mind ___. (waiting / helping / cooking / driving / walking) |
| 6 | **I'm interested in learning Japanese.** | 我对学日语感兴趣。 | be interested in + doing | I'm interested in ___. (studying abroad / photography / joining the club / volunteering) |
| 7 | **I'm good at cooking.** | 我擅长做饭。 | be good at + doing | I'm good at ___. (drawing / math / fixing things / remembering names / telling stories) |

### 🅰️ 喜好与活动 · 第 2 课：我想做/需要做

| # | 句块（直接背） | 释义 | 语法点 | 替换练习 |
|:-:|---------------|------|:------|:---------|
| 8 | **I want to go home.** | 我想回家。 | want + to do（不定式） | I want to ___. (travel / sleep / eat / learn Spanish / buy a new phone) |
| 9 | **I need to study for the exam.** | 我需要为考试学习。 | need + to do | I need to ___. (finish this / call my mom / take a break / find a job / save money) |
| 10 | **I hope to see you soon.** | 我希望很快见到你。 | hope + to do | I hope to ___. (visit Japan / pass the test / get better / hear from you / meet your family) |
| 11 | **I plan to move to Shanghai next year.** | 我计划明年搬到上海。 | plan + to do | I plan to ___. (start a business / get married / change jobs / learn to drive / retire early) |
| 12 | **I decided to stay home today.** | 我决定今天待在家。 | decide + to do | I decided to ___. (quit my job / go back to school / tell the truth / wait a bit longer / buy the red one) |
| 13 | **I forgot to bring my keys.** | 我忘了带钥匙。 | forget + to do（忘记要做的事） | I forgot to ___. (call you / lock the door / buy milk / do my homework / turn off the light) |
| 14 | **I tried to call you, but no one answered.** | 我试着打给你，但没人接。 | try + to do（努力去做） | I tried to ___. (open the door / fix it / explain / warn you / learn French) |

### 🅱️ 礼貌与请求 · 第 3 课：请你帮我...

| # | 句块（直接背） | 释义 | 语法点 | 替换练习 |
|:-:|---------------|------|:------|:---------|
| 15 | **Could you help me, please?** | 请问你能帮我吗？ | Could you...?（比 Can you 更礼貌） | Could you ___? (open the window / pass me the menu / explain this / wait a moment) |
| 16 | **Would you mind closing the door?** | 你介意关一下门吗？ | Would you mind + doing?（最礼貌） | Would you mind ___? (turning down the music / repeating that / moving your car / waiting outside) |
| 17 | **Can I help you with that?** | 需要我帮你吗？ | Can I help...?（主动提供帮助） | Can I help ___? (you carry that / you find something / with the dishes / clean up) |
| 18 | **Could I have a moment of your time?** | 我能占用你一点时间吗？ | Could I have...?（正式请求） | Could I have ___? (the bill / your attention / a glass of water / a word with you) |
| 19 | **Would you like me to drive?** | 你希望我来开车吗？ | Would you like me to...?（提供帮助） | Would you like me to ___? (pick you up / cook dinner / call them / explain again / come with you) |
| 20 | **I was wondering if you could help me.** | 我在想你能不能帮我。（极其委婉） | I was wondering if you could... | I was wondering if you could ___. (take a look / give me some advice / lend me your pen / check this for me) |
| 21 | **Do you mind if I sit here? — Not at all.** | 你介意我坐这儿吗？——完全不。 | Do you mind if I...? | Do you mind if I ___? (open the window / use your phone / join you / leave early / ask a question) |

### 🅱️ 礼貌与请求 · 第 4 课：你想要...吗？

| # | 句块（直接背） | 释义 | 语法点 | 替换练习 |
|:-:|---------------|------|:------|:---------|
| 22 | **Would you like some coffee? — Yes, please.** | 你想要点咖啡吗？——好的，请。 | Would you like + 名词？（礼貌询问） | Would you like ___? (some tea / a cookie / more water / a seat / something to eat) |
| 23 | **Would you like to join us for dinner?** | 你愿意和我们一起吃晚饭吗？ | Would you like to do?（礼貌邀请） | Would you like to ___? (come with us / watch a movie / go for a walk / try this dish / sit down) |
| 24 | **What would you like to drink? — Water, please.** | 你想喝什么？——水，谢谢。 | What would you like to...? | What would you like to ___? (eat / order / do / watch / talk about) |
| 25 | **How about going to the movies tonight?** | 今晚去看电影怎么样？ | How about + doing?（建议） | How about ___? (eating out / taking a break / trying this new place / calling her / going for a walk) |
| 26 | **Why don't we take a short break?** | 我们为什么不休息一下呢？ | Why don't we...?（提议） | Why don't we ___? (go together / have lunch / start early / ask for help / try again) |
| 27 | **Let's meet at 7, shall we?** | 我们 7 点见，好吗？ | Let's..., shall we? | Let's ___, shall we? (go / begin / order / decide / split the bill / call it a day) |
| 28 | **Shall I open the window?** | 要我开窗吗？ | Shall I...?（主动提供） | Shall I ___? (turn on the light / get you a drink / call a taxi / help you / wait for you) |

### 🅲 经历与持续 · 第 5 课：我去过/做过

| # | 句块（直接背） | 释义 | 语法点 | 替换练习 |
|:-:|---------------|------|:------|:---------|
| 29 | **I have been to Beijing.** | 我去过北京。 | have been to（去过某地） | I have been to ___. (Japan / the US / Hong Kong / the Great Wall / three countries) |
| 30 | **Have you ever been to Shanghai? — Yes, I have.** | 你去过上海吗？——去过。 | Have you ever been to...? | Have you ever been to ___? (Paris / a concert / this restaurant / the beach at night) |
| 31 | **I have never tried sushi.** | 我从来没试过寿司。 | have never + 过去分词 | I have never ___. (been abroad / driven a car / seen snow / eaten Indian food) |
| 32 | **She has already finished her homework.** | 她已经完成了作业。 | has already + 过去分词 | He has already ___. (left / eaten / called / seen that movie / paid the bill) |
| 33 | **I haven't eaten lunch yet.** | 我还没吃午饭。 | haven't...yet | I haven't ___ yet. (decided / finished / told him / started / gotten a reply / packed my bag) |

### 🅲 经历与持续 · 第 6 课：已经...多久了

| # | 句块（直接背） | 释义 | 语法点 | 替换练习 |
|:-:|---------------|------|:------|:---------|
| 36 | **I have lived here for five years.** | 我在这里住了五年了。 | have + 过去分词 + for + 时间段 | I have ___ for ___. (worked here for three years / known her for a long time / waited for an hour / studied English for two months) |
| 37 | **She has worked here since 2020.** | 她 2020 年以来一直在这里工作。 | have/has + 过去分词 + since + 时间点 | He has ___ since ___. (been a teacher since 2018 / played piano since he was 6 / lived alone since last year) |
| 38 | **How long have you been here? — For about an hour.** | 你在这里多久了？——大约一小时。 | How long have you...? | How long have you ___? (worked here / known him / waited / studied English / been married) |
| 39 | **This is the best movie I have ever seen.** | 这是我看过的最好的电影。 | the best...I have ever... | This is the ___ I have ever ___. (best meal I have ever had / most beautiful place I have ever visited / hardest test I have ever taken) |
| 40 | **I have known her since we were kids.** | 我从小就认识她了。 | have known + since + 过去从句 | I have ___ since ___. (liked music since I was young / wanted to be a doctor since I was a child / lived here since I was born) |
