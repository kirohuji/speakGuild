# 条件句与虚拟语气 · Zero/First/Second/Third Conditional · wish / if only（L2~L4）

## 学习包概览

本学习包是 **中级语法系列** 的重要组成部分，聚焦英语 **条件句（Conditionals）** 和 **虚拟语气（Subjunctive）** 的核心用法。**L2~L4（中级基础~高级）** 定位，精选 **40+ 个条件句句块（chunks）**，通过大量真实场景对比，帮助学习者掌握从"真实可能"到"虚拟相反"的完整条件句体系。

> 条件句是英语逻辑表达的核心工具。中文条件句只有一个"如果"，英文却有四种条件句 + 虚拟语气——每一种表达不同的"可能性程度"。没有掌握条件句，你就无法准确表达"可能发生""不可能发生""本可能发生但没发生"这些关键的区别。

### 中国学习者的常见痛点

| 中文 | 常见错误 | 正确用法 |
|------|---------|----------|
| 如果明天下雨，我就不去了。 | "If it will rain tomorrow, I won't go." | **If it rains tomorrow, I won't go.**（条件句不用 will） |
| 如果我是你，我就去。 | "If I am you, I will go." | **If I were you, I would go.**（虚拟用 were） |
| 如果我当时知道，我就来了。 | "If I know, I will come." | **If I had known, I would have come.**（过去相反） |
| 我希望我是你。 | "I wish I am you." | **I wish I were you.**（wish 后用过去时） |
| 除非你来，否则我不去。 | "Except you come, I won't go." | **I won't go unless you come.**（unless = if not） |

### 句块（chunk）vs 句型（pattern）

| | 句块（chunk） | 句型（pattern） |
|--|-------------|----------------|
| **是什么** | 可直接背诵的 **完整句子**（含条件+结果） | 带替换槽位的 **句型框架** |
| **作用** | 在语境中掌握条件句语感 | 替换条件/结果，举一反三 |
| **例子** | `If it rains, I'll stay home.` | `If it ___, I'll ___.`（slot: rains / stay home） |
| **对应文件** | `chunks.csv` | `sentence_patterns.csv` |

> **学习路径**：先背句块（建立条件句语感）→ 再练句型（替换条件/结果）→ 最终自由运用。

### 句块设计原则

| 原则 | 说明 | ✅ 好句块 | ❌ 差句块 |
|------|------|----------|----------|
| **完整可背** | 完整 if + 结果句子 | `If I had money, I would travel.` | `if + had`（孤立语法） |
| **场景实用** | 日常交流用得上 | `If you don't hurry, you'll miss the bus.` | 生造例句 |
| **对比清晰** | 同一场景不同条件对比 | `If I have time...` vs `If I had time...` | 只有一种情况 |
| **难度递进** | 从真实→虚拟 | Zero → First → Second → Third | 只做一种 |

### 适用对象

- **难度**：L2~L4（中级基础~高级）
- **前置要求**：掌握一般现在时、一般过去时、现在完成时
- **目标学习者**：希望准确表达条件逻辑和虚拟假设的学习者

### 包含场景

| 场景 | 核心语法 | 句块数 | 难度 |
|------|---------|:------:|:----:|
| 条件·零条件与第一条件 | 普遍真理 + 真实可能 | ~10 | L2 |
| 条件·第二条件 | 与现在/将来事实相反 | ~8 | L3 |
| 条件·第三条件 | 与过去事实相反 | ~8 | L3~L4 |
| 条件·wish 愿望句 | 表达愿望和遗憾 | ~8 | L3 |
| 条件·unless 与替代表达 | unless / as long as / provided / otherwise | ~8 | L2~L3 |
| **总计** | | **~42** | |

### 学习目标

完成本包学习后，学习者能够：

1. **零条件**：用 If + 现在时 + 现在时 表达普遍真理和习惯性结果
2. **第一条件**：用 If + 现在时 + will + 动词 表达真实可能的未来情况
3. **第二条件**：用 If + 过去时 + would + 动词 表达与现在/将来事实相反的假设
4. **第三条件**：用 If + 过去完成时 + would have + 过去分词 表达与过去事实相反的遗憾
5. **wish 愿望句**：用 wish + 过去时/过去完成时 表达对现在和过去的愿望与遗憾
6. **unless 与替代**：用 unless / as long as / provided / otherwise 丰富条件表达

## 句块参考（供数据生成用）

每行 = 一个完整句子，可直接写入 `chunks.csv`。`sentence_patterns.csv` 中的句型需另行从这些句块中提取替换槽位。

### 一、零条件（L2）—— 普遍真理 / 习惯结果

> 结构：If + 一般现在时, 一般现在时  |  用法：100% 确定

| # | 句块（直接背） | 释义 | 功能 | 替换练习 |
|:-:|---------------|------|:----:|:---------|
| 1 | **If you heat ice, it melts.** | 如果你加热冰，它就融化。 | 普遍真理 | If you ___ (heat water, it boils) |
| 2 | **If I don't sleep well, I feel tired the next day.** | 如果我睡不好，第二天就觉得累。 | 习惯结果 | If I ___ (eat too much, I feel sick) |
| 3 | **If you mix red and blue, you get purple.** | 如果你把红色和蓝色混合，得到紫色。 | 颜色混合 | If you mix ___ and ___, you get ___. |
| 4 | **If it rains, the ground gets wet.** | 如果下雨，地面就湿了。 | 自然规律 | If ___ (it snows, everything turns white) |
| 5 | **If I am late, my boss gets angry.** | 如果我迟到，老板会生气。 | 因果关系 | If ___ (I don't call, my mom worries) |
| 6 | **If you don't water plants, they die.** | 如果你不给植物浇水，它们就死了。 | 必要条件 | If you don't ___ (eat, you get hungry) |

### 二、第一条件（L2~L3）—— 真实可能

> 结构：If + 一般现在时, will/won't + 动词  |  用法：可能发生（50-90%）

| # | 句块（直接背） | 释义 | 功能 | 替换练习 |
|:-:|---------------|------|:----:|:---------|
| 7 | **If it rains tomorrow, I will stay home.** | 如果明天下雨，我就待在家。 | 真实条件 | If ___ (I have time, I'll help you) |
| 8 | **If you study hard, you will pass the exam.** | 如果你努力学习，你会通过考试。 | 鼓励 | If ___ (you practice daily, you'll improve) |
| 9 | **I will call you if I need help.** | 如果我需要帮助，我会打给你。 | if 在后 | I will ___ if ___ (come, I can make it) |
| 10 | **If you don't hurry, you will miss the bus.** | 如果你不快点，会错过公交。 | 否定条件 | If you don't ___ (leave now, you'll be late) |
| 11 | **She won't get the job if she doesn't prepare well.** | 如果她不好好准备，就得不到工作。 | 否定结果 | ___ won't ___ if ___ (He, succeed) |
| 12 | **If he arrives early, we can start the meeting sooner.** | 如果他早到，我们可以早点开会。 | 情态+条件 | If ___, we can ___ (everyone is ready, begin) |

### 三、第二条件（L3）—— 与现在/将来相反

> 结构：If + 一般过去时, would/could/might + 动词  |  用法：不太可能 / 与事实相反

| # | 句块（直接背） | 释义 | 功能 | 替换练习 |
|:-:|---------------|------|:----:|:---------|
| 13 | **If I had more money, I would travel around the world.** | 如果我有更多钱，我会环游世界。 | 与现在相反 | If I had ___, I would ___. (a car, drive to work) |
| 14 | **If I were you, I would accept the offer.** | 如果我是你，我会接受这个提议。 | 建议 | If I were you, I would ___ (take the job) |
| 15 | **If she knew the truth, she would be very sad.** | 如果她知道真相，她会很伤心。 | 假设结果 | If ___ knew ___, ___ would ___. (he) |
| 16 | **I would buy a house if I had enough money.** | 如果我有足够的钱，我会买房子。 | if 在后 | I would ___ if I had ___ (travel, more vacation) |
| 17 | **If he lived closer, he could visit us more often.** | 如果他住得近，他可以更常来看我们。 | could 可能 | If ___, ___ could ___ (we had a car, go camping) |
| 18 | **If I had a better job, I might move to a bigger city.** | 如果我有更好的工作，我可能会搬到大城市。 | might 可能 | If ___, ___ might ___ (I knew the answer, tell you) |

### 四、第三条件（L3~L4）—— 与过去相反

> 结构：If + 过去完成时, would/could/might have + 过去分词  |  用法：后悔 / 错过机会

| # | 句块（直接背） | 释义 | 功能 | 替换练习 |
|:-:|---------------|------|:----:|:---------|
| 19 | **If I had studied harder, I would have passed the exam.** | 如果我更努力学习，我就通过考试了。 | 后悔学习 | If I had ___, I would have ___ (prepared) |
| 20 | **If she had left earlier, she wouldn't have missed the train.** | 如果她早点出发，就不会错过火车。 | 后悔迟到 | If ___ had ___, ___ wouldn't have ___ |
| 21 | **If we had saved more money, we could have bought a house.** | 如果我们多存点钱，就能买房了。 | 错过机会 | If we had ___, we could have ___ (invested) |
| 22 | **I would have called you if I had known you were sick.** | 如果我知道你生病了，我就打给你了。 | if 在后 | I would have ___ if I had ___ (come, known) |
| 23 | **If he had taken the medicine, he might have recovered sooner.** | 如果他吃了药，他可能恢复得更快。 | might have | If he had ___, he might have ___ (listened) |
| 24 | **If they had asked for help, I would have been happy to assist.** | 如果他们请求帮助，我会很乐意帮忙的。 | 善意假设 | If they had ___, I would have ___ (told me) |

### 五、wish 愿望句（L3）—— 愿望与遗憾

> 结构：wish + 过去时（现在愿望）/ wish + 过去完成时（过去遗憾）

| # | 句块（直接背） | 释义 | 功能 | 替换练习 |
|:-:|---------------|------|:----:|:---------|
| 25 | **I wish I were taller.** | 我希望我更高一点。 | 现在愿望（外表） | I wish I were ___ (richer / thinner / younger) |
| 26 | **I wish I lived near the beach.** | 我希望我住在海边。 | 现在愿望（地点） | I wish I ___ (had a car / could fly / spoke French) |
| 27 | **She wishes she had more time to travel.** | 她希望有更多时间旅行。 | 第三人称愿望 | He wishes he ___ (knew the answer / were here) |
| 28 | **I wish it would stop raining.** | 我希望雨能停。 | 将来愿望 | I wish ___ would ___ (they, hurry up / you, listen) |
| 29 | **I wish I had studied harder in school.** | 我希望我上学时更努力学习。 | 过去遗憾 | I wish I had ___ (listened to my parents / saved more) |
| 30 | **She wishes she hadn't said that.** | 她希望她没说过那句话。 | 后悔说了 | I wish I hadn't ___ (eaten so much / been so rude) |
| 31 | **I wish I could go with you, but I can't.** | 我希望我能和你一起去，但我不能。 | 不能实现 | I wish I could ___ (help, but I'm busy) |
| 32 | **If only I had known the truth earlier!** | 要是我早知道真相就好了！ | if only 强调 | If only ___ had ___ (I, listened / we, left earlier) |

### 六、unless 与替代表达（L2~L3）

> unless / as long as / provided / even if / otherwise

| # | 句块（直接背） | 释义 | 功能 | 替换练习 |
|:-:|---------------|------|:----:|:---------|
| 33 | **I won't go unless you come with me.** | 除非你跟我一起去，否则我不去。 | unless = if not | I won't ___ unless ___ (call, it's urgent) |
| 34 | **You can't enter unless you have a ticket.** | 除非你有票，否则不能入场。 | 必要条件 | You can't ___ unless ___ (join, you're invited) |
| 35 | **You can borrow my car as long as you drive carefully.** | 只要你小心开，就可以借我的车。 | as long as = 只要 | You can ___ as long as ___ (stay, you keep quiet) |
| 36 | **I'll go provided that everyone else agrees.** | 只要其他人都同意，我就去。 | provided that = 假如 | I'll ___ provided that ___ (help, you pay me) |
| 37 | **I'll still love you even if you make mistakes.** | 即使你犯错，我仍然爱你。 | even if = 即使 | I'll ___ even if ___ (finish, it takes all night) |
| 38 | **Hurry up, otherwise we'll be late.** | 快点，否则我们要迟到了。 | otherwise = 否则 | Study hard, otherwise ___ (you'll fail) |
| 39 | **I'd better leave now, otherwise I'll miss the train.** | 我最好现在走，否则赶不上火车。 | otherwise 实用 | We should leave now, otherwise ___ (traffic) |
| 40 | **Whether you like it or not, we have to do it.** | 不管你喜欢与否，我们都得做。 | whether...or not | Whether ___ or not, ___ (you agree, I'll go) |

## 句型模板（供 `sentence_patterns.csv` 使用）

| # | 句型 | 替换槽 | 示例 | 条件类型 |
|:-:|:----|:-------|:-----|:---------|
| 1 | If you ___ (动词现在), it ___ (动词现在). | heat ice / melts | If you heat ice, it melts. | 零条件 |
| 2 | If I ___ (动词现在), I ___ (动词现在). | am late / gets angry | If I am late, my boss gets angry. | 零条件 |
| 3 | If it ___ (动词现在), I will ___ (动词). | rains / stay home | If it rains, I will stay home. | 第一条件 |
| 4 | If you ___ (动词现在), you will ___ (动词). | study hard / pass | If you study hard, you'll pass. | 第一条件 |
| 5 | If I ___ (动词过去), I would ___ (动词). | had money / travel | If I had money, I'd travel. | 第二条件 |
| 6 | If I were you, I would ___ (动词). | accept the offer | If I were you, I'd accept. | 第二条件 |
| 7 | If I had ___ (过去分词), I would have ___ (过去分词). | studied / passed | If I had studied, I'd have passed. | 第三条件 |
| 8 | I would have ___ if I had ___ . | called / known | I'd have called if I'd known. | 第三条件 |
| 9 | I wish I ___ (过去). | were taller | I wish I were taller. | wish 现在 |
| 10 | I wish I had ___ (过去分词). | studied harder | I wish I had studied harder. | wish 过去 |
| 11 | I won't ___ unless you ___ . | go / come | I won't go unless you come. | unless |
| 12 | You can ___ as long as you ___ . | borrow / drive carefully | You can borrow as long as you drive carefully. | as long as |
| 13 | ___, otherwise ___ . | Hurry up / we'll be late | Hurry up, otherwise we'll be late. | otherwise |
| 14 | I'll ___ even if ___ . | still love you / you make mistakes | I'll still love you even if you make mistakes. | even if |
