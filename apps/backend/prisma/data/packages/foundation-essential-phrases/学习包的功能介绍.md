# 基础④ · 疑问句实战 · 出行·消费·社交·电话·应急（L1）

## 学习包概览

本学习包是 **Foundation 基础系列** 的 **第四包**，也是整个基础阶段**最重要的口语能力包**。前三个包你学会了造句，这一包你学会**提问**——真正的对话是问答互动，不会问 = 不会聊。

> 💡 **为什么单独一个包？** 中文疑问句加个"吗"就行。英语要**疑问词前置 + 助动词倒装**——"Where are you from?" 不是 "You from where?"。这是中国学习者最核心的思维转换，必须大量场景化练习形成条件反射。

### 设计理念：场景驱动，而非语法分类

传统教材按语法分类教疑问句（Yes/No 问句 → Wh- 问句 → How 问句），学生学完还是不会在实际场景中提问。本包反过来——**按"你在什么场景下需要问什么"来组织**：

| 你身处… | 你需要会问… |
|:---------|:------------|
| 🅰️ 街头/车站 | Where is...? / Which bus...? / How far...? |
| 🅱️ 商店/餐厅 | How much...? / Do you have...? / What do you recommend? |
| 🅲 社交场合 | What do you do? / Where are you from? / Are you free...? |
| 🅳 打电话时 | Is Mr. Wang there? / Who's calling? / When is he back? |
| 🅴 紧急情况 | Help! Where is...? / Is there a doctor? / What do I do? |

> ⚠️ **语法边界**：本包严格只用 ①-③ 已学语法（be 动词、一般动词 do/does、第三人称单数）。情态动词（can/should）、过去时（did/was）、完成时（have been）均未学，不在本包出现。

> 🧠 **核心思维**（贯穿全包）：英语问句的 Wh-词**永远在最前面**，一上来就告诉对方"我要问什么类型的信息"。这和中文"先铺垫再问"的思维完全相反。

### 适用对象

- **难度**：L1（零基础~初级）
- **前置要求**：掌握 be 动词（①）、一般动词现在时（②）、第三人称（③）
- **目标**：能在 5 大真实场景中脱口而出正确问句，建立"疑问词优先"的英语思维
- **学习方式**：场景代入 → 整句模仿 → 替换槽位 → 自主提问

### 🅰️ 出行与交通 · 第 1 课：问路与位置

> 🧠 **Where 思维**：迷路了？先想"我要问地点"→ 脑子弹出 **Where** → 再搭后面的结构。

| # | 问句 | 自然回答 | 语法点 | 替换练习（换___槽位） |
|:-:|------|----------|:------|:---------|
| 1 | **Excuse me, where is the nearest subway station?** | It's just around the corner. | Where is + 地点？ | Where is the nearest ___? (bus stop / ATM / restroom / pharmacy / hospital / bank) |
| 2 | **Where are the restrooms?** | They're on the second floor. | Where are + 复数地点？ | Where are the ___? (elevators / stairs / exits / lockers / ticket machines) |
| 3 | **How do I get to the museum?** | Go straight and turn left at the corner. | How do I get to + 地点？ | How do I get to ___? (the airport / the train station / the city center / your hotel / this address) |
| 4 | **Is there a pharmacy near here?** | Yes, there's one across the street. | Is there + 单数名词 + near here? | Is there a ___ near here? (supermarket / post office / park / parking lot / good restaurant / coffee shop) |
| 5 | **How far is it from here?** | It's about 10 minutes on foot. | How far is + 地点？ | How far is ___ from here? (the airport / your hotel / the beach / downtown / the nearest hospital) |
| 6 | **Am I going the right way to the station?** | Yes, keep going straight. It's about 5 minutes. | Am I going the right way to...? | Am I going the right way to ___? (the museum / the park / the bus terminal / the library / the stadium) |
| 7 | **Do you have a map? — Sure. Let me show you.** | 有地图吗？——有的，我指给你看。 | Do you have + 名词？ | Do you have ___? (a city map / a guidebook / the address / a phone with GPS / the directions) |

### 🅰️ 出行与交通 · 第 2 课：问时间与交通

> 🧠 **What time / Which 思维**：赶车赶飞机？先锁定你要问什么——时间用 **What time**，交通线路用 **Which**。

| # | 问句 | 自然回答 | 语法点 | 替换练习（换___槽位） |
|:-:|------|----------|:------|:---------|
| 8 | **What time does the museum open?** | It opens at 9 am. | What time does + 主语 + 动词？ | What time does ___ open/close/start? (the store / the bank / the movie / the meeting / your flight) |
| 9 | **What time is the next train to Shanghai?** | At 3:15 pm. | What time is + 名词？ | What time is ___? (the last bus / your flight / the check-in / the show / the appointment) |
| 10 | **Which bus goes to the city center?** | Bus number 5 goes there. | Which + 名词 + goes to...? | Which ___ goes to ___? (train / the airport / line / the museum / subway / the stadium) |
| 11 | **How long does it take to get there?** | About 30 minutes by bus. | How long does it take to...? | How long does it take to ___? (walk there / drive / fly / get through security / get a taxi) |
| 12 | **Where do I buy a ticket?** | You can buy one at the machine over there. | Where do I + 动词？ | Where do I ___? (pay / check in / get a taxi / wait for the bus / pick up my luggage) |
| 13 | **Does this bus stop at the museum?** | Yes, it's the third stop. | Does this + 名词 + 动词...? | Does this ___ stop at/go to ___? (train / the airport / line / downtown / bus / the hospital) |
| 14 | **Is this seat taken?** | No, go ahead. | Is this + 名词 + taken/free? | Is this ___ taken/free? (seat / table / spot / chair / parking space / locker) |

### 🅱️ 消费与服务 · 第 3 课：购物问询

> 🧠 **How much / Do you have 思维**：进商店先想"我要问价格（How much）还是问有没有（Do you have）"。

| # | 问句 | 自然回答 | 语法点 | 替换练习（换___槽位） |
|:-:|------|----------|:------|:---------|
| 15 | **How much is this?** | It's 120 yuan. | How much is + 单数？ | How much is ___? (that / the red one / this shirt / a cup of coffee / the ticket) |
| 16 | **How much are these?** | They're 80 yuan each. | How much are + 复数？ | How much are ___? (these shoes / the apples / those flowers / the tickets / the souvenirs) |
| 17 | **Do you have this in a small size? — Let me check. Yes, here you are.** | 这个有小号吗？——我看看。有，给你。 | Do you have this in + 名词？ | Do you have this in ___? (blue / another color / a different style / medium / large) |
| 18 | **I want to try this on. Where is the fitting room? — Over there, on the left.** | 我想试穿一下。试衣间在哪？——那边，左手边。 | I want to try + 名词 + on | I want to try ___ on. Where is the fitting room? (this jacket / these shoes / that dress / those pants / this hat / the scarf) |
| 19 | **This is a little expensive. Do you have other ones? — Yes, we have these. They're on sale.** | 这个有点贵。有别的吗？——有的，这些在打折。 | Do you have other + 名词？ | Do you have other ___? (colors / styles / sizes / options / brands / ones) |
| 20 | **Do you take cards? — Yes, we take all major cards.** | 可以刷卡吗？——可以，主流银行卡都收。 | Do you take + 支付方式？ | Do you take ___? (cash / WeChat / Alipay / credit cards / phone payments) |
| 21 | **I need a receipt, please. — Of course. Here you are.** | 我需要一张收据。——当然。给你。 | I need + 名词 | I need ___. (a bag / a refund / an exchange / a discount / your business card / the warranty) |

### 🅱️ 消费与服务 · 第 4 课：餐厅与服务

> 🧠 **I want / What do you 思维**：坐下先想——要东西用 **I want**（I want the menu），问推荐用 **What do you recommend**，问信息用 **Is/Does**（Is service included? / Does this contain...?）。

> ⚠️ 本包刻意避免 Can/Could/Would 等情态动词（它们在基础⑦才学），用 I want / I need / Please 等 L1 已知词汇代替，同样自然。

| # | 问句 | 自然回答 | 语法点 | 替换练习（换___槽位） |
|:-:|------|----------|:------|:---------|
| 22 | **I want to see the menu, please. — Here you go.** | 我想看一下菜单。——给您。 | I want to see + 名词 | I want to see ___? (the wine list / the dessert menu / the specials / your ID / the room / the options) |
| 23 | **What do you recommend?** | The grilled fish is very popular. | What do you recommend? | What do you recommend for ___? (a starter / the main course / dessert / drinks / someone who likes spicy food) |
| 24 | **Does this dish contain nuts?** | No, it doesn't. It's nut-free. | Does this + 名词 + contain/have...? | Does this contain/have ___? (dairy / gluten / pork / alcohol / MSG / any allergens) |
| 25 | **I want the bill, please. — Sure. It's 185 yuan in total.** | 我要买单。——好的，一共 185 元。 | I want + 名词 | I want ___? (more water / some napkins / a fork / another plate / a takeaway box / the Wi-Fi password) |
| 26 | **Is service charge included?** | Yes, it's already included. | Is + 名词 + included? | Is ___ included? (tax / tip / breakfast / parking / Wi-Fi / the entrance fee) |
| 27 | **Do you have any vegetarian options?** | Yes, we have several. Let me show you. | Do you have any + 形容词 + 名词？ | Do you have any ___ options? (vegan / gluten-free / halal / kids' / non-spicy / local / seasonal) |
| 28 | **Is there a table by the window? — Of course. Right this way.** | 有靠窗的桌子吗？——当然。这边请。 | Is there a table + 位置？ | Is there a table ___? (outside / in the corner / at the bar / near the stage / in the non-smoking area / somewhere quiet) |

### 🅲 社交与寒暄 · 第 5 课：认识新朋友

> 🧠 **社交问句链思维**：英语社交靠"问"来推进对话。不问 = 冷场。从 **What do you do?** → **Where are you from?** → **What do you like?**，形成自然的"认识一个人"问句链。

| # | 问句 | 自然回答 | 语法点 | 替换练习（换___槽位） |
|:-:|------|----------|:------|:---------|
| 29 | **What do you do?** | I'm a student. / I work in a hospital. | What do you do?（问职业） | What do you do? — I'm a ___. / I work in ___. (teacher / a bank / designer / a school / IT / marketing) |
| 30 | **Where are you from?** | I'm from Chengdu. How about you? | Where are you from? | Where are you from? — I'm from ___. (Beijing / Guangdong / Japan / a small town / the south / the US) |
| 31 | **How's it going?** | Pretty good! And you? | How's it going?（随意问候） | How's it going? — ___. (Not bad / Great / Can't complain / Same as usual / Busy but good / Everything's fine) |
| 32 | **What do you do for fun?** | I like watching movies and hiking. | What do you do for fun? | What do you do ___? (on weekends / after work / in your free time / to relax / for exercise / when you're bored) |
| 33 | **What kind of music do you like?** | I like pop and a little bit of jazz. | What kind of + 名词 + do you like? | What kind of ___ do you like? (movies / food / books / sports / games / TV shows / animals / art) |
| 34 | **Do you like traveling?** | Yes, I love it! I've been to five countries. | Do you like + doing/名词？ | Do you like ___? (cooking / hiking / reading / watching sports / trying new food / learning languages) |
| 35 | **What countries do you want to visit? — I want to go to Japan and France.** | 你想去哪些国家？——我想去日本和法国。 | What + 名词 + do you want to...? | What ___ do you want to ___? (cities / visit / food / try / places / see / activities / do) |

### 🅲 社交与寒暄 · 第 6 课：约见面与关心

> 🧠 **约见思维**：从 Are you free → Do you want to → What time / Where，形成"约见面三步曲"。

| # | 问句 | 自然回答 | 语法点 | 替换练习（换___槽位） |
|:-:|------|----------|:------|:---------|
| 36 | **Are you free this weekend?** | Yes, I am. Why? | Are you free + 时间？ | Are you free ___? (tonight / tomorrow / after work / next Friday / right now / on Sunday / this evening) |
| 37 | **Do you want to grab lunch together?** | I'd love to! | Do you want to + 动词...? | Do you want to ___? (get a coffee / go shopping / see a movie / take a walk / try that new place / hang out sometime / play basketball) |
| 38 | **What time do you want to meet? — How about 12:30?** | 你想几点见面？——12:30 怎么样？ | What time do you want to + 动词？ | What time do you want to ___? (meet / start / leave / arrive / have dinner / come over) |
| 39 | **Where do you want to meet? — Let's meet at the entrance.** | 你想在哪里见？——在入口处见吧。 | Where do you want to + 动词？ | Where do you want to ___? (meet / go / eat / sit / wait) |
| 40 | **How is your day? — It's good! A little busy, but good.** | 你今天怎么样？——挺好的！有点忙，但不错。 | How is + 时间/事物？ | How is ___? (your day / everything / your work / your family / your new job / life) |
| 41 | **Is everything okay? You look tired.** | Yeah, I'm fine. Just didn't sleep well. | Is everything okay? | Is everything ___? (alright / okay / going well / sorted out / ready for tomorrow / clear) |
| 42 | **Do you need help? — That's very kind of you. I think I'm okay.** | 你需要帮忙吗？——你真好。我想我还行。 | Do you need + 名词/help? | Do you need ___? (help / anything / a hand / some water / a break / directions) |

### 🅳 电话与联络 · 第 7 课：打电话找人

> 🧠 **电话思维**：电话英语 = 自报家门（Hello, this is...）→ 说明来意（Is Mr. Wang there?）→ 确认对方（Who's calling?）。三步走，不慌张。

| # | 问句 | 自然回答 | 语法点 | 替换练习（换___槽位） |
|:-:|------|----------|:------|:---------|
| 43 | **Hello, is Mr. Wang there, please? — Speaking. / Just a moment, please.** | 你好，请问王先生在吗？——我就是。/ 请稍等。 | Is + 人 + there? | Is ___ there? (the manager / your parents / someone in charge / the doctor / your colleague) |
| 44 | **Who's calling, please?** | This is Li Ming from ABC Company. | Who's calling? | Who's calling? — This is ___. (your student / Tom's friend / the delivery person / the landlord / your neighbor) |
| 45 | **Is Mr. Wang available?** | Sorry, he's in a meeting right now. | Is + 人 + available? | Is ___ available? (the doctor / your boss / the technician / customer service / the person in charge) |
| 46 | **When is he back? — He is back in about an hour.** | 他什么时候回来？——大约一小时后回来。 | When is + 主语 + back/available? | When is ___ back? (she / the manager / your colleague / the doctor / the next train) |
| 47 | **I want to call back. When is a good time? — Try after 3 pm. He is free then.** | 我想再打过来。什么时候合适？——下午 3 点以后。他那时有空。 | I want to call back. When is a good time? | I want to call back. When is ___ free? (he / she / the manager / your colleague / the person in charge) |
| 48 | **Do you hear me? The signal is bad here. — Yes, I hear you. Do you hear me now?** | 你听得到我吗？这里信号不好。——听得到。你现在听得到我吗？ | Do you hear me? | Do you ___ me? (hear / see / understand / follow) |
| 49 | **Sorry, I think you have the wrong number.** | Oh, I'm sorry. I'll check the number again. | You have the wrong number. | Sorry, ___ (this isn't the hospital / there's no Tom here / you dialed the wrong extension / I'm not the person you're looking for) |

### 🅳 电话与联络 · 第 8 课：留言与跟进

> 🧠 **留言思维**：要找的人不在 → **I want to leave a message.** → 请对方传话 → **Please ask him to call me back.** → 留下关键信息。

| # | 问句 | 自然回答 | 语法点 | 替换练习（换___槽位） |
|:-:|------|----------|:------|:---------|
| 50 | **I want to leave a message. Is that okay? — Sure. Go ahead.** | 我想留个言。可以吗？——当然。请说。 | I want to leave a message. | I want to leave a message for ___. (Mr. Wang / the doctor / your boss / the manager / the person in charge) |
| 51 | **Please take a message for me. — Of course. What do you want me to tell him?** | 请帮我传个话。——当然。你想让我告诉他什么？ | Please take a message. | Please ___ a message. (take / pass on / send / leave) |
| 52 | **Please ask him to call me back. — Sure. What's your name and number?** | 请让他给我回电话。——好的。你的名字和号码是？ | Please ask + 人 + to + 动词...? | Please ask him to ___. (call me / email me / text me / check his messages / confirm the time) |
| 53 | **Please tell him I called. — I will. Don't worry.** | 请告诉他我打过电话。——我会的。别担心。 | Please tell + 人 + 从句 | Please tell him ___? (I call again / I need to change the time / the meeting is at 3 / I send an email / I see his message) |
| 54 | **When is a good time to call back?** | Try around 4 pm. He is free then. | When is a good time to...? | When is a good time to ___? (call / reach you / come by / have a meeting / drop in / chat / discuss this) |
| 55 | **Does he know I called? — Yes, he does. He says he calls you tonight.** | 他知道我打过电话吗？——知道。他说今晚打给你。 | Does + 主语 + know...? | Does ___ know ___? (she / I called / they / about the meeting / the boss / about the problem / your parents / you are here) |
| 56 | **Do I call back or wait for his call? — He says he calls you, so just wait.** | 我打回去还是等他电话？——他说会打给你，等着吧。 | Do I + 动词...or...? | Do I ___ or ___? (wait / call again / email / text / come in person / send a message / try tomorrow / leave another message) |

### 🅴 应急与求助 · 第 9 课：紧急呼救

> 🧠 **紧急思维**：紧急情况下，第一句一定要短！**Help! / Call the police! / Fire!** ——然后才是描述发生了什么。问句用来获取关键信息：**Where is...? / How do I...? / Is there...?**

| # | 问句 | 自然回答 | 语法点 | 替换练习（换___槽位） |
|:-:|------|----------|:------|:---------|
| 57 | **Help! Is anyone here? — I hear you! What's wrong?** | 救命！有人在吗？——我听到你了！出什么事了？ | Help! + 呼救确认 | Help! ___! (I'm stuck / I've fallen / someone broke in / there's a fire / my child is missing) |
| 58 | **Where is the nearest hospital?** | It's about 3 kilometers from here. I'll call an ambulance. | Where is the nearest + 地点？ | Where is the nearest ___? (police station / fire station / emergency room / pharmacy / shelter / exit / first aid kit) |
| 59 | **How do I call an ambulance?** | Dial 120. Tell them your location. | How do I + 动词...? | How do I ___? (call the police / report a fire / get emergency help / find a doctor / contact my embassy / get to the hospital) |
| 60 | **Is there a doctor here?** | I'm a doctor. What happened? | Is there a + 职业 + here? | Is there a ___ here? (nurse / police officer / security guard / translator / first aider / someone who speaks Chinese) |
| 61 | **Is anyone calling the police? — Yes, I call them right now. They are on their way.** | 有人报警了吗？——有，我正在打。他们已经在路上了。 | Is anyone + doing...? | Is anyone ___? (calling an ambulance / helping / looking for my child / reporting this / contacting his family) |
| 62 | **Is everyone okay?** | I think so. One person is a little hurt. | Is everyone + 形容词？ | Is everyone ___? (safe / accounted for / out of the building / okay / alright / here / still inside) |
| 63 | **What happened? Tell me what you see. — A car hits the pole over there. It happens so fast.** | 发生什么了？告诉我你看到什么。——一辆车撞到了那边的柱子。发生得太快了。 | What happened? Tell me... | What happened? Tell me ___? (what you see / who is here / when it happens / where it comes from / how it starts / if anyone is hurt) |

### 🅴 应急与求助 · 第 10 课：看病与求助

> 🧠 **看病思维**：先说自己怎么了（I have a... / I don't feel...）→ 再问关键信息（Where is...? / What do I do? / Do I need...?）。

| # | 问句 | 自然回答 | 语法点 | 替换练习（换___槽位） |
|:-:|------|----------|:------|:---------|
| 64 | **I don't feel well. Where is the nearest hospital?** | It's about 10 minutes by taxi. Do you need me to call one? | I don't feel well. Where is...? | I don't feel well. Where is ___? (a pharmacy / a clinic / a doctor / the emergency room / somewhere I can sit down) |
| 65 | **What do I do? I have a bad headache. — You lie down and rest. Take some medicine if you have it.** | 我该怎么办？我头疼得厉害。——你躺下休息。有药的话吃点药。 | What do I do? I have a... | What do I do? I have a ___. (fever / stomachache / sore throat / toothache / back pain / cold / cough) |
