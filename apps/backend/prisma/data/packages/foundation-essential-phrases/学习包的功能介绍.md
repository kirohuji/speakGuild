# 基础④ · 疑问句体系 · Yes/No问句 · Wh-问句 · How问句（L1）

## 学习包概览

本学习包是 **Foundation 基础系列** 的 **第四包**。你将系统学习英语的 **疑问句体系**——包括 Yes/No 问句、6W 问句（What/Who/Where/When/Why/Which）以及各种 How 问句。

> 💡 **为什么这是第四包？** 会了陈述句（我是谁/我做什么/他做什么），下一步就是**会提问**。真正的交流不是单方面陈述，而是问答互动。**
> 
> 中文疑问句只要加"吗"或改语调，英语则要助动词提前/疑问词前置——这是中国学习者的核心难点。

### 本包核心语法

| 语法点 | 问句类型 | 示例 |
|:------|:---------|:-----|
| **be 动词疑问** | Are you...? / Is it...? | Are you happy? Is it big? |
| **一般动词疑问** | Do you...? / Does he...? | Do you like coffee? Does she work? |
| **情态动词疑问** | Can you...? / Should I...? | Can you help me? |
| **过去式疑问** | Did you...? / Were you...? | Did you eat? Were you there? |
| **What 问句** | What is...? / What do...? | What is your name? What do you do? |
| **Who 问句** | Who is...? / Who does...? | Who is she? Who knows the answer? |
| **Where 问句** | Where is...? / Where do...? | Where is the bathroom? Where do you live? |
| **When 问句** | When do...? / When is...? | When do you get up? When is your birthday? |
| **Why 问句** | Why do...? / Why is...? | Why are you late? Why do you like it? |
| **Which 问句** | Which one? / Which do...? | Which one is yours? Which do you prefer? |
| **How 问句** | How many/much/far/long/often | How many books? How much is it? |

### 适用对象

- **难度**：L1（零基础~初级）
- **前置要求**：掌握 be 动词、一般动词现在时、第三人称
- **目标**：能在各种场景中正确提问，实现真正的英语对话

### 🅱️ Wh- 问句 · 第 3 课：6W 问句

| # | 句块（直接背） | 释义 | 语法点 | 替换练习 |
|:-:|---------------|------|:------|:---------|
| 13 | **What is your name? — My name is Tom.** | 你叫什么名字？——我叫 Tom。 | What + be + ...？ | What is ___? (your phone number / this / that) |
| 14 | **What do you do? — I am a teacher.** | 你是做什么的？——我是老师。 | What do + 主语 + do？ | What do you ___? (study / want / need / like) |
| 15 | **Who is she? — She is my sister.** | 她是谁？——她是我妹妹。 | Who + be + ...？ | Who is ___? (he / that man / your teacher) |
| 16 | **Where is the bathroom? — It's over there.** | 洗手间在哪里？——在那里。 | Where + be + 名词？ | Where is ___? (my phone / the bus stop / your office) |
| 17 | **Where do you live? — I live in Beijing.** | 你住在哪里？——我住在北京。 | Where do + 主语 + 动词？ | Where do you ___? (work / study / come from) |
| 18 | **When do you get up? — I get up at 7.** | 你几点起床？——我 7 点。 | When do + 主语 + 动词？ | When do you ___? (eat lunch / go home / go to bed) |
| 19 | **Why are you late? — Because I missed the bus.** | 你为什么迟到？——因为我错过了公交。 | Why + be + 主语 + ...？ | Why are you ___? (tired / sad / angry / happy) |
| 20 | **Why do you like it? — Because it's fun.** | 你为什么喜欢它？——因为好玩。 | Why do + 主语 + 动词？ | Why do you ___? (like it / want to go / study English) |
| 21 | **Which one is yours? — The blue one.** | 哪个是你的？——蓝色的那个。 | Which + 名词 + be？ | Which ___ is ___? (book / yours / seat / mine) |
| 22 | **Which do you prefer, tea or coffee? — Tea.** | 你更喜欢哪个，茶还是咖啡？——茶。 | Which do + 主语 + 动词？ | Which do you ___? (like better / want / choose) |

### 🅲 How 问句 · 第 5 课：数量/价格/距离/时间

| # | 句块（直接背） | 释义 | 语法点 | 替换练习 |
|:-:|---------------|------|:------|:---------|
| 29 | **How many books do you have? — I have ten.** | 你有多少本书？——十本。 | How many + 复数名词？ | How many ___ do you have? (pens / friends / cats) |
| 30 | **How much is this? — It's 20 dollars.** | 这个多少钱？——20 美元。 | How much + be + 名词？ | How much is ___? (the ticket / your phone / this shirt) |
| 31 | **How much water do you drink? — About 2 liters.** | 你喝多少水？——大约 2 升。 | How much + 不可数名词？ | How much ___ do you need? (time / money / sugar) |
