# 基础③ · 第三人称与一般现在时 · 描述他人 · 他/她的日常 · 对比你我（L1）

## 学习包概览

本学习包是 **Foundation 基础系列** 的 **第三包**。你将学习英语中最容易出错的语法点——**第三人称单数**（He/She/It + 动词 + s/es）。

> 💡 **为什么这是第三包？** 中文说"他吃""她喜欢"，动词不变。英语必须加 s——"He eats""She likes"。这是中国学习者最顽固的语法错误之一。**必须单独一个包打透。**

### 本包核心语法

| 语法点 | 说明 |
|:------|:-----|
| **第三人称单数 + s/es** | He eats / She goes / It works |
| **have → has** | He has a car. / She has a cat. |
| **do → does** | He does his homework. / Does he like...? |
| **否定：doesn't** | He doesn't eat meat. / She doesn't like coffee. |
| **疑问：Does...?** | Does he / Does she...? + 简短回答 |
| **所有格** | my / your / his / her / its / our / their |
| **名词所有格 's** | Tom's book / my mother's name |

### 第一包 vs 第二包 vs 第三包

| 包 | 重点 | 例句 |
|:--|:----|:-----|
| ① be 动词 | I am / You are / It is | I am a student. |
| ② 一般动词（我/你） | I eat / You like / We go | I eat breakfast. |
| **③ 第三人称（他/她/它）** | **He eats / She likes / It works** | **He eats breakfast.** |

### 适用对象

- **难度**：L1（零基础~初级）
- **前置要求**：掌握 be 动词和一般动词现在时（I/you/we/they）
- **目标**：彻底掌握第三人称单数变化，不再说"He eat""She like"

## 句型模板（供 `sentence_patterns.csv` 使用）

| # | 句型 | 替换槽 | 示例 | 训练功能 |

| 3 | His/Her ___ is ___. | name / Tom / phone / new | His name is Tom. | 所有格 |
| 4 | He/She likes ___. | coffee / sports / music | He likes coffee. | like → likes |
| 5 | He/She likes to ___. | read / swim / travel | She likes to read. | likes to + do |
| 6 | He/She doesn't like ___. | meat / coffee / cold weather | He doesn't like meat. | 否定 |
| 7 | Does he/she like ___? | sports / dogs / music | Does she like music? | 疑问句 |
| 8 | He/She ___ at ___. | gets up / 7 / goes to bed / 11 | He gets up at 7. | 日常动作 |
| 9 | He/She doesn't ___. | eat meat / drink coffee / smoke | She doesn't drink coffee. | doesn't 否定 |
| 10 | He/She works in a ___. | hospital / school / bank | She works in a hospital. | 工作地点 |
| 11 | I ___ but he/she ___ . | like coffee / likes tea | I like coffee but she likes tea. | 对比 |
| 12 | I don't ___ but he/she ___ . | have a car / has a car | I don't have a car but he has a car. | 否定vs肯定 |

| 16 | Everyone ___ him/her. | likes / knows / loves | Everyone likes him. | 不定代词三单 |

| 18 | Where does he/she ___? | work / live / study / go | Where does he work? | 疑问+地点 |
