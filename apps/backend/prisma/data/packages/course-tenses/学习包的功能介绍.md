# 英语时态大全 · 六种核心时态精练（L2~L3）

## 学习包概览

本学习包是 **语法专项系列** 中**最重要**的组成部分之一，聚焦英语 **六种核心时态**。**L2~L3（中级基础~中级提高）** 定位，精选 **70+ 个时态句块（chunks）**—— 每个句块在完整句子中呈现时态用法，通过大量对比练习建立牢固的时态意识。

> 时态是英语语法的基石。中文没有动词变形，中国学习者对时态缺乏天然语感。本包通过 **大量对比句块**（一般现在 vs 现在进行、过去时 vs 完成时、will vs going to），帮助学习者在语境中自然掌握每种时态的核心用法。

### 覆盖时态

| # | 时态 | 场景 | 难度 |
|:-:|------|------|:----:|
| 1 | 一般现在时 vs 现在进行时 | 习惯 vs 当下 | L2 |
| 2 | 一般过去时 vs 过去进行时 | 完成 vs 持续 | L2 |
| 3 | 现在完成时（经历/已做） | 已做/未做/从未 | L2 |
| 4 | 现在完成时 vs 一般过去时 | 有无具体时间 | L2~L3 |
| 5 | 将来时（will / going to） | 计划 vs 预测 | L2 |
| 6 | 过去完成时 | 过去的过去 | L3 |
| 7 | 时态综合叙事 | 叙述中自然切换 | L3 |

### 句块（chunk）vs 句型（pattern）

| | 句块（chunk） | 句型（pattern） |
|--|-------------|----------------|
| **是什么** | 可直接背诵的 **完整句子**（含时态对比） | 带替换槽位的 **句型框架** |
| **作用** | 在语境中建立时态语感 | 替换动词/时间，练习变位 |
| **例子** | `I usually wake up at 7. Right now I'm reading a book.` | `I usually ___ at 7. Right now I'm ___ing a book.` |
| **对应文件** | `chunks.csv` | `sentence_patterns.csv` |

> **核心学习法**：每个重要时态概念都配 **对比句块**（如 一般现在 vs 现在进行），让学习者在一句话中体会时态差异。

### 适用对象

- **难度**：L2~L3（中级基础~中级提高）
- **前置要求**：掌握基本动词和时态基本概念
- **目标学习者**：希望彻底搞定时态用法、不再混淆时态的学习者

### 学习目标

完成本包学习后，学习者能够：

1. **一般现在 vs 现在进行**：区分习惯/事实（I work here）和当下/临时（I'm working from home）
2. **一般过去 vs 过去进行**：区分完成动作（I called her）和背景描述（I was calling her when...）
3. **现在完成时**：用 ever/never/already/yet/just/for/since 正确表达经历和已做事项
4. **完成时 vs 过去时**：理解"有无具体时间"的决定性差异
5. **将来时**：区分 will（即时决定/预测）和 going to（计划/迹象）
6. **过去完成时**：用 had done 表达"过去的过去"时间关系
7. **叙事能力**：在描述经历和讲故事时自然切换多种时态

### 一、一般现在 vs 现在进行（L2）

> 一般现在 = 习惯/事实/常态；现在进行 = 此刻/临时/趋势

| # | 句块（直接背） | 时态 | 功能 |
|:-:|---------------|:----:|:----:|
| 1 | I usually wake up at 7 every morning. | 一般现在 | 日常习惯 |
| 2 | She always drinks coffee in the morning. | 一般现在 | 第三人称习惯 |
| 3 | The sun rises in the east. | 一般现在 | 普遍事实 |
| 4 | I don't usually eat meat for breakfast. | 一般现在(否定) | 否定习惯 |
| 5 | Right now I'm reading a great book. | 现在进行 | 此刻活动 |
| 6 | Look! It is raining outside. | 现在进行 | 此刻正在 |
| 7 | She is staying with her friend this week. | 现在进行 | 临时情况 |
| 8 | He is working from home this month. | 现在进行 | 当前阶段 |
| 9 | He works in a bank. | 一般现在 | 固定工作 |
| 10 | Are you listening to me right now? | 现在进行(疑问) | 此刻疑问 |

### 二、一般过去 vs 过去进行（L2）

> 一般过去 = 完成的过去动作；过去进行 = 过去某时正在做的事

| # | 句块（直接背） | 时态 | 功能 |
|:-:|---------------|:----:|:----:|
| 1 | I visited my grandparents yesterday. | 一般过去 | 完成动作 |
| 2 | She went to Paris last summer. | 一般过去 | 过去事件 |
| 3 | We had dinner at a nice restaurant last night. | 一般过去 | 晚餐经历 |
| 4 | I didn't see him at the party. | 一般过去(否定) | 过去否定 |
| 5 | Did you enjoy the movie? | 一般过去(疑问) | 过去疑问 |
| 6 | I was sleeping when the alarm went off. | 过去进行 | 被打断 |
| 7 | They were watching TV when I arrived. | 过去进行 | 背景动作 |
| 8 | He was walking to work when it started to rain. | 过去进行 | 突发情境 |
| 9 | While I was cooking, he was setting the table. | 过去进行×2 | 同时动作 |
| 10 | I was doing my homework all evening. | 过去进行 | 持续背景 |

### 三、现在完成时·经历与已做（L2）

> have/has + 过去分词：ever/never/already/yet/just/for/since

| # | 句块（直接背） | 用法 | 功能 |
|:-:|---------------|:----:|:----:|
| 1 | I have already finished my homework. | already | 已经完成 |
| 2 | She has never been to Japan. | never | 从未经历 |
| 3 | Have you ever tried sushi? | ever | 疑问经历 |
| 4 | I have just eaten lunch. | just | 刚刚完成 |
| 5 | They have already left the office. | already | 已经离开 |
| 6 | He hasn't finished his report yet. | not yet | 还未完成 |
| 7 | I have been to Beijing twice. | been to | 去过次数 |
| 8 | She has traveled to many countries. | life experience | 人生经历 |
| 9 | We have known each other for ten years. | for + 时段 | 持续(for) |
| 10 | I've lived in this city since 2020. | since + 起点 | 持续(since) |

### 四、现在完成时 vs 一般过去时（L2~L3）

> 完成时 = 无具体时间 / 与现在相关；过去时 = 有具体时间 / 已结束

| # | 句块（直接背） | 对比 | 说明 |
|:-:|---------------|:----:|------|
| 1 | I saw that movie yesterday. / I have seen that movie. | 过去 vs 完成 | yesterday vs no time |
| 2 | She has gone to Shanghai. / She has been to Shanghai. | gone vs been | 不在此地 vs 去过 |
| 3 | I finished my homework at 8 PM. / I have finished my homework. | 具体时间 vs 无 | 完成时间 vs 当前状态 |
| 4 | When did you arrive? / Have you arrived yet? | 过去疑问 vs 完成疑问 | 问时间 vs 问状态 |
| 5 | I ate a lot at the party. / I have eaten too much. | 过去事件 vs 当前感受 | 描述 vs 现在结果 |
| 6 | He lived in London for five years. / He has lived in London for five years. | 过去(已离开) vs 至今(仍在) | 结束 vs 持续 |
| 7 | I never tried sushi before. / I have never tried sushi. | 过去叙述 vs 完成经历 | 语境不同 |
| 8 | She visited Paris last year. / She has visited Paris twice. | 过去 + 具体时间 vs 完成 + 次数 | 具体 vs 概括 |

### 五、将来时（L2）

> will（即时决定/预测/承诺）vs going to（计划/迹象）vs 进行时（安排）vs 一般现在（时刻表）

| # | 句块（直接背） | 结构 | 功能 |
|:-:|---------------|:----:|:----:|
| 1 | I will help you with your homework. | will + verb | 主动提供 |
| 2 | I think it will rain later. | will + verb | 预测 |
| 3 | I won't forget your kindness. | won't + verb | 否定承诺 |
| 4 | I'm going to start a new job next month. | going to | 已有计划 |
| 5 | She is going to apply for that position. | going to | 意向 |
| 6 | Look at those clouds! It's going to rain. | going to(迹象) | 迹象预测 |
| 7 | The train leaves at 6 PM tonight. | 一般现在(计划) | 时刻表 |
| 8 | She is meeting her friend for lunch tomorrow. | 现在进行(安排) | 确定安排 |
| 9 | Are you going to join us this weekend? | going to(疑问) | 计划询问 |
| 10 | I'll call you when I arrive at the station. | will + 时间从句 | 将来承诺 |

### 六、过去完成时（L3）

> had + 过去分词 = 表达"过去的过去"，常用于叙事中建立时间先后

| # | 句块（直接背） | 用法 | 功能 |
|:-:|---------------|:----:|:----:|
| 1 | When I arrived, she had already left. | already + had done | 过去的过去 |
| 2 | I had never tried sushi before that day. | never + had done | 在此之前从未 |
| 3 | By the time I got there, the movie had started. | by the time | 截止到某时 |
| 4 | After I had finished my work, I went home. | after + had done | 先完成再回家 |
| 5 | She had been studying for hours before the exam. | had been doing | 过去完成进行 |
| 6 | I realized I had made a mistake. | 意识到已发生 | 事后认识 |
| 7 | He had lost his keys, so he couldn't get in. | had done + 结果 | 原因结果 |
| 8 | They had already eaten when I arrived at the restaurant. | already + had eaten | 先吃后到 |
| 9 | I had just finished cleaning when the phone rang. | just + had done | 刚做完时 |

### 七、时态综合叙事（L3）

> 在叙述经历/故事时灵活切换多种时态

| # | 句块（直接背） | 时态组合 |
|:-:|---------------|----------|
| 1 | I usually wake up at 7, but today I woke up late. | 一般现在 → 一般过去 |
| 2 | She has been learning English for three years, and she speaks very well. | 现在完成进行 → 一般现在 |
| 3 | While I was traveling in Japan, I tried many local dishes. | 过去进行(背景) → 一般过去(事件) |
| 4 | By the time I got home, my family had already had dinner. | 一般过去 → 过去完成 |
| 5 | I have never been to the US, but I'm going to visit next year. | 现在完成 → be going to |
| 6 | He said he would call, but he hasn't called yet. | 一般过去(转述) → 现在完成 |
| 7 | When I was a child, I didn't like vegetables, but now I eat them every day. | 一般过去(习惯) → 一般现在(对比) |
| 8 | I'm reading a great book at the moment; I will lend it to you when I finish. | 现在进行 → will → 一般现在 |
| 9 | She has already been to three countries this year, and she is planning another trip in December. | 现在完成 → 现在进行(计划) |
| 10 | While I was walking home, I realized I had left my keys at the office. | 过去进行 → 一般过去 → 过去完成 |
