# 基础⑥ · 过去经历 · 一般过去时 & 完成时入门 · 动词变形与不规则动词（L1）

## 学习包概览

本学习包是 **基础系列（Foundation）· 第 6 包**，也是从"说现在"到"说过去"的 **时态拓展包**。前五个包你都在用现在时表达——现在你要学会用英语讲述**过去的事**：昨天做了什么、上周去了哪里、以前是什么样子。

> 🎯 **核心目标**：学完本包，你能用一般过去时描述过去的经历和状态，用现在完成时表达"去过/做过/还没做"，掌握 30 个核心不规则动词的三种形式——**从"说现在"到"说过去"。**

### 为什么过去时需要专门一个包？

中文说"我昨天去学校"和"我每天去学校"，动词"去"不变，加个"昨天"就行。但英语**动词必须变形式**：

- 我**每天**去学校。→ I **go** to school every day.
- 我**昨天**去学校。→ I **went** to school yesterday.

不仅如此，英语还有**规则动词**（work→worked）和**不规则动词**（go→went，eat→ate）两套变化规则。不规则动词必须死记硬背——这是英语学习中最大的记忆挑战之一。

同时，本包引入**现在完成时**（I have been to...）的最基础用法。完成时和过去时的核心区别是：

- **I went to Beijing last year.**（强调**什么时候**去的）
- **I have been to Beijing.**（强调**有没有**这种经历，不关心具体时间）

> ❌ 中国人常犯错误：*I go to the park yesterday. / *He didn't went. / *I have been to Beijing last year.（完成时不能加具体时间）
> ✅ 正确：**I went to the park yesterday. / He didn't go. / I went to Beijing last year. / I have been to Beijing.**

### 🅳 完成时入门（5 主题，15 句块）

#### 第 7 课：去过/做过

> 🧠 **核心思维**：一般过去时说"**什么时候**做了某事"（I went last year），完成时说"**有没有这种经历**"（I have been）。完成时结构永远是 **have/has + 过去分词**。

| 主题 | 句块 | 释义 | 替换练习 |
|:----|:-----|:-----|:---------|
| 去过某地 | **I have been to Beijing.** | 我去过北京。 | I have been to ___.（Japan / the US / Hong Kong / the Great Wall / three countries） |
| 去过某地 | **Have you ever been to Shanghai? — Yes, I have.** | 你去过上海吗？——去过。 | Have you ever been to ___?（Paris / a concert / this restaurant / abroad） |
| 做过某事 | **I have never tried hot pot.** | 我从来没试过火锅。 | I have never ___.（been abroad / driven a car / seen snow / eaten Indian food / ridden a horse） |
| 做过几次 | **She has seen that movie three times.** | 那部电影她看过三次了。 | He/She has ___ + 次数.（read this book / twice / visited Japan / once / watched it / many times） |
| 问有没有 | **Have you finished your homework? — Yes, I have.** | 你做完作业了吗？——做完了。 | Have you ___?（eaten / called her / seen this / read the news / decided） |
| 刚刚发生 | **I have lost my keys! Do you see them?** | 我钥匙丢了！你看到了吗？ | I have ___!（lost my phone / forgotten my password / broken my glasses / made a mistake） |
| 已经完成 | **She has already left the office.** | 她已经离开办公室了。 | He/She has already ___.（finished / eaten / called / paid the bill / gone home） |

#### 第 8 课：刚刚/还没/多久了

> 🧠 **核心思维**：完成时 + **just** = 刚刚 / + **yet** = 还没 / + **for/since** = 持续了多久。

| 主题 | 句块 | 释义 | 替换练习 |
|:----|:-----|:-----|:---------|
| 刚刚 | **I have just arrived at the station.** | 我刚到车站。 | I have just ___.（finished / eaten / heard the news / talked to him / realized） |
| 还没 | **I haven't eaten lunch yet.** | 我还没吃午饭。 | I haven't ___ yet.（decided / finished / told him / started / gotten a reply） |
| 问还没 | **Have you eaten yet? — No, not yet.** | 你吃了吗？——还没。 | Have you ___ yet?（finished / called her / seen the doctor / booked the ticket） |
| 多久了 for | **I have lived here for five years.** | 我在这里住了五年了。 | I have ___ for ___.（worked here / three years / known her / a long time / studied English / two months） |
| 多久了 since | **She has worked here since 2020.** | 她 2020 年以来一直在这里工作。 | He has ___ since ___.（been a teacher / 2018 / played piano / he was 6 / lived alone） |
| 问多久 | **How long have you been here? — For about an hour.** | 你在这里多久了？——大约一小时。 | How long have you ___?（worked here / known him / waited / studied English / been married） |
| 最高级+完成时 | **This is the best movie I have ever seen!** | 这是我看过的最好的电影！ | This is the ___ I have ever ___.（best meal / had / most beautiful place / visited / hardest test / taken / funniest joke / heard） |
| 完成时 vs 过去时 | **I have been to Japan. I went there last year.** | 我去过日本。我去年去的。 | I have been to ___. I went there ___.（the US / in 2019 / Beijing / two years ago） |

### 🅴 过去与现在对比（5 主题，14 句块）

#### 第 9 课：回忆过去 / used to

> 🧠 **核心用法**：**used to + 动词原形** = 过去经常做但现在已经不做了。这是英语里表达"过去习惯"的最常用方式，一般过去时无法表达这种"以前常做现在不做"的含义。

| 主题 | 句块 | 释义 | 替换练习 |
|:----|:-----|:-----|:---------|
| 回忆过去 | **I remember when I was a child, I played outside every day.** | 我记得小时候每天都在外面玩。 | I remember when I was a child, I ___ every day.（went fishing / rode my bike / watched cartoons） |
| 回忆过去 | **Those were the good old days.** | 那些是美好的旧时光。 | Those were the ___ days.（happy / fun / best / simple） |
| Used to 习惯 | **I used to play the piano, but now I don't.** | 我以前弹钢琴，但现在不弹了。 | I used to ___, but now I don't.（live in a small town / drink coffee / stay up late / collect stamps） |
| Used to 习惯 | **She used to have long hair, but now she has short hair.** | 她以前留长发，但现在剪短了。 | He/She used to ___, but now ___.（wear glasses / wear contacts / be shy / be outgoing / take the bus / drive） |
| Used to 否定 | **I didn't use to like vegetables, but now I love them.** | 我以前不喜欢吃蔬菜，但现在很喜欢。 | I didn't use to ___, but now I ___.（get up early / like coffee / enjoy running / eat healthy food） |
| Used to 疑问 | **Did you use to live here? — Yes, I did. / No, I didn't.** | 你以前住这里吗？——是的。 | Did you use to ___?（play soccer / study at this school / have a pet / collect anything） |
| 过去与现在对比 | **Life was simpler before. Now everything is so fast.** | 以前生活更简单。现在一切都太快了。 | Life was ___ before. Now ___.（cheaper / more expensive / slower / busier / quieter / noisier） |

#### 第 10 课：今昔对比

| 主题 | 句块 | 释义 | 替换练习 |
|:----|:-----|:-----|:---------|
| 过去vs现在 | **When I was young, I stayed up late. Now I go to bed early.** | 我年轻时熬夜。现在我早睡。 | When I was ___, I ___. Now I ___.（in college / partied a lot / a kid / was afraid of the dark / in school / played sports） |
| 过去vs现在 | **I always watched TV after school when I was a kid.** | 我小时候放学总看电视。 | I always ___ when I was a ___.（played outside / kid / studied hard / student / helped my mom / child） |
| 生活变化 | **I have changed a lot since then.** | 从那时起我变了很多。 | I have ___ a lot since then.（grown / learned / improved / traveled / matured / experienced） |
| 生活变化 | **Things have changed so much in the past ten years.** | 过去十年变化太大了。 | Things have changed so much in the past ___.（five years / few months / decade / century） |

## 附：过去时 vs 完成时速查卡

```
📐 结构对比

  一般过去时                         现在完成时
  ──────────────────────             ──────────────────────
  主语 + 动词过去式                   主语 + have/has + 过去分词
  I worked.                          I have worked.
  He went.                           He has gone.
  They ate.                          They have eaten.

  否定：I didn't work.               否定：I haven't worked.
  疑问：Did you work?                疑问：Have you worked?

💡 核心区别（记这两组就够了）：

  具体的过去 vs 经历（不强调时间）：
    I went to Beijing last year.（知道是去年去的）
    I have been to Beijing.（不关心什么时候，只说有这种经历）

  动作完成 vs 持续到现在：
    I finished my homework at 8 pm.（8点做完了）
    I have finished my homework.（现在做完了，结果在眼前）

📝 时间词速查——过去时专用 / 完成时专用 / 两者皆可

  ┌─────────────────────┬──────────────────────┬─────────────────────┐
  │   仅过去时           │   仅完成时            │   两者皆可           │
  ├─────────────────────┼──────────────────────┼─────────────────────┤
  │ yesterday           │ ever / never         │ this morning        │
  │ last week/month     │ just / already       │ today               │
  │ ...ago              │ yet / not yet        │ before              │
  │ in 2018/2020        │ for / since          │ recently            │
  │ when I was a child  │ How long have...     │ once / twice        │
  └─────────────────────┴──────────────────────┴─────────────────────┘

⚠️ 最容易犯的错误：
  ❌ I have been to Beijing last year.   → 完成时不能加具体过去时间
  ✅ I went to Beijing last year.        → 有具体时间用过去时
  ✅ I have been to Beijing.             → 没时间/不强调时间用完成时
  
  ❌ He didn't went home yesterday.      → didn't 后必须用动词原形
  ✅ He didn't go home yesterday.
  
  ❌ She has went to the store.          → has 后必须用过去分词
  ✅ She has gone to the store.
```
