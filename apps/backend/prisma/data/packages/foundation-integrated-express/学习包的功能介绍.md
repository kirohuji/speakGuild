# 基础⑧ · 连词与连接 · 并列转折 · 因果条件 · 举例顺序（L1）

## 学习包概览

本学习包是 **Foundation 基础系列** 的 **第八包**。你将学习英语的 **连接词和基本从句**——把简单句连接成复杂句，表达因果、转折、时间、条件等逻辑关系。

> 💡 **为什么是第八包？** 有了前面的七包，你已经能说各种简单句了。但真正的交流需要连接想法——"我去了但是没找到""如果下雨我就不去了""她来之前我吃完了"。连词和从句让你从"说短句"升级到"说段落"。

### 本包核心语法

| 连接类型 | 连接词 | 示例 |
|:---------|:-------|:-----|
| **并列** | and / or / but | I like coffee and tea. / Is it big or small? |
| **因果** | because / so | I stayed home because I was sick. / I was tired so I went home. |
| **时间** | when / while / before / after | When I arrived, she was eating. / I always brush my teeth before bed. |
| **条件** | if / unless | If it rains, I'll stay home. / I won't go unless you come. |

> ⚠️ 本包仅覆盖 L1 级别连词。让步（although）和定语从句（who/which/that）为 L2 内容，不在本包出现。

### 适用对象

- **难度**：L1（零基础~初级）
- **前置要求**：完成前7个基础包
- **目标**：能用连词和从句连接想法，表达更复杂的逻辑关系

## 句块参考（供数据生成用）

> 💡 **本包设计思路**：连词（and/but/because/so/if/when）是"语言胶水"——把简单句粘成段落。每个句块先给两个独立短句，再用连词合成一句，让学习者直观感受"连接"的力量。

### 🅰️ 并列与转折 · 第 1 课：和/或者/但是

| # | 句块（直接背） | 释义 | 语法点 | 替换练习 |
|:-:|---------------|------|:------|:---------|
| 1 | **I like coffee and tea.** | 我喜欢咖啡和茶。 | and（并列添加） | I like ___ and ___. (cats and dogs / reading and writing / music and art) |
| 2 | **She is smart and kind.** | 她聪明又善良。 | and 连接形容词 | He is ___ and ___. (tall and handsome / young and energetic / funny and friendly) |
| 3 | **I went to the store and bought some milk.** | 我去了商店，买了些牛奶。 | and 连接两个动作 | I ___ and ___. (came home and cooked dinner / sat down and read a book) |
| 4 | **Do you want tea or coffee?** | 你想喝茶还是咖啡？ | or（选择） | Do you want ___ or ___? (this or that / to stay or to go / pizza or pasta) |
| 5 | **Is it big or small?** | 它是大的还是小的？ | or 连接反义词 | Is it ___ or ___? (hot or cold / new or old / cheap or expensive) |
| 6 | **I like him, but he doesn't like me.** | 我喜欢他，但他不喜欢我。 | but（转折） | I ___, but ___. (want to go, but I'm busy / tried my best, but I failed) |
| 7 | **The food is cheap, but it's delicious.** | 食物便宜，但是很好吃。 | but 对比 | ___ is ___, but ___. (the hotel is small, but it's clean / he's young, but very smart) |

### 🅰️ 并列与转折 · 第 2 课：因为/所以

| # | 句块（直接背） | 释义 | 语法点 | 替换练习 |
|:-:|---------------|------|:------|:---------|
| 8 | **I stayed home because I was sick.** | 我待在家，因为我病了。 | because + 原因从句 | I ___ because ___. (was late because I missed the bus / ate a lot because I was hungry) |
| 9 | **She is happy because she passed the test.** | 她开心，因为她通过了考试。 | because 后置原因 | ___ because ___. (He's tired because he worked all night / We're excited because it's our first trip) |
| 10 | **Because it rained, we stayed inside.** | 因为下雨了，我们待在了屋里。 | Because 前置 | Because ___, ___. (it was cold, I wore a jacket / I was tired, I went to bed early) |
| 11 | **I was tired, so I went to bed early.** | 我累了，所以我早早上床了。 | so（结果） | I was ___, so I ___. (hungry, so I ate / busy, so I couldn't come / bored, so I watched TV) |
| 12 | **It started to rain, so we took a taxi.** | 开始下雨了，所以我们打了车。 | so 引出自然结果 | ___, so ___. (the store was closed, so I went home / I lost my phone, so I bought a new one) |
| 13 | **I don't eat meat, so I ordered a salad.** | 我不吃肉，所以我点了沙拉。 | 前提 + so + 结果 | I ___, so I ___. (can't swim, so I stayed on the beach / woke up late, so I skipped breakfast) |

### 🅱️ 时间与条件 · 第 3 课：当...时/在...之前/之后

| # | 句块（直接背） | 释义 | 语法点 | 替换练习 |
|:-:|---------------|------|:------|:---------|
| 14 | **When I arrived, she was cooking.** | 我到的时候，她正在做饭。 | When + 过去时, 过去进行时 | When I ___, ___. (called, he was sleeping / got home, they were watching TV) |
| 15 | **I always listen to music when I run.** | 我跑步的时候总是听音乐。 | When + 一般现在, 一般现在（习惯） | I always ___ when I ___. (drink coffee when I wake up / read when I take the bus) |
| 16 | **Call me when you get home.** | 你到家的时候给我打电话。 | When + 现在时表将来 | ___ when you ___. (text me when you arrive / tell her when you see her) |
| 17 | **I brush my teeth before I go to bed.** | 我睡觉前刷牙。 | before + 从句 | I ___ before I ___. (eat breakfast before I leave / check my email before I start work) |
| 18 | **After I finished work, I went to the gym.** | 我下班后去了健身房。 | After + 过去时, 过去时 | After I ___, I ___. (woke up, I took a shower / ate dinner, I watched a movie) |
| 19 | **We played games while we waited.** | 我们等的期间玩了游戏。 | while（同时进行） | I ___ while ___. (listened to music while I worked / talked on the phone while I walked) |
| 20 | **Please wait here until I come back.** | 请在这里等到我回来。 | until（直到...才停） | ___ until ___. (stay here until it stops raining / keep trying until you succeed) |

### 🅱️ 时间与条件 · 第 4 课：如果/除非

| # | 句块（直接背） | 释义 | 语法点 | 替换练习 |
|:-:|---------------|------|:------|:---------|
| 21 | **If it rains tomorrow, I'll stay home.** | 如果明天下雨，我就待在家。 | If + 现在时, will（第一条件） | If ___, I'll ___. (I have time, I'll call you / you're free, we can meet / it's cheap, I'll buy it) |
| 22 | **If you study hard, you will pass the test.** | 如果你努力学习，你会通过考试的。 | If + 条件, will + 结果 | If you ___, you will ___. (practice every day, get better / eat well, feel better / save money, be rich) |
| 23 | **Let me know if you need help.** | 如果你需要帮助就告诉我。 | 祈使句 + if | ___ if ___. (call me if you get lost / take an umbrella if it rains / tell her if you see her) |
| 24 | **I won't go unless you come with me.** | 除非你跟我一起去，否则我不去。 | unless = if not | I won't ___ unless ___. (eat unless I'm hungry / leave unless you ask me to / buy it unless it's cheap) |
| 25 | **You can't enter unless you have a ticket.** | 除非你有票，否则你不能进。 | can't...unless | You can't ___ unless ___. (drive unless you have a license / pass unless you study / go out unless you finish) |
| 26 | **If I were you, I would take the job.** | 如果我是你，我会接受那份工作。 | If I were you, I would...（虚拟建议） | If I were you, I would ___. (say sorry / go to the doctor / study abroad / wait a bit longer) |
| 27 | **As long as you try your best, it's okay.** | 只要你尽力了，就没关系。 | as long as（只要） | As long as ___, ___. (you're happy, I'm happy / we're together, everything is fine / it doesn't rain, we'll go) |

### 🅲 举例与顺序 · 第 5 课：举例与补充

| # | 句块（直接背） | 释义 | 语法点 | 替换练习 |
|:-:|---------------|------|:------|:---------|
| 28 | **I like sports. For example, I play basketball every weekend.** | 我喜欢运动。比如，我每个周末打篮球。 | For example, ...（举例） | I like ___. For example, I ___. (music / listen to pop / movies / watch comedies / food / love hot pot) |
| 29 | **She is kind. Also, she is very hardworking.** | 她很善良。而且，她非常勤奋。 | Also, ...（补充） | He is ___. Also, he is ___. (smart / funny / tall / strong / rich / generous / young / brave) |
| 30 | **I like coffee. He likes coffee, too.** | 我喜欢咖啡。他也喜欢咖啡。 | ..., too.（句末补充） | I ___. He ___, too. (like tea / likes tea / play guitar / plays guitar / am from Beijing / is from Beijing) |
| 31 | **She speaks English, and she speaks Japanese as well.** | 她说英语，而且她也说日语。 | ..., as well.（也，句末） | He ___, and he ___ as well. (can cook / can bake / plays piano / plays violin / is a teacher / is a writer) |
| 32 | **I don't like coffee. I don't like tea, either.** | 我不喜欢咖啡。我也不喜欢茶。 | not...either（否定句中的"也"） | I don't ___. I don't ___, either. (eat meat / eat fish / drink alcohol / smoke / like loud music / like cold weather) |
| 33 | **It's a nice restaurant. What's more, the prices are good.** | 这家餐厅不错。而且，价格也实惠。 | What's more, ...（递进补充） | It's a ___. What's more, ___. (good movie / it's free / nice hotel / it's near the beach / fun game / it's easy to learn) |
| 34 | **I want to go, but I have no time. Besides, I don't have money.** | 我想去，但我没时间。再说，我也没钱。 | Besides, ...（追加理由） | I want to ___, but ___. Besides, ___. (travel / I'm busy / tickets are expensive / eat out / I'm tired / I need to save money) |

### 🅲 举例与顺序 · 第 6 课：时间顺序叙述

| # | 句块（直接背） | 释义 | 语法点 | 替换练习 |
|:-:|---------------|------|:------|:---------|
| 35 | **First, I get up. Then, I take a shower. After that, I eat breakfast.** | 首先，我起床。然后，我洗澡。之后，我吃早餐。 | First...Then...After that...（时间顺序） | First, I ___. Then, I ___. After that, I ___. (do homework / watch TV / go to bed) |
| 36 | **I went to the supermarket. Next, I went to the bank. Finally, I went home.** | 我去了超市。接着，我去了银行。最后，我回家了。 | Next, ... Finally, ... | I went to ___. Next, I went to ___. Finally, I went to ___. (the park / the library / home / the café / the post office / work) |
| 37 | **At first, I didn't like the job. But later, I started to enjoy it.** | 起初，我不喜欢这份工作。但后来，我开始喜欢了。 | At first, ... But later, ... | At first, ___. But later, ___. (I was scared / I felt better / it was hard / it became easy / she was shy / she opened up) |
| 38 | **While I was cooking, my sister was setting the table.** | 我在做饭的时候，我妹妹在摆桌子。 | While + 过去进行, 过去进行 | While I was ___, ___ was ___. (studying / my roommate was sleeping / working / my friend was waiting / driving / she was reading) |
| 39 | **Before you leave, please turn off the lights.** | 在你离开之前，请关灯。 | Before + 从句, 祈使句 | Before you ___, please ___. (go / lock the door / eat / wash your hands / sleep / brush your teeth) |
| 40 | **After you finish, let me know. Then we can go together.** | 你做完之后告诉我。然后我们可以一起去。 | After + 从句, 祈使句. Then... | After you ___, let me know. Then ___. (arrive / we can meet / decide / I'll book it / call me / we'll discuss) |

## 句型模板（供 `sentence_patterns.csv` 使用）

| # | 句型 | 替换槽 | 示例 | 训练功能 |
|:-:|:----|:-------|:-----|:---------|
| 1 | I like ___ and ___. | coffee, tea / cats, dogs / reading, writing | I like coffee and tea. | and 并列 |
| 2 | Do you want ___ or ___? | tea, coffee / this, that / to stay, to go | Do you want tea or coffee? | or 选择 |
| 3 | ___, but ___. | I like him, he doesn't like me / it's cheap, it's delicious | I like him, but he doesn't like me. | but 转折 |
| 4 | ___ because ___. | I stayed home, I was sick / she is happy, she passed | I stayed home because I was sick. | because 原因 |
| 5 | ___, so ___. | I was tired, I went to bed / it rained, we took a taxi | I was tired, so I went to bed early. | so 结果 |
| 6 | When I ___, ___. | arrived, she was cooking / called, he was sleeping | When I arrived, she was cooking. | When 时间从句 |
| 7 | ___ before I ___. | I brush my teeth, go to bed / I eat breakfast, leave | I brush my teeth before I go to bed. | before |
| 8 | After I ___, I ___. | finished work, went to the gym / woke up, took a shower | After I finished work, I went to the gym. | after |
| 9 | ___ while ___. | I listened to music, I worked / we talked, we walked | I listened to music while I worked. | while 同时 |
| 10 | If ___, I'll ___. | it rains, stay home / I have time, call you / you study, pass | If it rains tomorrow, I'll stay home. | if 条件 |
| 11 | I won't ___ unless ___. | go, you come / eat, I'm hungry / leave, you ask me | I won't go unless you come with me. | unless |
| 12 | If I were you, I would ___. | take the job, say sorry, go to the doctor, study abroad | If I were you, I would take the job. | 虚拟建议 |
| 13 | For example, I ___. | play basketball, listen to pop, watch comedies, love hot pot | I like sports. For example, I play basketball every weekend. | 举例 |
| 14 | He/She is ___. Also, he/she is ___. | kind, hardworking / smart, funny / tall, strong | She is kind. Also, she is very hardworking. | 递进补充 |
| 15 | I ___. He/She ___, too. | like coffee, likes coffee / play guitar, plays guitar | I like coffee. He likes coffee, too. | too 句末补充 |
| 16 | I don't ___. I don't ___, either. | eat meat, eat fish / drink, smoke / like it, like that | I don't like coffee. I don't like tea, either. | not...either 否定补充 |
| 17 | First, I ___. Then, I ___. After that, I ___. | get up, take a shower, eat breakfast | First, I get up. Then, I take a shower. After that, I eat breakfast. | 时间顺序 |
| 18 | Before you ___, please ___. / After you ___, let me know. | leave, turn off the lights / finish, let me know | Before you leave, please turn off the lights. | 前后顺序 |
