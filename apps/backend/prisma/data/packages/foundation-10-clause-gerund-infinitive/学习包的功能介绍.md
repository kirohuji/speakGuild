# 基础⑩ · 从句入门与动词不定式/动名词入门 · 宾语从句 · 定语从句 · 不定式 · 动名词（L1~L2）

## 学习包概览

### 整体描述
本学习包是基础系列（Foundation）· 第 10 包，也是**最后一包**。它有两个使命：
1. **从句入门**——学会把句子"嵌套"到另一个句子中（宾语从句、定语从句）
2. **非谓语动词入门**——学会动词的"名词化"使用（不定式、动名词）

> 🎯 核心目标：学完本包，你能用宾语从句表达想法和转述，用定语从句精确描述人物事物，用不定式表达意图和目的，用动名词表达喜好和活动——**从"简单句"到"复杂句"，完成基础阶段。**

### 为什么从句和非谓语动词放在最后一包？
前面 9 包你学的是："我吃""他走了""明天会下雨""如果你来，我会很高兴"——这些全是**简单句**或**连词连接的并列/复合句**。

但真正的英语交流需要更复杂的结构：
- 宾语从句：**I think (that) you are right.**（一个句子作为另一个句子的宾语）
- 定语从句：**The book that I read is very good.**（一个句子修饰一个名词）
- 不定式：**I want to go home.** / **I came here to learn English.**（动词作名词/副词）
- 动名词：**Swimming is fun.** / **I enjoy reading.**（动词作名词）

这是从"中级入门"到"真正流利"的**最后一道门槛**。学完本包，你就掌握了英语核心语法的全部骨架。

## 设计哲学

### 两层词汇分配策略

本包是从句/不定式/动名词驱动的**高级语法学习包**，核心词汇分为两类：

| 层级 | 来源 | 作用 | 本包占比 |
|------|------|------|:--------:|
| **核心词汇** | 包⑩新引入（包①-⑨未覆盖） | 从句结构模板 + 非谓语动词模式 | 159 词/短语 |
| **扩展词汇** | 复用包①-⑨ | 被嵌套的内容实义词 | ~180 词 |

> 🧠 **核心洞察**：英语的"复杂句"能力取决于从句嵌套和非谓语动词。本包 65% 的词汇（think/know/believe/want/like/enjoy 等）已在包①-⑨作为核心词出现——它们作为"被嵌套的内容载体"复用。真正的新知识是**从句结构本身**（that/who/which/where 引导的嵌套句）和**动词非谓语形式的选择**（to do vs doing）。

### 累加式设计——基础系列的终点

```
包①-③ 基础时态 → 包④ 短语动词 → 包⑤ 观点 → 包⑥ 社交 → 包⑦ 将来时+情态
        ↓                                                    ↓
包⑧ 连词（句子间连接）  →  包⑨ 比较级（精确描述）  →  包⑩ 从句+非谓语（句子内嵌套）
```

### 从句结构驱动词汇分配

| 语法结构 | 核心内容 | 示例 |
|----------|---------|------|
| that 宾语从句 | 思维/转述动词 + that 从句模板 | I think (that)..., He said (that)... |
| wh- 宾语从句 | wh-词引导的嵌套问句 | I don't know what/where/why... |
| that/who 定语从句 | 关系代词引导的名词修饰 | the book that I read, the person who called |
| where/when/why 定语从句 | 关系副词引导的修饰 | the place where we met, the reason why |
| to 不定式 | 意愿/目的/评价结构 | want to, in order to, It's important to |
| -ing 动名词 | 喜好/擅长/主语结构 | enjoy -ing, be good at -ing, Swimming is fun |

### 非谓语动词选择规则

本包的核心教学难点是**不定式 vs 动名词的选择**——这是中国人最常出错的领域：
- 有些动词只接不定式（want to, decide to, promise to）
- 有些动词只接动名词（enjoy -ing, finish -ing, avoid -ing）
- 有些动词接两者意思不同（stop smoking vs stop to smoke）

## 设计清单

### 常见分配错误

| 错误类型 | 错误示例 | 正确做法 |
|----------|---------|---------|
| 把基础思维动词当核心词 | ❌ think/know/believe/want 标为核心 | ✅ 这些在包①已出现，应标为扩展 |
| 把基础关系词当核心词 | ❌ that/who/which/where 标为核心 | ✅ 这些在包①-⑧已出现，应标为扩展 |
| 把喜好动词当核心词 | ❌ like/love/enjoy/hate 标为核心 | ✅ 这些在包①已出现，应标为扩展 |
| 不定式漏 to | ❌ I want go home. | ✅ I want to go home. |
| 动名词用 to | ❌ I enjoy to read. | ✅ I enjoy reading. |
| 介词后用不定式 | ❌ Thank you for to help. | ✅ Thank you for helping. |
| stop + to do vs doing 混淆 | ❌ I stopped to smoke. (想表达"戒烟") | ✅ I stopped smoking. (stop + doing = 停止做) |

### 🅰️ 表达想法（5 主题，14 句块）

#### 第 1 课：我认为/我知道

| 主题 | 句块 | 释义 | 替换练习 |
|:----|:-----|:-----|:---------|
| 我认为… | **I think (that) you are right.** | 我认为你是对的。 | I think (that) ___. (he is nice / it's a good idea / we should go / she is busy) |
| 我认为… | **I don't think (that) it's a good idea.** | 我认为这不是个好主意。 | I don't think (that) ___. (he will come / it's true / she likes it / we have time) |
| 我认为… | **I believe (that) he is telling the truth.** | 我相信他说的是实话。 | I believe (that) ___. (you can do it / she is honest / things will get better) |
| 我知道… | **I know (that) you are busy.** | 我知道你很忙。 | I know (that) ___. (he is a good person / it's not easy / she works hard / you mean well) |
| 我知道… | **I'm sure (that) everything will be fine.** | 我确定一切都会好的。 | I'm sure (that) ___. (he will come / we can finish / she knows the answer / it's safe) |
| 我知道… | **I'm sorry (that) I'm late.** | 抱歉我迟到了。 | I'm sorry (that) ___. (I forgot / I missed the call / I didn't call / I broke it) |

## 语法速查卡

### 附：宾语从句速查卡

```
📐 宾语从句 = 一个完整的句子作为动词的"宾语"

✅ 基本结构：主语 + 动词 + (that) + 完整句子
  I think (that) you are right.
  She said (that) she was happy.
  I know (that) he is busy.

💡 that 在口语中经常省略：
  I think you are right. (= I think that you are right.)

⚠️ 否定转移：think/believe/suppose 的否定在从句中移到主句
  ❌ I think you are not right.
  ✅ I don't think you are right.

✅ wh- 宾语从句：动词 + wh-词 + 句子
  I know what you mean.
  Can you tell me where he is?
  I don't understand why she left.
```

### 附：定语从句速查卡

```
📐 定语从句 = 一个完整的句子作为名词的"修饰语"

           ✅ 例句                                  ❌ 错误
指物：    The book THAT I read is good.            The book I read is good (口语可接受但正式缺关系词)
指人：    The person WHO called is my boss.        The person called is my boss (歧义)
地点：    The restaurant WHERE we met is nice.     The restaurant we met is nice (缺where)
原因：    The reason WHY I came is simple.         The reason I came is simple (why可省略)

💡 关系代词选择规则：
  that → 指人或物（通用）     I like the movie THAT you recommended.
  who  → 指人（主语）         The man WHO helped me is kind.
  which → 指物（正式）        The book WHICH I bought is expensive.
  where → 地点               The park WHERE we walked is beautiful.
  why   → 原因               The reason WHY he left is unclear.

⚠️ 非限制性定语从句（本包不教，L2 级别）：
  ❌ My brother, who lives in Beijing, is a doctor. → 这是非限制性，L1~L2 不要求
```

### 附：不定式 vs 动名词对比速查卡

```
📐 不定式 (to + 动词原形)  vs  动名词 (动词 + -ing)

两者都是"动词的名词化"——但用法不同。

🅰️ 有些动词后只能接不定式：
  want / hope / decide / promise / plan / choose / agree / refuse
  ✅ I want to go.   ❌ I want going.
  ✅ I decided to leave.   ❌ I decided leaving.

🅱️ 有些动词后只能接动名词：
  enjoy / finish / avoid / miss / suggest / practice / can't stand / give up
  ✅ I enjoy reading.  ❌ I enjoy to read.
  ✅ I finished working.  ❌ I finished to work.

🅲 有些动词后接两者意思相同：
  like / love / hate / start / begin / continue
  I like swimming. = I like to swim.
  It started raining. = It started to rain.

🅳 有些动词后接两者意思不同（最易混淆）：
  stop + 动名词 = 停止做某事         I stopped smoking. (我戒烟了)
  stop + 不定式 = 停下来去做某事     I stopped to smoke. (我停下来去抽烟)

  remember + 动名词 = 记得做过某事   I remember locking the door. (记得锁了)
  remember + 不定式 = 记得要做某事   I remember to lock the door. (记得要锁)

  forget + 动名词 = 忘了做过某事     I forgot locking the door. (忘了锁过门)
  forget + 不定式 = 忘了要做某事     I forgot to lock the door. (忘了锁门)

🅴 不定式 vs 动名词作主语：
  动名词作主语更自然（日常口语）：
    Swimming is fun.  ✅ 自然
    To swim is fun.   ✅ 语法正确但正式
    It's fun to swim. ✅ 最常见（用it作形式主语）

💡 记住这个核心规则就够了：
  不定式 → 想做/需要做/承诺做（意图、目的、未发生）
  动名词 → 享受/完成/避免（实际做、已发生、习惯）
```
