# 基础⑤ · 现在进行时 vs 一般现在时 · 此刻正在做的事 · 临时 vs 习惯（L1~L2）

## 学习包概览

本学习包是 **Foundation 基础系列** 的 **第五包**。你将学习英语的 **现在进行时（Present Continuous）**，并与已经学过的一般现在时做**对比学习**。

> 💡 **时态对比是核心**：中文说"我吃早餐"和"我正在吃早餐"只差一个"正在"，但英语用两种完全不同的时态。本包的每个句块都同时呈现两个时态的对比，帮你建立"习惯 vs 此刻"的时态意识。

### 本包核心语法

| 语法点 | 一般现在时 | 现在进行时 |
|:------|:-----------|:-----------|
| **用法** | 习惯/事实/常态 | 此刻/临时/正在 |
| **结构** | I eat... / He eats... | I am eating... / He is eating... |
| **时间词** | every day, usually, always | now, right now, at the moment |
| **对比** | I work in Beijing. (长期工作) | I'm working from home today. (今天临时) |

### 适用对象

- **难度**：L1~L2（零基础~初级）
- **前置要求**：be动词 + 一般动词现在时
- **目标**：能区分"习惯"和"正在"，正确使用两种时态

### 🅳 have 与 get · 第 7 课：have 的两种用法

> 🧠 **核心洞察**：have 是英语里最"分裂"的动词——表"拥有"时**绝对不能**用进行时（❌ I'm having a car），表"吃/喝/经历"时**完全可以**用进行时（✅ I'm having lunch）。这个区别是 L1 学习者的高频错误来源。

| # | 一般现在（拥有/习惯） | 现在进行（动作/经历） | 释义 | 为什么不能/能？ |
|:-:|:----------------|:----------------|:-----|:-----------|
| 41 | I have a car. | ~~I'm having a car.~~ ❌ | 我有一辆车。 | **拥有** = 状态，不用进行时 |
| 42 | I have two brothers. | ~~I'm having two brothers.~~ ❌ | 我有两个兄弟。 | **家庭关系** = 状态，不用进行时 |
| 43 | I have a headache. | ~~I'm having a headache.~~ ❌ | 我头疼。 | **身体状态** = 状态，不用进行时 |
| 44 | I have breakfast at 7. | I'm having breakfast right now. ✅ | 我7点吃早餐。/ 我正在吃早餐。 | **吃** = 动作，可用进行时 |
| 45 | I have a meeting at 3. | I'm having a meeting right now. ✅ | 我3点有个会。/ 我正在开会。 | **参加** = 动作，可用进行时 |
| 46 | I have a good time at parties. | I'm having a great time! ✅ | 我在派对上玩得开心。/ 我正玩得很开心！ | **经历** = 动作，可用进行时 |
| 47 | She has a lot of work every day. | She's having a busy week. ✅ | 她每天有很多工作。/ 她这周很忙。 | **经历一段时期** = 可用进行时 |

### 🅳 have 与 get · 第 8 课：get 的日常表达

> 🧠 **核心洞察**：get 是英语里最"万能"的动词，口语中出现频率极高。它有四种最常见的用法，每种都有不同的"习惯 vs 此刻"对比。

| # | 一般现在（习惯/常态） | 现在进行（此刻/变化） | 释义 | get 的含义 |
|:-:|:----------------|:----------------|:-----|:-----------|
| 48 | I get up at 7 every day. | I'm getting up now. Wait a minute. | 我每天7点起。/ 我正在起床，等一下。 | get up = 起床 |
| 49 | I get home at 6 pm. | I'm getting home now. See you soon. | 我下午6点到家。/ 我快到家了，一会儿见。 | get home = 到家 |
| 50 | I get tired easily. | I'm getting tired. Let's take a break. | 我容易累。/ 我有点累了，休息一下吧。 | get + 形容词 = 变得... |
| 51 | It gets dark early in winter. | It's getting dark. Let's go inside. | 冬天天黑得早。/ 天快黑了，我们进去吧。 | get dark = 变暗 |
| 52 | I get a lot of emails at work. | I'm getting a lot of spam these days. | 我上班收到很多邮件。/ 我最近收到好多垃圾邮件。 | get = 收到 |
| 53 | She gets angry when people are late. | She's getting angry. Let's go now! | 别人迟到她会生气。/ 她要生气了，快走吧！ | get angry = 生气 |
| 54 | He gets along well with his classmates. | He's getting along better with them now. | 他和同学相处得好。/ 他现在和他们相处得更好了。 | get along = 相处 |

## 句型模板（供 `sentence_patterns.csv` 使用）

| # | 句型 | 替换槽 | 示例 | 训练功能 |
|:-:|:----|:-------|:-----|:---------|
| 1 | I ___ every day, but right now I'm ___. | eat breakfast, eating lunch | I eat breakfast every day, but right now I'm eating lunch. | 习惯 vs 此刻对比 |
| 2 | He usually ___, but today he's ___. | takes the bus, taking a taxi | He usually takes the bus, but today he's taking a taxi. | 常态 vs 今日对比 |
| 3 | I'm ___ right now. | eating, reading, working, cooking | I'm eating right now. | 此刻正在做 |
| 4 | She's ___ at the moment. | sleeping, studying, working, driving | She's sleeping at the moment. | 她此刻正在做 |
| 5 | It's ___ outside. | raining, snowing, getting dark | It's raining outside. | 此刻天气 |
| 6 | I ___ at ___ (时间). | get up, 7 / go to bed, 11 | I get up at 7. | 日常习惯时间 |
| 7 | I always ___. | get up early, drink water, say thank you | I always get up early. | always 频率 |
| 8 | I usually ___. | drink coffee, take the bus, eat at home | I usually drink coffee in the morning. | usually 频率 |
| 9 | I sometimes ___. | watch TV, go out, cook dinner | I sometimes watch TV after work. | sometimes 频率 |
| 10 | I never ___. | smoke, eat meat, drink alcohol | I never smoke. | never 否定频率 |
| 11 | She is always ___. | late, happy, busy, kind | She is always late for class. | be + always |
| 12 | ___ is/are ___ right now. | He, working / They, playing | He is working right now. | 主语+进行时 |
| 13 | Look! ___ is/are ___. | It, raining / They, coming | Look! It's raining. | Look! + 进行时 |
| 14 | I think ___ is ___. | he, sleeping / she, busy | I think he is sleeping. | think + 进行时推测 |
| 15 | ___ but ___. | I like tea, she likes coffee | I like tea but she likes coffee. (频率副词句可接) | 对比连接 |
| 16 | I have ___. (NOT I'm having) | a car, two brothers, a headache, blue eyes | I have a car. | have = 拥有（不用进行时） |
| 17 | I'm having ___. (experience) | breakfast, lunch, a meeting, a great time, a busy week | I'm having lunch right now. | have = 吃/经历（可用进行时） |
| 18 | I get up at ___, but right now I'm getting up. | 7, it's 7:05 | I get up at 7 every day, but right now I'm getting up. | get up 习惯 vs 此刻 |
| 19 | I'm getting ___. (changing state) | tired, hungry, angry, dark, cold, late, worried | I'm getting tired. Let's take a break. | get + 形容词 = 正在变得 |
| 20 | It's getting ___. Let's ___. | dark, go inside / cold, wear a jacket / late, hurry up | It's getting dark. Let's go inside. | get + 形容词+建议 |
| 21 | I get ___ every day, but today I'm getting ___. | a lot of emails, spam / home at 6, home late / to work by bus, a taxi | I get home at 6 every day, but today I'm getting home late. | get 习惯 vs 今天例外 |
| 22 | He/She gets ___ when..., and right now he/she is getting ___. | angry, people are late / angry, because we're late | She gets angry when people are late, and right now she's getting angry. | get 习惯 vs 此刻变化 |

## 核心词汇（供 `scene_vocabulary.csv` 使用）

| 类别 | 词汇 |
|:----|:-----|
| **现在进行时结构** | am / is / are + verb-ing / I'm / He's / She's / They're / We're / It's |
| **常用动词(-ing)** | eating / drinking / reading / watching / working / studying / cooking / sleeping / waiting / listening / playing / running / writing / driving / teaching / thinking / raining / staying / wearing / getting |
| **频率副词** | always / usually / often / sometimes / rarely / never |
| **时间标记(现在)** | now / right now / at the moment / today / this week / this month / tonight |
| **时间标记(习惯)** | every day / every morning / on weekends / at night / after dinner / in the morning |
| **感官/状态动词** | think / feel / look / see / hear / know / want / need / like / love / hate |
| **对比连接词** | but / and / usually...but today... |
| **have 的双面性** | have = 拥有(状态,不用进行时): have a car / have a brother / have a headache / have blue eyes / have a dog || have = 吃/经历(动作,可用进行时): have breakfast / have lunch / have a meeting / have a good time / have a party / have a busy week / have fun / have trouble |
| **get 的四种用法** | get up / get home / get to work / get to school (到达) || get tired / get angry / get dark / get cold / get hungry / get worried / get better / get worse (变得) || get a lot of emails / get a message / get a call / get a present (收到) || get along with / get through / get over / get by (短语动词) |
| **get 时间对比标志** | every day / usually / often vs right now / today / these days / at the moment / Look! |
