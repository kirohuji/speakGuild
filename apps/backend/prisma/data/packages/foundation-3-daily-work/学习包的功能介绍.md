# 基础③ · 第三人称与一般现在时 · 描述他人 · 他/她的日常 · 对比你我（L1）

## 学习包概览

本学习包是 **Foundation 基础系列** 的 **第三包**。你将学习英语中最容易出错的语法点——**第三人称单数**（He/She/It + 动词 + s/es）。

> 💡 **为什么这是第三包？** 中文说"他吃""她喜欢"，动词不变。英语必须加 s——"He eats""She likes"。这是中国学习者最顽固的语法错误之一。**必须单独一个包打透。**

### 本包核心语法

| 语法点 | 说明 |
|:------|:-----|
| **第三人称单数 + s/es** | He eats / She goes / It works |
| **have → has** | He has a car. / She has a cat. |
| **do → does** | He does his homework. / Does he like...? |
| **否定：doesn't** | He doesn't eat meat. / She doesn't like coffee. |
| **疑问：Does...?** | Does he / Does she...? + 简短回答 |
| **所有格** | my / your / his / her / its / our / their |
| **名词所有格 's** | Tom's book / my mother's name |

### 第一包 vs 第二包 vs 第三包

| 包 | 重点 | 例句 |
|:--|:----|:-----|
| ① be 动词 | I am / You are / It is | I am a student. |
| ② 一般动词（我/你） | I eat / You like / We go | I eat breakfast. |
| **③ 第三人称（他/她/它）** | **He eats / She likes / It works** | **He eats breakfast.** |

### 适用对象

- **难度**：L1（零基础~初级）
- **前置要求**：掌握 be 动词和一般动词现在时（I/you/we/they）
- **目标**：彻底掌握第三人称单数变化，不再说"He eat""She like"

### 🅳 职业与叙事 · 第 8 课：讲故事与场景描述

| # | 句块（直接背） | 释义 | 语法点 | 替换练习 |
|:-:|---------------|------|:------|:---------|
| 48 | **Look! The boy is running after his dog.** | 看！那个男孩在追他的狗。 | Look! + 第三人称进行时描述 | Look! The ___ is ___. (girl / dancing / cat / climbing the tree / bird / singing / baby / smiling) |
| 49 | **I saw a man on the street. He looked very happy.** | 我在街上看到一个男人。他看起来很开心。 | 过去时叙述链（I saw...He looked...） | I saw ___. He/She looked ___. (a woman / sad / a child / lost / a couple / in love / an old man / tired) |
| 50 | **She opened the door and smiled at me.** | 她打开门，对我笑了笑。 | 过去时 + and + 过去时（连贯动作） | He/She ___ed and ___ed. (stood up and walked away / sat down and started reading / turned around and saw me / stopped and looked back) |
| 51 | **There was a big dog in the park. It was very friendly.** | 公园里有一只大狗。它很友好。 | There was + 名词 + 第三人称描述 | There was a ___ in ___. It was ___. (cat / the garden / cute / man / the store / suspicious / bird / the tree / beautiful) |
| 52 | **Suddenly, the lights went out. Everyone was scared.** | 突然，灯灭了。大家都很害怕。 | Suddenly, + 过去事件 + 反应 | Suddenly, ___. Everyone/He/She ___. (it started to rain / ran for cover / the phone rang / jumped / the car stopped / got out to check) |
| 53 | **In the picture, a girl is reading a book under a tree.** | 画里，一个女孩在树下看书。 | In the picture, + 第三人称进行时描述 | In the picture, ___ is ___ing ___. (a boy / playing / with a ball / a family / having / a picnic / two cats / sleeping / on the sofa / some birds / flying / in the sky) |
| 54 | **He told me a funny story. I laughed so hard!** | 他给我讲了个搞笑的故事。我笑死了！ | 第三人称过去叙述 + 第一人称反应 | He/She told me ___. I ___. (a secret / was surprised / the news / felt happy / a joke / smiled / his plan / said yes / her dream / was moved) |
