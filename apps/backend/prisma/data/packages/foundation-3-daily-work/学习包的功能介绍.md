# 基础③ · 描述他人 · 第三人称与一般现在时 · 他/她的日常 · 对比你我 · 职业与叙事（L1）

## 学习包概览

本学习包是 **基础系列（Foundation）· 第 3 包**，也是攻克英语最顽固语法错误的 **关键一包**。在熟练掌握第一/二人称一般动词的基础上，本包将带你进入 **第三人称单数（He/She/It + 动词 + s/es）** 的世界——这是中文母语者最常出错、也最需要刻意练习的语法点。

> 🎯 **核心目标**：学完本包，你能准确用第三人称描述他人、对比不同人称、用叙事语言讲述简单故事——**从"我做什么"到"他/她做什么"。**

### 为什么第三人称单数如此重要？

中文说"他吃""她喜欢""猫跑"，动词从来不变。但英语不一样——**只要主语是 he/she/it 或单个的人/物，动词必须加 s/es**：

- 我吃 → I **eat** 　　他吃 → He **eat****s**
- 我喜欢 → I **like** 　　她喜欢 → She **like****s**
- 我有 → I **have** 　　他有 → He **has**（特殊变化）

这是中国学习者**最顽固、最难改**的语法错误之一。即使到了中高级水平，很多人在口语中还是会说 *He eat breakfast* / *She like coffee*。**必须专门花一个包打透这个习惯。**

> ❌ 中国人常犯错误：*He eat breakfast every day. / *She like music. / *He have a car.
> ✅ 正确：**He eats breakfast every day. / She likes music. / He has a car.**

同时，本包也会延伸到**人称代词（he/she/they）**、**所有格（his/her/their）**、**名词所有格（Tom's）**、以及**复数主语**和**不定代词**的用法，让你从"只有一个我"扩展到"描述全世界"。

### 和前两包的关系

| 包 | 重点 | 例句 |
|:--|:----|:-----|
| ① be 动词 | I am / You are / It is | I am a student. |
| ② 一般动词（我/你/我们/他们） | I eat / You like / We go / They live | I eat breakfast every day. |
| **③ 第三人称（他/她/它/所有人）** | **He eats / She likes / It works / Everyone knows** | **He eats breakfast at 7.** |
| 从"我" | 到"你/我们/他们" | **到"他/她/它/所有人"** |

### 覆盖核心语法

| 语法点 | 句块数 | 说明 |
|:-------|:------:|:-----|
| 第三人称 be 动词：He/She/It is | ~6 | He is / She is / It is（已学过，巩固） |
| 第三人称一般动词加 -s | ~8 | He eats / She likes / It works（核心规则） |
| 第三人称加 -es（s/sh/ch/x/o 结尾） | ~4 | He goes / She watches / It does（特殊拼写） |
| 第三人称 y→ies（辅音+y 结尾） | ~2 | He studies / She tries / It flies（变 y 为 i） |
| have → has | ~4 | He has / She has / It has（特殊变化） |
| 否定：doesn't + 动词原形 | ~6 | He doesn't eat / She doesn't like（否定句） |
| 一般疑问：Does he/she...? | ~4 | Does he like? / Does she have?（疑问+简短回答） |
| 特殊疑问：What/Where/When does...? | ~4 | What does he do? / Where does she live? |
| 所有格代词：his / her / their | ~4 | His name / Her car / Their house |
| 名词所有格 's | ~2 | Tom's book / My mother's name |
| 复数主语：They / My parents / People | ~4 | They live / My parents work / People speak |
| 不定代词：Everyone / Someone / Nobody | ~3 | Everyone likes / Someone knows / Nobody cares |
| 简单叙事连接 | ~5 | Look! / Suddenly / Then / In the picture / There was |

## 附：第三人称单数动词变化速查卡

```
          ✅ 肯定句                          ❌ 否定句                          ❓ 疑问句
I         I eat breakfast.                  I don't eat breakfast.             Do I eat breakfast?
You       You eat breakfast.                You don't eat breakfast.           Do you eat breakfast?
He        He eats breakfast.                He doesn't eat breakfast.          Does he eat breakfast?
She       She eats breakfast.               She doesn't eat breakfast.         Does she eat breakfast?
It        It looks good.                    It doesn't look good.              Does it look good?
We        We eat breakfast.                 We don't eat breakfast.            Do we eat breakfast?
They      They eat breakfast.               They don't eat breakfast.          Do they eat breakfast?

📝 动词加 -s 规则（第三人称单数）：
  一般情况加 -s                  eat → eats, like → likes, play → plays
  以 s / sh / ch / x / o 结尾加 -es   go → goes, watch → watches, wash → washes, fix → fixes, do → does
  辅音 + y 结尾 → 变 y 为 ies   study → studies, try → tries, fly → flies, carry → carries
  特殊变化 have → has            He has a car. (不是 He haves a car.)

💡 常见加 -es 动词速记：
  go → goes      do → does      watch → watches      wash → washes
  fix → fixes    pass → passes  catch → catches      teach → teaches

💡 简短回答速记（重点！）：
  Do you like coffee?     → Yes, I do. / No, I don't.
  Does he like coffee?    → Yes, he does. / No, he doesn't.
  ❌ 不能说 Yes, he likes.  /  No, he doesn't like.
  ✅ 必须用助动词回答：Yes, he does. / No, he doesn't.
```

## 附：人称代词与所有格速查卡

```
人称代词                       所有格代词                   名词性物主代词
I       → my      (我的)       → mine     (我的东西)
You     → your    (你的)       → yours    (你的东西)
He      → his     (他的)       → his      (他的东西)
She     → her     (她的)       → hers     (她的东西)
It      → its     (它的)       → its      (它的东西)
We      → our     (我们的)     → ours     (我们的东西)
They    → their   (他们的)     → theirs   (他们的东西)

📝 名词所有格 's：
  单数名词 + 's  →  Tom's book  /  my mother's car  /  the cat's tail
  复数以 s 结尾 + ' →  my parents' house  /  the students' teacher
  复数不以 s 结尾 + 's →  the children's park  /  the women's room

💡 容易混淆的三对：
  It's  ≠  Its      (It's = It is / Its = 它的)
  Who's ≠  Whose    (Who's = Who is / Whose = 谁的)
  There's = There is (不是 their)
```
