# 基础⑦ · 将来时与情态动词 · 计划预测 · 能力请求 · 建议义务（L1~L2）

## 学习包概览

本学习包是 **Foundation 基础系列** 的 **第七包**。你将学习 **将来时（will / be going to）** 和 **情态动词（can / must / should / may）**——表达计划、预测、能力、请求、建议和义务。

> 💡 **将来时 + 情态动词** 在一起是因为它们都涉及"非现实"——还没发生的事（将来）和可能/应该/必须做的事（情态）。这些表达让你的英语从"描述现实"升级到"谈论可能性和计划"。

### 本包核心语法

| 语法点 | 说明 | 示例 |
|:------|:-----|:-----|
| **will + 动词** | 即时决定/预测 | I'll call you. It will rain. |
| **be going to + 动词** | 计划/迹象 | I'm going to travel. It's going to rain. |
| **can** | 能力/许可 | I can swim. Can I open the window? |
| **must** | 必须(主观) | I must finish this today. |
| **should** | 建议 | You should see a doctor. |
| **may / might** | 可能/正式许可 | It may rain. May I come in? |
| **have to** | 必须(客观) | I have to work tomorrow. |
| **mustn't / don't have to** | 禁止/不必 | You mustn't park here. / You don't have to come. |

### 适用对象

- **难度**：L1~L2
- **前置要求**：一般现在时 + 一般过去时
- **目标**：能用将来时谈论计划和预测，能用情态动词表达各种态度

## 句块参考（供数据生成用）

> 💡 **本包设计思路**：将来时（will / be going to）和情态动词（can / must / should / may）共享一个核心特征——动词后面**永远接原形**，不加 s、不加 ed、不加 ing。抓住这个共性，两个语法点可以一起学。

### 🅳 情态疑问句 · 第 7 课：请求与许可（Can/Could）

> 🧠 **核心思维**：情态动词的疑问句和 be/do 疑问句规则一样——**情态动词提前到主语前面**。Can I...? Could you...? 是英语里最常用的礼貌请求句式，一句 **Could you...** 比 **Can you...** 更礼貌一个级别。

| # | 问句 | 自然回答 | 语法点 | 替换练习（换___槽位） |
|:-:|------|----------|:------|:---------|
| 41 | **Can I use your phone? — Sure, go ahead.** | 能用一下你的手机吗？——当然，用吧。 | Can I + 动词原形？（请求许可） | Can I ___? (sit here / come in / borrow this / ask you something / leave early / open the window) |
| 42 | **Can you help me with this bag? — Of course!** | 能帮我拿一下这个包吗？——当然！ | Can you + 动词原形？（请求帮助） | Can you ___? (hold this / take a photo of us / tell me the time / show me the way / pass me the menu) |
| 43 | **Could you tell me how to get to the museum? — Sure. Go straight and turn left.** | 您能告诉我去博物馆怎么走吗？——当然。直走左转。 | Could you...? (比 Can you 更礼貌) | Could you tell me how to get to ___? (the airport / the train station / the city center / this address / the nearest pharmacy) |
| 44 | **Could I have the bill, please? — Of course. Right away.** | 请给我账单好吗？——当然，马上。 | Could I have...? (极礼貌请求) | Could I have ___? (a glass of water / the menu / more time / your name / a receipt / the check) |
| 45 | **Can I try this on? — Sure, the fitting room is over there.** | 我能试穿吗？——当然，试衣间在那边。 | Can I try...on? (购物试穿) | Can I try ___ on? (this jacket / these shoes / that dress / this hat / the scarf) |
| 46 | **Could you speak more slowly, please? — Yes, of course. Is this better?** | 您能说慢一点吗？——当然。这样好点吗？ | Could you + 动词 + 副词？ | Could you ___? (speak louder / repeat that / wait a moment / spell your name / write it down) |
| 47 | **Can I get a receipt, please? — Here you are.** | 能给我一张收据吗？——给你。 | Can I get + 名词？（请求物品） | Can I get ___? (a bag / a refund / some water / extra napkins / your business card) |

### 🅳 情态疑问句 · 第 8 课：建议与意愿（Should/Would/Will）

> 🧠 **核心思维**：**Should I...?** = 我该不该做？（征求意见）/ **Would you like...?** = 你想要...吗？（礼貌提议）/ **Will you...?** = 你愿意...吗？（请求意愿）。三个情态动词各管一种社交场景。

| # | 问句 | 自然回答 | 语法点 | 替换练习（换___槽位） |
|:-:|------|----------|:------|:---------|
| 48 | **What should I do? — You should go to the information desk.** | 我该怎么办？——你应该去问询台。 | What should I do? | What should I ___? (say / bring / wear / buy for her / order / prepare) |
| 49 | **Should I take a taxi or the subway? — The subway is faster and cheaper.** | 我该打车还是坐地铁？——地铁更快更便宜。 | Should I + A or B? | Should I ___ or ___? (wait / call again / book in advance / go now / buy it / think about it / tell him / keep it a secret) |
| 50 | **Would you like some coffee? — Yes, please. / No, thank you.** | 您要来点咖啡吗？——好的。/ 不了，谢谢。 | Would you like + 名词？（礼貌提供） | Would you like ___? (some tea / a cookie / more water / something to eat / a seat / sugar with that) |
| 51 | **Would you like to join us for dinner? — I'd love to!** | 你愿意和我们一起吃晚饭吗？——我很乐意！ | Would you like to + 动词？（礼貌邀请） | Would you like to ___? (come with us / watch a movie / go for a walk / try this dish / sit here / dance) |
| 52 | **What would you like to drink? — Water, please.** | 您想喝点什么？——水，谢谢。 | What would you like to...? (餐厅/服务) | What would you like to ___? (eat / order / do / watch / start with / talk about) |
| 53 | **Will you help me with this? — Sure, no problem.** | 你能帮我弄一下这个吗？——当然，没问题。 | Will you + 动词？（请求意愿） | Will you ___? (come to my party / be my friend / marry me / call me later / wait for me / promise me something) |
| 54 | **Do you think I should go to the doctor? — Yes, you should. It looks serious.** | 你觉得我该去看医生吗？——该去。看起来挺严重的。 | Do you think I should...? | Do you think I should ___? (tell her / wait / apply for it / take the job / say sorry / buy it / worry about this) |
