# 基础⑩ · 基础补缺与综合运用 · 数字·时间·祈使句 · 礼貌表达（L1）

## 学习包概览

本学习包是 **基础系列（Foundation）· 第 10 包**，也是最后一包。它有两个使命：① **补缺**——把前面 9 个包没系统覆盖的基础知识点（数字、时间、星期、祈使句）集中补上；② **综合**——用前面学过的语法在真实场景中综合运用。

> 🎯 **核心目标**：学完本包，你能用数字、时间、日期做日常交流，用祈使句下指令，用礼貌表达提出请求和邀请——**完成基础阶段的所有"生存必备"技能。**

### 和基础①-⑨的关系

| 包 | 重点 | 句子类型 |
|:--|:----|:---------|
| ①-⑨ | 时态、情态、连词、比较 | **复杂句**（各种时态和逻辑关系） |
| **⑩ 补缺综合** | 数字/时间/祈使句/礼貌 | **实用句**（生存场景中的标准表达） |

### 🅰️ 数字与计数（5 主题，12 句块）

#### 第 1 课：数字 1-100

> 🧠 **规律速记**：1-12 没规律，13-19 加 -teen（thirteen, fourteen...），20-90 整十加 -ty（twenty, thirty...）。

| 主题 | 句块 | 释义 | 语法点 |
|:----|:-----|:-----|:------|
| 数字 1-10 | **one, two, three, four, five, six, seven, eight, nine, ten** | 1-10 | 基数词 1-10 |
| 数字 11-19 | **eleven, twelve, thirteen, fourteen, fifteen, sixteen, seventeen, eighteen, nineteen** | 11-19 | -teen（注意 13/15 拼写） |
| 整十 | **twenty, thirty, forty, fifty, sixty, seventy, eighty, ninety, one hundred** | 20-100 | -ty（forty 没有 u） |
| 组合 | **twenty-one, thirty-five, sixty-eight, ninety-nine** | 21/35/68/99 | 几十几 = 整十-个位 |
| 年龄 | **I am 25 years old.** | 我 25 岁。 | I am + 数字 + years old |
| 价格 | **It's 120 yuan.** / **It's 50 dollars.** | 120 元。/ 50 美元。 | It's + 数字 + yuan/dollars |

#### 第 2 课：序数与数量

| 主题 | 句块 | 释义 | 替换练习 |
|:----|:-----|:-----|:---------|
| 序数词 | **first, second, third, fourth, fifth, sixth** | 第一 ~ 第六 | the ___ one (first / second / third / fourth / fifth) |
| 序数+楼层 | **It's on the second floor.** | 在二楼。 | It's on the ___ floor. (first / third / fifth / tenth) |
| 序数+次数 | **This is my first time here.** | 我第一次来这里。 | This is my ___ time. (second / third / first / last) |
| 问数量 | **How many books do you have? — I have ten.** | 你有多少本书？——十本。 | How many ___ do you have? (pens / friends / brothers / cats) |
| 存在数量 | **There are seven days in a week.** | 一周有七天。 | There are ___ in ___. (12 months / a year / 24 hours / a day) |
| 约数 | **About 100 people came to the party.** | 大约 100 人来了派对。 | About ___ ___ came. (20 / students / 50 / guests / 200 / fans / 1,000 / people) |

### 🅱️ 时间与日期（5 主题，13 句块）

#### 第 3 课：时刻表达

> 🧠 **公式**：整点 = ...o'clock / 半点 = half past... / 一刻 = quarter past... / 差一刻 = quarter to...

| 主题 | 句块 | 释义 | 替换练习 |
|:----|:-----|:-----|:---------|
| 整点 | **What time is it? — It's 3 o'clock.** | 几点了？——三点。 | What time is it? — It's ___ o'clock. (1 / 5 / 7 / 10 / 12) |
| 半点 | **It's half past seven. (7:30)** | 七点半。 | It's half past ___. (one / three / six / nine / twelve) |
| 一刻 | **It's a quarter past eight. (8:15)** | 八点一刻。 | It's a quarter past ___. (two / five / ten / eleven) |
| 差一刻 | **It's a quarter to nine. (8:45)** | 差一刻九点。 | It's a quarter to ___. (three / six / ten / twelve) |
| at+时间 | **I get up at 7 o'clock every morning.** | 我每天早上 7 点起床。 | I ___ at ___ o'clock. (go to bed / 11 / have lunch / 12) |
| am/pm | **The meeting starts at 2:30 pm.** | 会议下午 2:30 开始。 | The ___ starts at ___. (movie / 7 pm / class / 9 am / party / 8 pm) |
| 直接读 | **It's 5:20. We're late!** | 5:20 了。我们迟到了！ | It's ___. We're ___! (8:45 / almost there / 9:55 / really late) |

|:----|:-----|:-----|:---------|
| 星期 | **Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday, Sunday** | 星期一 ~ 星期日 | on ___ (Monday / Friday / Sunday / the weekend / weekdays) |
| 月份 | **January, February, March, April, May, June, July, August, September, October, November, December** | 一月 ~ 十二月 | in ___ (January / March / July / October / December) |

### 🅲 祈使句（5 主题，12 句块）

#### 第 5 课：日常指令

> 🧠 **公式**：动词原形 = 命令（Sit down.）/ Don't + 动词 = 禁止（Don't worry.）/ Let's + 动词 = 提议（Let's go.）

| 主题 | 句块 | 释义 | 替换练习 |
|:----|:-----|:-----|:---------|
| 肯定指令 | **Sit down, please.** | 请坐。 | ___ please. (Stand up / Come in / Open the door / Close the window / Wait here / Listen to me) |
| 否定禁止 | **Don't worry. Everything is fine.** | 别担心。一切都好。 | Don't ___. (be late / forget / be sad / give up / touch that / tell anyone) |
| Let's 提议 | **Let's go! We're late.** | 走吧！我们迟到了。 | Let's ___! (eat / start / dance / take a break / try it / watch a movie) |
| Please 礼貌 | **Please wait a moment. I'll be right back.** | 请稍等。我马上回来。 | Please ___. (come in / sit here / sign here / follow me / fill out this form) |
| 引起注意 | **Look! The bus is coming.** | 看！公交车来了。 | Look! ___ / Listen! ___ (it's snowing / the baby is crying / someone is calling you) |
| Be+形容词 | **Be careful! The floor is wet.** | 小心！地板是湿的。 | Be ___! (quiet / patient / brave / honest / on time / safe / kind) |
| 客气表达 | **Have a seat. Make yourself at home.** | 请坐。别客气。 | Have a ___. (drink / good day / nice trip / safe journey) |

#### 第 6 课：提醒与建议

| 主题 | 句块 | 释义 | 替换练习 |
|:----|:-----|:-----|:---------|
| 提醒别忘 | **Don't forget to call your mom.** | 别忘了给你妈妈打电话。 | Don't forget to ___. (lock the door / bring your passport / turn off the lights / say thank you) |
| 慢慢来 | **Take your time. There's no rush.** | 慢慢来。不急。 | Take your time. ___ (I'll wait / no rush / think about it / enjoy your meal / read it carefully) |
| 请进 | **Come in! The door is open.** | 进来！门开着。 | ___! (Come in / Go ahead / After you / Step inside / Feel free to look around) |
| 自助 | **Help yourself to some coffee.** | 自己倒咖啡喝吧。 | Help yourself to ___. (some tea / the snacks / more food / the cake / the cookies) |
| 鼓励 | **Cheer up! Things will get better.** | 振作起来！一切会好起来的。 | ___! (Cheer up / Calm down / Wake up / Hurry up / Speak up / Slow down / Keep going) |
| 告知 | **Let me know if you need anything.** | 需要什么就告诉我。 | Let me know if ___. (you need help / you have questions / anything changes / you're free) |

### 🅳 礼貌表达（5 主题，13 句块）

#### 第 7 课：请求与提议

| 主题 | 句块 | 释义 | 替换练习 |
|:----|:-----|:-----|:---------|
| 礼貌请求 | **Could you help me, please? — Of course.** | 能请你帮我一下吗？——当然。 | Could you ___? (open the window / pass me the salt / tell me the time / wait a moment / repeat that) |
| 礼貌提供 | **Would you like some coffee? — Yes, please.** | 来点咖啡吗？——好的。 | Would you like ___? (some tea / a cookie / more water / something to eat / a seat) |
| 主动提供 | **Shall I open the window? — Yes, please.** | 要我开窗吗？——好的。 | Shall I ___? (turn on the light / get you a drink / call a taxi / help you / carry that) |
| 主动帮忙 | **Let me help you with that.** | 让我帮你拿。 | Let me ___. (help you / carry that / open the door / show you / explain / check / clean up) |
| 委婉意愿 | **I'd like a cup of coffee, please.** | 我想要一杯咖啡。 | I'd like ___. (a glass of water / the menu / to order / to pay / a table for two) |
| 委婉询问 | **Do you mind if I sit here? — Not at all.** | 你介意我坐这儿吗？——完全不。 | Do you mind if I ___? (open the window / use your phone / join you / leave early / ask a question) |
| 极其委婉 | **I was wondering if you could help me.** | 我在想你能不能帮我。 | I was wondering if you could ___. (take a look / give me advice / lend me a pen / check this) |

#### 第 8 课：邀请与回应

| 主题 | 句块 | 释义 | 替换练习 |
|:----|:-----|:-----|:---------|
| 礼貌邀请 | **Would you like to join us for dinner? — I'd love to!** | 你愿意和我们一起吃晚饭吗？——我很乐意！ | Would you like to ___? (come with us / watch a movie / go for a walk / try this dish / have a drink) |
| 提议 How about | **How about going to the movies tonight? — Sounds great!** | 今晚去看电影怎么样？——听起来不错！ | How about ___? (eating out / taking a break / trying this new place / calling her / getting a drink) |
| 提议 Why don't | **Why don't we go together? — Good idea!** | 我们为什么不一起去呢？——好主意！ | Why don't we ___? (go together / have lunch / start early / ask for help / try again) |
| 征求同意 | **Let's meet at 7, shall we? — Perfect.** | 我们 7 点见，好吗？——好。 | Let's ___, shall we? (go / begin / order / decide / split the bill / call it a day) |
| 礼貌接受 | **Thanks for the invitation. I'd love to come!** | 谢谢邀请。我很乐意来！ | Thanks for the invitation. I'd love to ___. (come / join / go / participate / be there) |
| 礼貌拒绝 | **Thanks, but I'm afraid I can't make it.** | 谢谢邀请，但恐怕我去不了。 | Thanks, but I'm afraid I can't ___. (come / make it / stay long / join you / say yes) |

### 🅴 综合运用（5 主题，12 句块）

#### 第 9 课：综合对话 1（餐厅/出行）

| 主题 | 句块 | 释义 | 运用的技能 |
|:----|:-----|:-----|:----------|
| 餐厅综合 | **I'd like a table for two, please. — Of course. Right this way. Here is the menu.** | 我想要一张两人的桌。请这边走。这是菜单。 | 礼貌表达 I'd like + 数字 two + 祈使句 |
| 餐厅综合 | **Could you bring me the bill, please? How much is it? — It's 85 yuan.** | 请给我账单好吗？多少钱？——85 元。 | 礼貌请求 Could you + 价格数字 + 回答 |
| 出行综合 | **Excuse me, could you tell me what time the next bus leaves?** | 打扰了，请问下一班车几点发车？ | 礼貌请求 Could you + 时间 what time |
| 出行综合 | **Please take the number 5 bus and get off at the third stop. It's about 20 minutes.** | 请坐 5 路车，第三站下车。大约 20 分钟。 | 祈使句 Please + 数字 5 + 序数 third + 时间 20 minutes |

#### 第 10 课：综合对话 2（社交/购物）

| 主题 | 句块 | 释义 | 运用的技能 |
|:----|:-----|:-----|:----------|
| 社交综合 | **Would you like to join our party on Saturday, June 15th? — I'd love to!** | 你愿意参加我们 6 月 15 日周六的派对吗？——很乐意！ | 邀请 Would you like + 星期 Saturday + 日期 June 15th + 接受 |
| 社交综合 | **What time does it start? At 7 pm. Don't be late!** | 几点开始？晚上 7 点。别迟到！ | 时间 What time + at 7 pm + 祈使否定 Don't be late |
| 购物综合 | **How about this blue one? It's cheaper than the red one. And the quality is better.** | 这个蓝色的怎么样？比红色的便宜，质量也更好。 | 提议 How about + 比较级 cheaper/better + and |
| 购物综合 | **Let me try it on. Where is the fitting room? — It's on the second floor, next to the escalator.** | 让我试试。试衣间在哪？——在二楼，扶梯旁边。 | Let me + 祈使句 + 序数 second + 介词 next to |
| 自由表达 | **Let's meet at 6:30 at the entrance. Don't forget to bring your camera!** | 我们 6:30 在入口处见。别忘了带相机！ | Let's + 时间 6:30 + 地点 entrance + 否定祈使 Don't forget |
| 自由表达 | **Take your time choosing. Let me know if you need help. Have a nice day!** | 慢慢挑。需要帮助就告诉我。祝愉快！ | 祈使 Take your time + Let me know + 祝愿 Have a nice day |

## 附：基础数字、时间与祈使句速查卡

### 数字 1-100 速查

```
1-10:    one two three four five six seven eight nine ten
11-19:   eleven twelve thirteen fourteen fifteen sixteen seventeen eighteen nineteen
20-90:   twenty thirty forty fifty sixty seventy eighty ninety
组合:    几十-个位 (twenty-one, thirty-five, ninety-nine)
100:     one hundred
1,000:   one thousand
```

### 时刻表达速查

```
整点:     It's 3 o'clock.
半点:     It's half past 3. (3:30)
一刻:     It's a quarter past 3. (3:15)
差一刻:   It's a quarter to 4. (3:45)
数字直接: It's three twenty. (3:20)
上午/下午: 3 am / 3 pm
```

### 祈使句结构速查

```
✅ 肯定祈使句：动词原形开头
  Sit down. / Open the door. / Wait here. / Be careful.
  Please + 动词原形更礼貌 → Please sit down.

❌ 否定祈使句：Don't + 动词原形
  Don't worry. / Don't be late. / Don't forget to call.

💡 Let's 提议：Let's + 动词原形
  Let's go! / Let's eat. / Let's take a break.
  附加问句征求同意：Let's..., shall we? → Let's meet at 7, shall we?
```

## 输出训练流水线（Warmup Pipeline）

每个训练主题配有 `warmup_pipeline.json` 输出训练流水线，包含题型：

| 题型 | 说明 | 示例 |
|:----|:-----|:-----|
| **number_drill** | 数字听写/认读 | 说"35" → thirty-five / 说"第5" → fifth |
| **time_drill** | 时间表达转换 | 7:30 → half past seven / 8:45 → a quarter to nine |
| **imperative_form** | 判断祈使句类别 | "Don't worry" → 否定祈使 / "Let's go" → Let's 提议 |
| **polite_conversion** | 普通句→礼貌句 | "Open the window" → "Could you open the window, please?" |
| **integrated_dialogue** | 综合对话补全 | 餐厅场景中填入正确的数字/时间/祈使/礼貌表达 |

覆盖全部 25 个训练主题，L1 难度，重点是把本包的四个补缺技能综合运用。
