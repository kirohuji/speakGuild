# 任务链 09 · 破冰 💬

> **场景**：💬 人来人往 | **难度**：L2~L3 (IELTS 6~7) | **任务**：3 | **主题**：社交/闲聊/建立关系

---

## 剧情概述

"怎么和陌生人开始一段对话，然后自然地结束它"——这是很多英语学习者最心虚的事。这条链训练你从搭讪到退出全流程的表达，让你在社交场合不再躲在角落刷手机。

---

## 任务 1 · 咖啡店里和旁边的人搭话

**情境**：你在咖啡店等朋友，旁边坐着一个人也在等人。你们对视了两次，你觉得应该说点什么，但不知道从哪开始。

**NPC**：咖啡店里的陌生人 — 友好但不会主动开口，如果你先说话会很乐意聊。

### 目标句块

| # | 句块 | 用途 |
|---|------|------|
| 1 | `Are you waiting for someone too, or just enjoying a quiet afternoon?` | 以共同处境自然开场 |
| 2 | `I notice you're reading ___—I've been meaning to pick that up. Any good?` | 从对方正在做的事切入 |
| 3 | `I'm ___ by the way—didn't mean to interrupt your reading.` | 自我介绍 + 礼貌表示不打扰 |
| 4 | `I won't keep you—just thought I'd say hi since we're both killing time here.` | 给对方退出空间 |

### 对话流程（3 轮）

| 轮次 | NPC | 期望用户表达（参考） | 提示 |
|------|-----|-------------------|------|
| 1 | "Yeah, my friend's always late. You too?" | "Same here—she said 'five minutes' twenty minutes ago. Are you waiting for someone as well, or just enjoying a quiet afternoon?" | `as well` / `enjoying a quiet afternoon` |
| 2 | "Waiting for a friend too. Just passing the time with this book." | "I noticed you're reading Klara and the Sun—I've been meaning to pick that up. Is it living up to the hype?" | `been meaning to` / `living up to the hype` |
| 3 | "It's a slow burn, but worth it. I'm Alex, by the way." | "Nice to meet you—I'm ___. Didn't mean to interrupt. I won't keep you—just thought I'd say hi since we're both killing time." | `didn't mean to interrupt` / `killing time` |

## 任务 2 · 对方说的梗你没听懂，怎么化解

**情境**：聊天时对方说了一个流行文化梗或本地笑话，周围人都笑了，但你完全没听懂。你需要在不破坏气氛的情况下处理这个尴尬。

**NPC**：派对上的新朋友 — 健谈、爱开玩笑，不是故意让你难堪。

### 目标句块

| # | 句块 | 用途 |
|---|------|------|
| 1 | `I feel like I'm missing some context here—what's the reference?` | 坦诚没听懂，请对方解释 |
| 2 | `I'm not sure I caught that—could you fill me in?` | 更简洁的版本 |
| 3 | `Ah, that's a cultural gap on my part—I didn't grow up with that show.` | 把"没听懂"转化为"文化差异" |
| 4 | `Well, now I've got something to add to my watchlist.` | 轻松收尾 |

### 对话流程（2 轮）

| 轮次 | NPC | 期望用户表达（参考） | 提示 |
|------|-----|-------------------|------|
| 1 | "So I basically pulled a Michael Scott in that meeting—full cringe." | *(周围人笑，你没懂)* "I feel like I'm missing some context—what's the reference? I'm not sure I caught that." | `missing context` / `what's the reference` / `caught that` |
| 2 | "Oh, from The Office! Michael Scott—the boss who's always saying the wrong thing?" | "Ah, that's a cultural gap on my part—I didn't grow up with that show. Well, now I've got something to add to my watchlist." | `cultural gap` / `add to my watchlist` |

## 任务 3 · 聊得来但想走了，怎么自然结束

**情境**：你们聊得不错，但你已经迟到了。你需要在不显得粗鲁的情况下结束对话，同时不把"以后再聊"变成一句空话。

**NPC**：刚认识的新朋友 — 聊得正开心，但如果你要走也不会介意。

### 目标句块

| # | 句块 | 用途 |
|---|------|------|
| 1 | `I've got to run, unfortunately—I'm already running late for something.` | 说明要走的原因 |
| 2 | `I've really enjoyed this conversation—it's not often you meet someone who's into ___ too.` | 真诚肯定这次对话 |
| 3 | `Let me grab your contact info before I take off—would be great to continue this another time.` | 把告别变成关系延续 |
| 4 | `I'll shoot you a message—maybe we can grab a coffee properly sometime.` | 提出具体的跟进动作 |

### 对话流程（2 轮）

| 轮次 | NPC | 期望用户表达（参考） | 提示 |
|------|-----|-------------------|------|
| 1 | "So anyway, that's how I ended up with a pet iguana for three weeks." | "That's an incredible story. Listen, I've got to run—I'm already running late. I've really enjoyed this—it's not often you meet another iguana enthusiast." | `got to run` / `running late` / `not often you meet` |
| 2 | "Ha, likewise! Good meeting you." | "Let me grab your number before I take off—would be great to continue this another time. I'll shoot you a message—maybe grab a proper coffee sometime?" | `grab your number` / `shoot you a message` / `proper coffee` |
