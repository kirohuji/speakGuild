# 任务链 06 · 买东西 🛒

> **场景**：🏠 日常闯关 | **难度**：L2~L3 (IELTS 6~7) | **任务**：3 | **主题**：购物/商品/退换

---

## 剧情概述

在英语国家购物不只是"付钱走人"。找不到东西要问、价格错了要理论、买到坏的要退——每个环节都在练你的表达能力。

---

## 任务 1 · 找不到想要的调料，问店员

**情境**：你在超市找了十分钟都没找到"dark soy sauce"。你需要的是一种特定的调料，但你不确定这里的超市是怎么分类的。

**NPC**：超市理货员 — 正在补货，可以带你去但不太会描述东西在哪。

### 目标句块

| # | 句块 | 用途 |
|---|------|------|
| 1 | `I've been wandering around for ages trying to track down ___—could you point me in the right direction?` | 幽默开场 + 求助 |
| 2 | `I'm after a specific variety—not the regular one.` | 说明你要的是特定品类 |
| 3 | `If you could physically show me where it is, that'd be a huge help.` | 请求带路（不是指方向） |
| 4 | `Aisle 7, third shelf down—got it. Cheers for that.` | 确认位置 + 道谢 |

### 对话流程（3 轮）

| 轮次 | NPC | 期望用户表达（参考） | 提示 |
|------|-----|-------------------|------|
| 1 | "You look a bit lost—need a hand?" | "I've been wandering around for ages trying to track down dark soy sauce. Could you point me in the right direction?" | `wandering around` / `track down` |
| 2 | "Soy sauce is in the Asian foods aisle—aisle 7, I think." | "Great, thanks. I'm after a specific variety though—dark soy sauce, not the regular light one. Would that be in the same aisle?" | `I'm after` / `specific variety` |
| 3 | "Should be, yeah. Third shelf down, right-hand side." | "Aisle 7, third shelf down—got it. Thanks for that—you've saved me another ten minutes of wandering." | `saved me` |

## 任务 2 · 结账发现价格不对，去服务台

**情境**：你买了一台打折的电饭煲，标价 $39.99，但小票上显示收了 $54.99。你回到服务台理论。

**NPC**：服务台员工 — 每天处理价格争议，需要看到证据才给退款。

### 目标句块

| # | 句块 | 用途 |
|---|------|------|
| 1 | `I believe I've been overcharged—the shelf price and the receipt don't match up.` | 礼貌指出问题 |
| 2 | `The tag on the shelf clearly stated $39.99. I wouldn't have picked it up otherwise.` | 提供证据 |
| 3 | `Could you do a price check to verify? I'm happy to wait.` | 建议对方核实 |
| 4 | `I'd appreciate having the difference refunded, if that's standard practice.` | 提出诉求 |

### 对话流程（3 轮）

| 轮次 | NPC | 期望用户表达（参考） | 提示 |
|------|-----|-------------------|------|
| 1 | "How can I help?" | "Hi—I believe I've been overcharged. I picked up this rice cooker from the clearance shelf at $39.99, but the receipt shows $54.99." | `overcharged` / `don't match up` |
| 2 | "Let me check. Sometimes the sale prices haven't updated in the system." | "The shelf tag clearly stated $39.99—I wouldn't have picked it up otherwise. Could you do a price check to verify?" | `wouldn't have... otherwise` / `price check` |
| 3 | "You're right—it should have been $39.99. I'll refund the difference. That's $15 plus tax back to your card." | "Lovely, thank you. I figured it was just a system glitch—glad we could sort it out." | `system glitch` / `sort it out` |

## 任务 3 · 网购商品有问题，要求退货

**情境**：你在网上买了一件夹克，收到后发现拉链卡住、一个口袋里还有线头。你想退货，但退货政策说"未使用、带标签"才能退——你已经剪了标签。

**NPC**：客服人员（线上聊天/电话）— 按退货政策办事，但对明显有质量问题的商品可以破例。

### 目标句块

| # | 句块 | 用途 |
|---|------|------|
| 1 | `I received my order today, but unfortunately the item appears to be defective.` | 清晰陈述问题 |
| 2 | `The zipper jams halfway up, and there's loose stitching on the inside.` | 具体描述缺陷 |
| 3 | `I'm aware the policy states items must be unworn with tags attached, but this is a manufacturing flaw rather than a change of mind.` | 承认政策但申辩例外 |
| 4 | `I'd be happy to provide photos if that helps expedite the process.` | 主动提供证据 |

### 对话流程（3 轮）

| 轮次 | NPC | 期望用户表达（参考） | 提示 |
|------|-----|-------------------|------|
| 1 | "Customer service, how can I assist?" | "Hi—I received order #88214 today, but unfortunately the jacket appears to be defective. The zipper jams halfway up and there's loose stitching inside." | `defective` / `jams` / `loose stitching` |
| 2 | "Have you removed the tags? Our policy requires items to be returned with tags attached." | "I have, I'm afraid—I didn't notice the flaws until after I'd removed them. But this is a manufacturing issue rather than a change of mind. I'd be happy to send photos." | `manufacturing flaw` / `change of mind` |
| 3 | "Send the photos to this email. If I can verify the defect, I'll authorize a return with free shipping." | "Will do—I'll send them right away. Thanks for being flexible about this—I do appreciate it." | `verify the defect` / `being flexible` |
