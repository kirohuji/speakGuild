# 任务链 08 · 紧急情况 🆘

> **场景**：🏥 搞定麻烦 | **难度**：L3~L4 (IELTS 7~8) | **任务**：3 | **主题**：安全/紧急/求助

---

## 剧情概述

紧急情况下的英语对话是所有非母语者最大的恐惧——压力大、信息多、错一个字可能后果严重。这条链训练你在高压下保持清晰、准确、冷静的表达。

---

## 任务 1 · 朋友受伤了，打急救电话

**情境**：你和朋友在公园打篮球，他跳起来落地时扭伤了脚踝，肿得厉害，站不起来。你需要打 911（或当地急救号码）。

**NPC**：911 接线员 — 冷静、高效，按标准流程提问（地址→发生了什么→伤者状态→是否有生命危险）。

### 目标句块

| # | 句块 | 用途 |
|---|------|------|
| 1 | `I need an ambulance—my friend has sustained a lower leg injury and can't bear any weight on it.` | 清晰说明需求 + 伤情 |
| 2 | `He's conscious and breathing normally, but he's in considerable pain.` | 说明生命体征 |
| 3 | `We're at the basketball court in Riverside Park, near the intersection of Oak and 3rd.` | 给出精确位置 |
| 4 | `I'll stay on the line—just tell me what you need me to do in the meantime.` | 表达配合意愿 |

### 对话流程（4 轮）

| 轮次 | NPC | 期望用户表达（参考） | 提示 |
|------|-----|-------------------|------|
| 1 | "911, what's your emergency?" | "I need an ambulance. My friend sustained a lower leg injury playing basketball—he can't bear any weight on it, and there's significant swelling." | `sustained` / `bear weight` / `swelling` |
| 2 | "Is he conscious? Is he breathing normally?" | "Yes, he's conscious and breathing normally. He's in considerable pain, but there's no bleeding and he didn't hit his head." | `conscious` / `considerable pain` |
| 3 | "What's your location?" | "We're at the basketball court in Riverside Park, near the intersection of Oak Street and 3rd Avenue. There's a parking lot about fifty meters east of us." | `intersection` / `fifty meters east` |
| 4 | "An ambulance is on the way. Stay with him and keep him still. Don't let him put weight on that ankle." | "Understood—I'll keep him still and stay on the line. Is there anything else I should do in the meantime?" | `in the meantime` |

## 任务 2 · 街头丢了钱包和护照，找警察

**情境**：你在旅游时发现背包拉链开着，钱包和护照都不见了。你不知道是被偷了还是自己掉了，但你需要警察出具一份报告才能去使馆补办护照。

**NPC**：警察 — 公事公办，需要你提供尽可能多的细节才能立案。

### 目标句块

| # | 句块 | 用途 |
|---|------|------|
| 1 | `I'd like to report a theft—or possibly a loss—my wallet and passport are missing.` | 清晰说明报案类型 |
| 2 | `I can't pinpoint exactly when it happened—I only noticed about twenty minutes ago.` | 诚实说明不确定时间 |
| 3 | `Would filing a police report enable me to get a replacement passport from my embassy?` | 确认报案的实际用途 |
| 4 | `Is there a reference number I can quote when I contact the embassy?` | 索取案件编号 |

### 对话流程（4 轮）

| 轮次 | NPC | 期望用户表达（参考） | 提示 |
|------|-----|-------------------|------|
| 1 | "You're reporting a stolen item?" | "I'd like to report a theft—or possibly a loss, I'm not entirely sure. My wallet and passport are both missing from my backpack." | `report a theft` / `missing from` |
| 2 | "When and where did you last have them?" | "I last used my wallet about an hour ago at a café on Harbor Street. I can't pinpoint exactly when it went missing—I only noticed twenty minutes ago." | `pinpoint` / `noticed` |
| 3 | "I'll file a report. This will help with your embassy—they usually require a police report for a replacement passport." | "That's exactly what I was hoping. Would the report enable me to get an emergency passport, or just a standard replacement?" | `enable me to` / `emergency passport` |
| 4 | "It should cover both. Here's your report and the reference number." | "Thank you. Is this reference number what I should quote when I contact the embassy, or is there a separate case number?" | `reference number` / `quote` |

## 任务 3 · 航班取消，在柜台争取最快改签

**情境**：你到了机场才发现航班被取消了——天气原因。下一班要等到明天，但你有重要的会明天早上。你需要和地勤谈判，争取最优方案。

**NPC**：航空公司地勤主管 — 处理了一天的取消航班，疲惫但有权限做特殊安排。

### 目标句块

| # | 句块 | 用途 |
|---|------|------|
| 1 | `I understand it's weather-related and beyond your control, but I'm in a bit of a bind.` | 先表示理解再说明困难 |
| 2 | `Are there any alternative routes—even with a connection—that could get me there by tomorrow morning?` | 询问替代航线 |
| 3 | `I'd be willing to fly into a nearby airport and make my own way from there if that opens up options.` | 展示灵活性 |
| 4 | `If there's genuinely nothing tonight, could you at least confirm me on the first flight out tomorrow and arrange accommodation?` | 退而求其次，争取补偿 |

### 对话流程（4 轮）

| 轮次 | NPC | 期望用户表达（参考） | 提示 |
|------|-----|-------------------|------|
| 1 | "Flight 447 is cancelled due to severe weather. We can rebook you on tomorrow's 7 a.m. flight." | "I understand it's weather-related—that's beyond your control. But I'm in a bit of a bind: I have a meeting at 9 a.m. tomorrow. Are there any alternative routes, even with a connection?" | `beyond your control` / `in a bind` / `alternative routes` |
| 2 | "Let me check… there's a flight via Chicago that lands at 6 a.m., but the connection is tight—only 45 minutes." | "That's cutting it fine, but I'd be willing to risk it. Alternatively, I'd even fly into a nearby airport—say, Baltimore—and make my own way from there." | `cutting it fine` / `make my own way` |
| 3 | "Chicago is your best bet. There's one seat left. Shall I book it?" | "Yes, please lock that in. And if I miss the connection for any reason, what's the fallback—would I be rebooked automatically?" | `lock that in` / `fallback` |
| 4 | "You'd be rebooked on the next available flight. Here's your new boarding pass. Good luck." | "Thanks for going above and beyond—I know you've had a rough day. Fingers crossed I make that connection." | `going above and beyond` / `fingers crossed` |
