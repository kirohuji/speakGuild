# 任务链 04 · 报到日 📋

> **场景**：🌍 落地生根 | **难度**：L2~L3 (IELTS 6~7) | **任务**：3 | **主题**：教育/行政/注册

---

## 剧情概述

报到日应该是兴奋的，但你的名字不在系统里，想选的课满了，迎新会上还被突然点名发言。这一天你需要说服、解释、即兴表达——用英语。

---

## 任务 1 · 注册系统里查不到你的名字

**情境**：你排在长长的注册队伍里终于到了窗口，但工作人员在系统里搜了两遍都没找到你的名字。你的录取通知书就在手里，但系统不认。

**NPC**：注册处职员 — 按流程办事，对系统很信任，对纸质文件持怀疑态度。

### 目标句块

| # | 句块 | 用途 |
|---|------|------|
| 1 | `I have my offer letter right here—could we cross-reference with that?` | 提供替代验证方式 |
| 2 | `Is there perhaps a discrepancy between the system and the admissions record?` | 礼貌地质疑系统 |
| 3 | `I've traveled a considerable distance to be here, so I'm hoping we can get to the bottom of this.` | 施压但不失礼 |
| 4 | `If there's someone from admissions we could loop in, I'd be more than happy to wait.` | 提出升级方案 |

### 对话流程（4 轮）

| 轮次 | NPC | 期望用户表达（参考） | 提示 |
|------|-----|-------------------|------|
| 1 | "I've searched twice—your name isn't coming up in the system. Are you sure you've completed all the pre-enrollment steps?" | "I'm fairly certain I have. I have my offer letter right here—could we cross-reference with the student ID on that?" | `fairly certain` / `cross-reference` |
| 2 | "Hmm, the ID matches but the name doesn't appear. This is unusual." | "Is there perhaps a discrepancy between the system and the admissions record? I've traveled quite a distance—I'm hoping we can get to the bottom of this." | `discrepancy` / `get to the bottom of` |
| 3 | "Let me call someone from admissions. Bear with me—this might take a few minutes." | "Of course. If there's someone we could loop in, I'm more than happy to wait." | `bear with me` / `loop in` |
| 4 | "Found the issue—there was a typo in your surname in the database. Fixed. Here's your registration packet." | "What a relief. Thank you for persevering—I really appreciate you going the extra mile." | `persevering` / `going the extra mile` |

## 任务 2 · 想选的课满了，说服辅导员加名额

**情境**：你规划好的课表里有一门核心课已经满员了。如果没有这门课，你的整个学期计划都会被打乱。你需要说服辅导员给你加一个名额。

**NPC**：Dr. Williams，学术辅导员 — 严谨但有同理心，手上确实有一两个机动名额但不轻易给。

### 目标句块

| # | 句块 | 用途 |
|---|------|------|
| 1 | `I noticed that ___ is already at capacity. I was wondering if there's any flexibility.` | 委婉提出请求 |
| 2 | `It's a prerequisite for my intended major, so deferring it would set me back an entire semester.` | 说明利害关系 |
| 3 | `I'd be willing to attend every lecture in person and sit in on any overflow sessions.` | 展示诚意和主动性 |
| 4 | `Is there a waitlist I could put my name on, or someone who handles overrides?` | 询问具体路径 |

### 对话流程（3 轮）

| 轮次 | NPC | 期望用户表达（参考） | 提示 |
|------|-----|-------------------|------|
| 1 | "What can I help you with?" | "I noticed PSYC 201 is already at capacity. I was wondering if there's any flexibility—it's a prerequisite for my major, so deferring would set me back an entire semester." | `at capacity` / `prerequisite` / `set me back` |
| 2 | "That's a popular course. I do have a couple of override slots, but I need a compelling reason." | "I'd be willing to attend every single lecture—no skipping. I'd also sit in on overflow sessions if that helps. This course is the linchpin of my entire schedule." | `compelling` / `overflow sessions` / `linchpin` |
| 3 | "Alright, you've convinced me. I'll authorize an override. But don't make me regret it." | "You won't. Thank you so much—I genuinely mean that. I won't let the opportunity go to waste." | `authorize an override` / `go to waste` |

## 任务 3 · 迎新会突然被 cue：30 秒自我介绍

**情境**：迎新破冰环节，主持人随机点名让大家自我介绍。你正低头看手机，突然听到自己的名字。30 秒，你需要在没有任何准备的情况下来一段让人记住的自我介绍。

**NPC**：迎新会主持人 + 约 30 个新生（你对着所有人说话）

### 目标句块

| # | 句块 | 用途 |
|---|------|------|
| 1 | `Alright, where to begin? I'm ___, originally from ___.` | 自然开场 |
| 2 | `I'm here to major in ___, though ask me again in a year and that might change.` | 轻松带出专业（加自嘲） |
| 3 | `Outside of academics, I'm really into ___—if anyone shares that interest, come find me later.` | 抛出一个社交钩子 |
| 4 | `Looking forward to getting to know you all—and surviving the cafeteria food together.` | 幽默收尾 |

### 对话流程（1 轮独白 + 主持人回应）

| 环节 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| **主持人** | "Next up—let's hear from… [your name]! Come on, don't be shy." |
| **你（30秒独白）** | "Alright, where to begin? I'm ___, originally from Beijing. I'm here to major in Psychology—though ask me again in a year, that might change. Outside of academics, I'm really into hiking and street photography, so if anyone shares those interests, come find me later. Looking forward to getting to know you all—and surviving the cafeteria food together. Cheers." |
| **主持人** | "Surviving the cafeteria food—that's the real major, isn't it? Thanks, that was great!" |
