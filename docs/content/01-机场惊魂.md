# 任务链 01 · 机场惊魂 ✈️

> **场景**：🌍 落地生根 | **难度**：L2~L3 (IELTS 6~7) | **任务**：3 | **主题**：旅行/机场/托运

---

## 剧情概述

你第一次独自飞往英语国家。从值机柜台到登机口，每一步都出了状况。这条链训练你在机场高压环境下的应变表达——怎么协商、怎么确认信息、怎么在"没听懂"的情况下不慌。

---

## 任务 1 · 行李超重，和地勤斗智斗勇

**情境**：你的托运行李超重 5 公斤。地勤告诉你需要付 $120 超重费。你不想付这笔钱，试图争取通融。

**NPC**：Alex，地勤人员 — 按规章办事但通情达理，见过太多超重的乘客。

### 目标句块

| # | 句块 | 用途 |
|---|------|------|
| 1 | `I was under the impression that the allowance was 25kg, not 20.` | 委婉表达你原来的理解 |
| 2 | `Is there any leeway regarding the weight limit?` | 询问是否有通融余地 |
| 3 | `Would it be feasible for me to repack and redistribute?` | 礼貌提出替代方案 |
| 4 | `I'd really rather not incur an additional charge if it can be helped.` | 表达不想付钱的立场 |

### 对话流程（4 轮）

| 轮次 | NPC | 期望用户表达（参考） | 提示 |
|------|-----|-------------------|------|
| 1 | "Your suitcase is 5kg over the limit. That'll be a $120 surcharge, I'm afraid." | "Oh, I was under the impression the allowance was 25kg. Is there any leeway at all?" | `under the impression` / `leeway` |
| 2 | "Unfortunately, the policy is strict. 20kg is the maximum for economy class." | "Would it be feasible for me to repack some items into my carry-on? I'd really rather not pay the surcharge." | `feasible` / `redistribute` / `rather not incur` |
| 3 | "You can try, but security might flag oversized carry-ons. You've got about 10 minutes before check-in closes." | "Fair enough—I'll repack what I can and come back. Thanks for the heads-up." | `fair enough` / `heads-up` |
| 4 | "Good luck. If it doesn't work out, the surcharge still applies." | "Understood. I appreciate your patience." | `understood` / `appreciate your patience` |

## 任务 2 · 安检被拦：包里那瓶水惹的祸

**情境**：过安检时，传送带停了。安检员从你包里拿出了一瓶 500ml 的饮用水——你完全忘了这回事。

**NPC**：安检员（严肃、高效，每天处理几百个和你一样的人）

### 目标句块

| # | 句块 | 用途 |
|---|------|------|
| 1 | `I completely overlooked that—my apologies.` | 承认疏忽，道歉 |
| 2 | `I hadn't realized liquids over 100ml were still prohibited.` | 解释原因（不是故意） |
| 3 | `Would you mind if I stepped aside to drink it, or does it need to be disposed of?` | 询问处理方式 |
| 4 | `In my defense, I've been up since 4 a.m.—my brain isn't fully functioning.` | 轻松化解尴尬（自嘲） |

### 对话流程（3 轮）

| 轮次 | NPC | 期望用户表达（参考） | 提示 |
|------|-----|-------------------|------|
| 1 | "Sir/Ma'am—this water bottle. You can't bring this through." | "Oh, I completely overlooked that. My apologies—I hadn't realized the 100ml rule still applied." | `overlooked` / `hadn't realized` |
| 2 | "It does. I'll need to confiscate it. Anything else in the bag?" | "No, that should be it. Would you mind if I stepped aside to drink it, or do you have to dispose of it?" | `confiscate` / `dispose of` |
| 3 | "I'll dispose of it. You're clear to proceed." | "Thank you. In my defense, I've been up since 4 a.m.—clearly not at my sharpest." | `in my defense` / `at my sharpest` |

## 任务 3 · 登机口改了，广播没听懂

**情境**：你在候机区刷手机，隐约听到广播提到了你的航班号，但语速太快没听清。你怀疑登机口被换了。

**NPC**：地勤人员 Jessica（在登机口柜台，正在处理登机手续）

### 目标句块

| # | 句块 | 用途 |
|---|------|------|
| 1 | `I seem to have missed an announcement regarding flight ___—could you clarify?` | 礼貌说明自己错过了信息 |
| 2 | `Just to double-check: has the departure gate been reallocated?` | 确认关键信息 |
| 3 | `I'd really appreciate it if you could point me in the right direction.` | 礼貌求助 |
| 4 | `I'd hate to miss my flight over something as trivial as a gate change.` | 表达紧迫感（带一点自嘲） |

### 对话流程（3 轮）

| 轮次 | NPC | 期望用户表达（参考） | 提示 |
|------|-----|-------------------|------|
| 1 | "Boarding for flight TK042 to London will begin shortly at Gate 47." | "Sorry to interrupt—I seem to have missed an announcement. Has the gate for TK042 been changed? I thought it was Gate 32." | `seem to have missed` / `reallocated` |
| 2 | "Yes, it was changed about 20 minutes ago. Gate 47, just down the corridor to your left." | "Gate 47, down to the left. Just to double-check—boarding hasn't started yet, has it?" | `just to double-check` / `boarding hasn't started` |
| 3 | "Not yet. You've got about 10 minutes. You'll make it." | "Brilliant, thank you. I'd hate to miss it over a gate change. Appreciate the help." | `I'd hate to miss` / `over something as trivial as` |
