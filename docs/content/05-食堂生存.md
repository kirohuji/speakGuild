# 任务链 05 · 食堂生存 🍽️

> **场景**：🏠 日常闯关 | **难度**：L1~L2 (IELTS 6) | **任务**：3 | **主题**：食物/烹饪/用餐

---

## 剧情概述

食堂是留学生日常英语的第一个战场。菜单看不懂、打饭阿姨手抖、饭卡突然没钱——这些小事每天都在发生，但每一件都需要你开口。

---

## 任务 1 · 今天菜单一个词都看不懂

**情境**：你站在食堂柜台前，今天的特色菜是"shepherd's pie with braised red cabbage and parsnip mash"。你只认识 pie 和 red。

**NPC**：打饭大叔 Dave — 嗓门大、语速快，但不介意解释，只要你问。

### 目标句块

| # | 句块 | 用途 |
|---|------|------|
| 1 | `I'm not familiar with a couple of items on today's menu—could you talk me through them?` | 请对方介绍菜品 |
| 2 | `What exactly is ___? I've never come across it before.` | 对特定菜名提问 |
| 3 | `Could you describe what it tastes like, or what's in it?` | 请求描述口感和成分 |
| 4 | `I'll give that a go—sounds intriguing.` | 勇敢尝试 |

### 对话流程（3 轮）

| 轮次 | NPC | 期望用户表达（参考） | 提示 |
|------|-----|-------------------|------|
| 1 | "What'll it be, love? Shepherd's pie's the special today!" | "I'm not familiar with a couple of items on today's menu—could you talk me through them? What exactly is shepherd's pie?" | `not familiar with` / `talk me through` |
| 2 | "It's minced lamb in gravy, topped with mashed potato, baked till golden. Comes with braised red cabbage and parsnip mash on the side." | "I've never come across parsnip before. Could you describe what it tastes like? Is it similar to potato?" | `come across` / `describe what it tastes like` |
| 3 | "Parsnip's sweeter than potato—kinda nutty. You'll either love it or hate it." | "I'll give it a go—sounds intriguing. One shepherd's pie, please." | `give it a go` / `intriguing` |

## 任务 2 · 打饭阿姨给太少了，委婉开口

**情境**：你点了烤鸡配蔬菜，但盘子里只有可怜的两小块鸡肉和一小撮西兰花。你觉得不够吃，但又不想显得贪心。

**NPC**：打饭阿姨 Margaret — 手很快，分量靠感觉，不介意多给一点但需要你开口。

### 目标句块

| # | 句块 | 用途 |
|---|------|------|
| 1 | `Would it be possible to get a slightly more generous portion of the chicken?` | 委婉请求加量 |
| 2 | `I don't mean to be picky, but I've got a long afternoon ahead and this might not quite tide me over.` | 解释原因 |
| 3 | `Just a spoonful or two more would make all the difference.` | 具体说明加多少 |
| 4 | `That's perfect—thanks for accommodating.` | 加到了就真诚感谢 |

### 对话流程（2 轮）

| 轮次 | NPC | 期望用户表达（参考） | 提示 |
|------|-----|-------------------|------|
| 1 | "There you go—roast chicken with seasonal veg." | "Thanks—would it be possible to get a slightly more generous portion of the chicken? I've got a long afternoon ahead and this might not quite tide me over." | `generous portion` / `tide me over` |
| 2 | "Say no more, dear. There you go—bit extra on the house." | "That's perfect—just a spoonful more makes all the difference. Thanks for accommodating." | `on the house` / `makes all the difference` / `accommodating` |

## 任务 3 · 饭卡余额不足，尴尬了

**情境**：你端着一托盘食物到了收银台，刷卡时机器响了——余额不足。后面排着五六个人，你感到所有人的目光都集中在你身上。

**NPC**：收银员（面无表情，每天处理几十次这种情况）

### 目标句块

| # | 句块 | 用途 |
|---|------|------|
| 1 | `Oh, that's embarrassing—I wasn't aware my balance was that low.` | 坦诚尴尬 |
| 2 | `Is there a top-up machine nearby where I could quickly add funds?` | 询问解决方案 |
| 3 | `Could you set this aside while I sort it out? I'll be right back.` | 请对方保留食物 |
| 4 | `Sorry for holding up the queue, everyone.` | 对后面排队的人表示歉意 |

### 对话流程（3 轮）

| 轮次 | NPC | 期望用户表达（参考） | 提示 |
|------|-----|-------------------|------|
| 1 | "Card declined. Insufficient funds." | "Oh, that's embarrassing—I wasn't aware my balance was that low. Is there a top-up machine nearby?" | `wasn't aware` / `top-up machine` |
| 2 | "There's one by the entrance. I can hold your tray, but be quick." | "Thank you—could you set it aside? I'll be right back. Sorry for holding up the queue, everyone." | `set aside` / `holding up the queue` |
| 3 | "You're back quick. Let's try again. … Approved. Have a good one." | "Phew—crisis averted. Thanks for your patience." | `crisis averted` |
