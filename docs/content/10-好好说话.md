# 任务链 10 · 好好说话 💬

> **场景**：💬 人来人往 | **难度**：L2~L3 (IELTS 6~7) | **任务**：3 | **主题**：情感/关系/道歉/感谢/安慰

---

## 剧情概述

最难的不是"点餐"或"问路"，而是"朋友失恋了你该怎么安慰""你做错了事该怎么道歉""别人帮了大忙你该怎么感谢"。这些对话没有标准答案，但有一些表达的框架可以让你说得真诚而不尴尬。

---

## 任务 1 · 朋友失恋了，怎么安慰

**情境**：你的好朋友刚结束一段三年的感情。你俩坐在公园长椅上，他不知道该说什么，你也不知道。但沉默不是最好的安慰。

**NPC**：好朋友 Chris — 情绪低落但还没崩溃，需要有人听他说，不是给他解决方案。

### 目标句块

| # | 句块 | 用途 |
|---|------|------|
| 1 | `I'm not going to pretend I know exactly what you're going through, but I'm here.` | 不假装理解，但表达陪伴 |
| 2 | `If you feel like talking about it, I'm all ears. If not, we can just sit here—no pressure either way.` | 给空间，不加压力 |
| 3 | `It's okay to not be okay for a while. You don't have to bounce back overnight.` | 允许对方难过 |
| 4 | `Whenever you feel up to it, let's do something that takes your mind off things—my treat.` | 提出一个轻松的出口 |

### 对话流程（3 轮）

| 轮次 | NPC | 期望用户表达（参考） | 提示 |
|------|-----|-------------------|------|
| 1 | *(沉默了一会儿)* "I don't even know what to say. It just… sucks." | "I'm not going to pretend I know exactly what you're going through. But I'm here—if you want to talk, I'm all ears. If not, we can just sit. No pressure." | `pretend I know` / `I'm all ears` / `no pressure` |
| 2 | "Thanks. I think I just need to not think about it for a bit, you know?" | "Completely get that. Whenever you feel up to it, let's go do something that takes your mind off things—road trip, movie marathon, whatever. My treat." | `feel up to it` / `takes your mind off things` / `my treat` |
| 3 | "That actually sounds really good. Maybe next weekend?" | "It's a plan. And Chris—it's okay to not be okay for a while. You don't have to bounce back overnight." | `it's okay to not be okay` / `bounce back` |

## 任务 2 · 室友帮了大忙，真诚感谢

**情境**：你上周感冒卧床，室友帮你带了三天饭、去药房买了药、还帮你跟教授请了假。你觉得一句"thank you"太轻了，想好好表达感激。

**NPC**：室友 Taylor — 觉得没什么大不了的，但你坚持要表达感谢。

### 目标句块

| # | 句块 | 用途 |
|---|------|------|
| 1 | `I wanted to say a proper thank you—not just a passing "thanks."` | 表示要认真感谢 |
| 2 | `You went out of your way for me last week, and I don't take that for granted.` | 肯定对方的付出 |
| 3 | `I genuinely don't know how I would've managed without you.` | 表达依赖感 |
| 4 | `Let me return the favor—dinner's on me this weekend, no arguments.` | 提出回报 |

### 对话流程（2 轮）

| 轮次 | NPC | 期望用户表达（参考） | 提示 |
|------|-----|-------------------|------|
| 1 | "Hey, you're looking better! Finally shook off that bug?" | "Finally, yeah. Listen—I wanted to say a proper thank you. You went out of your way for me last week, and I don't take that for granted. I genuinely don't know how I would've managed." | `proper thank you` / `went out of your way` / `don't take that for granted` |
| 2 | "Oh come on, it was nothing. Anyone would've done the same." | "No, seriously—it meant a lot. Let me return the favor: dinner's on me this weekend, no arguments. You pick the place." | `meant a lot` / `return the favor` / `no arguments` |

## 任务 3 · 你做错了事，需要认真道歉

**情境**：你答应帮朋友搬家但完全忘了这件事。朋友一个人搬完了所有东西，给你发了条"so that happened"的消息。你需要当面道歉，不是找借口，是让对方觉得你真心懊悔。

**NPC**：朋友 Jamie — 生气但更失望，需要听到真诚的道歉而不是解释。

### 目标句块

| # | 句块 | 用途 |
|---|------|------|
| 1 | `I owe you a serious apology—there's no excuse for what happened.` | 开门见山，不找借口 |
| 2 | `I let you down, and I've been kicking myself ever since I realized.` | 承认让对方失望 + 表达自责 |
| 3 | `I know "I forgot" sounds hollow, but I want you to know it wasn't out of not caring—it was pure disorganization on my part.` | 解释但不辩解 |
| 4 | `I'd like to make it up to you—whatever that looks like.` | 提出补救 |

### 对话流程（3 轮）

| 轮次 | NPC | 期望用户表达（参考） | 提示 |
|------|-----|-------------------|------|
| 1 | "Oh hey. Yeah, Saturday was… quite a day." | *(沉重地)* "I owe you a serious apology. There's no excuse—I completely forgot, and I let you down. I've been kicking myself ever since." | `owe you an apology` / `let you down` / `kicking myself` |
| 2 | "Honestly, what happened? You said you'd be there." | "I know 'I forgot' sounds hollow. It wasn't out of not caring—it was pure disorganization. I should've put it in my calendar, set a reminder, anything. I messed up." | `sounds hollow` / `not out of not caring` / `messed up` |
| 3 | *(沉默)* "…Alright. I appreciate you saying that." | "I'd like to make it up to you—whatever that looks like. I'll help you unpack every box, buy you dinner, whatever it takes. Just say the word." | `make it up to you` / `whatever it takes` / `say the word` |
