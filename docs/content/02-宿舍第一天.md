# 任务链 02 · 宿舍第一天 🏠

> **场景**：🌍 落地生根 | **难度**：L1~L2 (IELTS 6) | **任务**：3 | **主题**：住房/设施/规则

---

## 剧情概述

你刚到宿舍，发现一切和想象中不一样。房间分错了，室友是夜猫子，空调还坏了。这一天你需要在"礼貌"和"争取自己权益"之间找到平衡。

---

## 任务 1 · 房间被分错了，和宿管沟通

**情境**：你拿到的钥匙开不了门。宿管查了系统发现你的房间被分到了另一栋楼——但你所有的确认邮件都写的这栋。

**NPC**：Mrs. Chen，宿管阿姨 — 热心但有点健忘，喜欢唠家常，耳朵有点背。

### 目标句块

| # | 句块 | 用途 |
|---|------|------|
| 1 | `There seems to be a mix-up with my room assignment.` | 礼貌指出错误 |
| 2 | `I have it in writing that my room is in this building.` | 拿出证据，不卑不亢 |
| 3 | `Is there any way we could sort this out today?` | 强调紧迫性但保持礼貌 |
| 4 | `I'd rather not drag my luggage across campus if it can be avoided.` | 带点幽默表达诉求 |

### 对话流程（4 轮）

| 轮次 | NPC | 期望用户表达（参考） | 提示 |
|------|-----|-------------------|------|
| 1 | "Hmm, the key doesn't work? Let me check... Oh dear, the system has you in Building C, not here." | "There seems to be a mix-up. I have it in writing—my confirmation email clearly says Building A, Room 302." | `mix-up` / `have it in writing` |
| 2 | "Let me see... you're right, the email does say that. But the system says otherwise. This is a bit of a pickle." | "Is there any way we could sort this out today? I'd rather not drag my luggage across campus if it can be avoided." | `sort this out` / `drag my luggage` |
| 3 | "I'll call the housing office. Give me five minutes, dear." | "Of course, take your time. I'll just wait over here." | `take your time` |
| 4 | "Good news—they've fixed it in the system. Here's your new key. Sorry about the confusion!" | "No harm done. Thanks for sorting it out so quickly, Mrs. Chen." | `no harm done` / `sorting it out` |

## 任务 2 · 室友是个夜猫子：作息谈判

**情境**：你发现室友凌晨 2 点还在打游戏，戴着耳机但键盘声很大。你第二天早上 8 点有课，需要睡个好觉。

**NPC**：Jake，室友 — 友好但有点自我中心，不太意识到自己影响了别人。

### 目标句块

| # | 句块 | 用途 |
|---|------|------|
| 1 | `I don't mean to be difficult, but…` | 委婉开头 |
| 2 | `I was wondering if we could come to some kind of arrangement regarding quiet hours.` | 提议协商规则 |
| 3 | `I'm a pretty light sleeper, so even small noises tend to keep me up.` | 解释自己的情况 |
| 4 | `How would you feel about…?` | 征询对方意见 |

### 对话流程（3 轮）

| 轮次 | NPC | 期望用户表达（参考） | 提示 |
|------|-----|-------------------|------|
| 1 | "Oh hey! Sorry, was I too loud? I was in the middle of a raid—didn't realize the time." | "No worries—I don't mean to be difficult. I was wondering if we could come to some arrangement about quiet hours? I've got an 8 a.m. class." | `don't mean to be difficult` / `come to an arrangement` |
| 2 | "Yeah, sure. What were you thinking?" | "Maybe after midnight we keep it quiet? I'm a pretty light sleeper—even keyboard clicks tend to keep me up." | `light sleeper` / `tend to keep me up` |
| 3 | "That's fair. I'll switch to something quieter after midnight. Deal?" | "Deal. Thanks for being cool about it—I really appreciate that." | `being cool about it` |

## 任务 3 · 空调坏了，打电话报修

**情境**：房间里空调完全不制冷，室外 35°C。你得打电话给维修处，但他们似乎不怎么急。

**NPC**：维修处接线员 — 公事公办，按优先级排单，新人报到日是他们最忙的时候。

### 目标句块

| # | 句块 | 用途 |
|---|------|------|
| 1 | `I'm calling to report a faulty air conditioner in Room 302.` | 清晰说明问题 |
| 2 | `It's barely blowing any cold air at all.` | 描述症状 |
| 3 | `Is there any way to expedite this? The room is practically uninhabitable.` | 请求加急（带一点夸张） |
| 4 | `I'd be grateful if someone could swing by sometime today.` | 礼貌施压 |

### 对话流程（3 轮）

| 轮次 | NPC | 期望用户表达（参考） | 提示 |
|------|-----|-------------------|------|
| 1 | "Maintenance, how can I help?" | "Hi, I'm calling from Building A, Room 302. The air conditioner is faulty—it's barely blowing any cold air." | `faulty` / `barely blowing` |
| 2 | "I'll put in a work order. Someone should be able to get to it within 48 hours." | "Is there any way to expedite this? The room is practically uninhabitable in this heat—I'd be grateful if someone could swing by today." | `expedite` / `uninhabitable` / `swing by` |
| 3 | "I'll mark it as urgent. No promises, but I'll see what I can do." | "That's all I can ask for. Thanks for your help—fingers crossed." | `that's all I can ask for` / `fingers crossed` |
