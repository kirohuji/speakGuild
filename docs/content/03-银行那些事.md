# 任务链 03 · 银行那些事 🏦

> **场景**：🌍 落地生根 | **难度**：L2~L3 (IELTS 6~7) | **任务**：2 | **主题**：金融/银行业务

---

## 剧情概述

你在异国他乡需要一个银行账户——领工资、付房租、日常消费全靠它。但银行柜员的金融术语像一堵墙，ATM 还会吞卡。这条链训练你在金融场景下的"听不懂也要问清楚"能力。

---

## 任务 1 · 开户时柜员说的术语一个都听不懂

**情境**：你去银行开一个日常账户。柜员热情地介绍各种账户类型，但"overdraft protection""minimum balance""maintenance fee"这些词你只在考试阅读里见过，从来没在真实对话中听到过。

**NPC**：Sarah，银行柜员 — 友善高效，习惯了对国际客户放慢语速，但偶尔还是会蹦出术语。

### 目标句块

| # | 句块 | 用途 |
|---|------|------|
| 1 | `Could you walk me through the different account types?` | 请求逐步讲解 |
| 2 | `I'm not entirely clear on what ___ entails—could you elaborate?` | 请对方解释术语 |
| 3 | `What would be the most straightforward option for someone in my situation?` | 请求推荐 |
| 4 | `Just to clarify: does that mean I'd be charged if my balance dips below a certain threshold?` | 用自己的话复述确认 |

### 对话流程（4 轮）

| 轮次 | NPC | 期望用户表达（参考） | 提示 |
|------|-----|-------------------|------|
| 1 | "Welcome! Are you looking to open a checking account, a savings account, or both?" | "Could you walk me through the different types? I'm not entirely clear on what each one entails." | `walk me through` / `entails` |
| 2 | "Sure. A checking account is for daily transactions—it comes with a debit card. The savings account earns interest but has withdrawal limits." | "What would be the most straightforward option for someone like me—an international student who just needs to pay rent and buy groceries?" | `straightforward` / `in my situation` |
| 3 | "The student checking account would suit you. No monthly maintenance fee, but you'll need to maintain a minimum balance of $500 to avoid it." | "Just to clarify: if my balance dips below $500 at any point, I'd incur a fee? Or is it an average across the month?" | `just to clarify` / `dips below` / `incur a fee` |
| 4 | "It's an average daily balance, so a brief dip won't trigger it. Shall I set that up for you?" | "Yes, please. Also—does this account come with overdraft protection, or is that something I'd need to opt into separately?" | `overdraft protection` / `opt into` |

## 任务 2 · 卡被 ATM 吞了，紧急求助

**情境**：你在 ATM 取钱，输完密码后机器突然黑屏了——卡没吐出来，钱也没吐出来。你赶紧走进银行大厅求助。

**NPC**：银行大堂经理 — 习惯了处理 ATM 故障，但需要用户说清楚发生了什么。

### 目标句块

| # | 句块 | 用途 |
|---|------|------|
| 1 | `I've just had my card swallowed by the ATM outside.` | 清晰描述事件 |
| 2 | `The screen went blank mid-transaction before I could retrieve it.` | 说明时间节点 |
| 3 | `Is there a procedure for recovering a retained card?` | 询问处理流程 |
| 4 | `I'm a bit anxious because that's my only means of accessing my funds.` | 表达紧迫感 |

### 对话流程（3 轮）

| 轮次 | NPC | 期望用户表达（参考） | 提示 |
|------|-----|-------------------|------|
| 1 | "How can I help you? You look a bit rattled." | "I've just had my card swallowed by the ATM outside. The screen went blank mid-transaction—I couldn't retrieve it." | `swallowed` / `mid-transaction` / `retrieve` |
| 2 | "Oh no, that machine's been temperamental all week. Do you have ID on you?" | "Yes, here's my passport. Is there a procedure for recovering a retained card? I'm a bit anxious—that's my only means of accessing my funds." | `retained card` / `means of accessing my funds` |
| 3 | "I can retrieve it right now, actually. Just give me a moment to unlock the machine. You'll need to reset your PIN afterward." | "That's a relief—thank you. I'll reset the PIN straight away. Is there a more reliable ATM nearby you'd recommend?" | `that's a relief` / `straight away` / `reliable` |
